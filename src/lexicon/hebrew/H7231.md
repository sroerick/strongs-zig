# H7231 רָבַב / רבב

## râbab

_(raw-bab' | raw-BAHB | ra-VAHV)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; (compare [[H7241]]); also as denominative from [[H7233]]; properly, to cast together , i.e., increase, especially in number; to multiply by the myriad; increase, be many(-ifold), be more, multiply, ten thousands.

- to be or become many, be or become much, be or become great
    - (Qal)
        - to be or become many
        - to be or become great
        - to be long (of journey)
- (Pual) ten thousands
