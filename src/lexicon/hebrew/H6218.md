# H6218 עָשׂוֹר / עשור

## ʻâsôwr

_(aw-sore' | aw-SORE | ah-SORE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or עָשֹׂר; from [[H6235]]; ten; by abbreviated form ten strings, and so a decachord; (instrument of) ten (strings, -th).

- ten, decade
    - ten, tenth
    - ten-stringed, harp
