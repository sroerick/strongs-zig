# H7019 קַיִץ / קיץ

## qayits

_(kah'-yits | ka-YITS | ka-YEETS)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H6972]]; harvest (as the crop), whether the product (grain or fruit) or the (dry) season; summer (fruit, house).

- summer, summer-fruit
