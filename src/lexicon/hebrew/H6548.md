# H6548 פַּרְעֹה חׇפְרַע / פרעה חפרע

## Parʻôh Chophraʻ

_(par-o' khof-rah' | pahr-OH hope-RA | pahr-OH hofe-RA)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; the great house-covering evil; of Egyptian derivation; Paroh-Chophra, an Egyptian king; Pharaoh-hophra.

- the Pharaoh of Egypt at the time of the conquest by Nebuchadnezzar
