# H2596 חָנַךְ / חנך

## chânak

_(khaw-nak' | haw-NAHK | ha-NAHK)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; (compare [[H2614]]); properly, to narrow; figuratively, to initiate or discipline; dedicate, train up.

- to train, dedicate, inaugurate
    - (Qal)
        - to train, train up
        - to dedicate
