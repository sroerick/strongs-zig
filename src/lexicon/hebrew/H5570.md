# H5570 סְנָאָה / סנאה

## Çᵉnâʼâh

_(sen-aw-aw' | seh-naw-AW | seh-na-AH)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; thorny; from an unused root meaning to prick; thorny; Senaah, a place in Palestine; Senaah, Hassenaah (with the article).

- ancestor of a family who returned from exile with Zerubbabel
