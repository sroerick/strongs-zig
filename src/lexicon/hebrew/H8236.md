# H8236 שִׁפְרָה / שפרה

## Shiphrâh

_(shif-raw' | ship-RAW | sheef-RA)_

### Definition

proper feminine noun; fair; the same as [[H8235]]; Shiphrah, an Israelitess; Shiphrah.

- one of the two Hebrew midwives who were ordered by Pharaoh to kill all the male children born to the Hebrews but who disobeyed
