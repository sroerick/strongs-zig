# H7329 רָזָה / רזה

## râzâh

_(raw-zaw' | raw-DZAW | ra-ZA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to emaciate, i.e., make (become) thin (literally or figuratively); famish, wax lean.

- to be or become or grow lean
    - (Qal) to make lean
    - (Niphal) to be made lean
