# H6589 פָּשַׂק / פשק

## pâsaq

_(paw-sak' | paw-SAHK | pa-SAHK)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to dispart (the feet or lips), i.e., become licentious; open (wide).

- to part, open wide
    - (Qal)
        - to open wide
        - to be talkative (of the mouth)
    - (Piel) to open wide
