# H5098 נָהַם / נהם

## nâham

_(naw-ham' | naw-HAHM | na-HAHM)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to growl; mourn, roar(-ing).

- to growl, groan
    - (Qal)
        - to growl (of lion)
        - to groan (of sufferer)
