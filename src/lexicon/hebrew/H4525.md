# H4525 מַסְגֵּר / מסגר

## maçgêr

_(mas-gare' | mahs-ɡARE | mahs-ɡARE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H5462]]; a fastener, i.e., (of a person) a smith, (of a thing) a prison; prison, smith.

- a shutting up, locksmith, smith, dungeon, enclosure, builder of bulwarks
    - locksmith, smith
    - dungeon
