# H6555 פָּרַץ / פרץ

## pârats

_(paw-rats' | paw-RAHTS | pa-RAHTS)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to break out (in many applications, direct and indirect, literal and figurative); [[lexicon-key|×]] abroad, (make a) breach, break (away, down, -er, forth, in, up), burst out, come (spread) abroad, compel, disperse, grow, increase, open, press, scatter, urge.

- to break through or down or over, burst, breach
    - (Qal)
        - to break or burst out (from womb or enclosure)
        - to break through or down, make a breach in
        - to break into
        - to break open
        - to break up, break in pieces
        - to break out (violently) upon
        - to break over (limits), increase
        - to use violence
        - to burst open
            - to spread, distribute
    - (Niphal) to be broken through
    - (Pual) to be broken down
    - (Hithpael) to break away
