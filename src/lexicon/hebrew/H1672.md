# H1672 דָּאַג / דאג

## dâʼag

_(daw-ag' | daw-Aɡ | da-Aɡ)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; be anxious; be afraid (careful, sorry), sorrow, take thought.

- to fear, be anxious, be concerned, be afraid, be careful
    - (Qal)
        - to be anxious, be concerned
        - to fear, dread
