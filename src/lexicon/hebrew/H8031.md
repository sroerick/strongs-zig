# H8031 שָׁלִשָׁה / שלשה

## Shâlishâh

_(shaw-lee-shaw' | shaw-lih-SHAW | sha-lee-SHA)_

### Definition

proper feminine noun; thirs; feminine from [[H8027]]; trebled land; Shalishah, a place in Palestine; Shalisha.

- a district near Mount Ephraim through which Saul passed as he searched for the asses
