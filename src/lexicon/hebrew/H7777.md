# H7777 שׁוּעָל / שועל

## Shûwʻâl

_(shoo-awl' | shoo-AWL | shoo-AL)_

### Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; jackal; the same as [[H7776]]; Shual, the name of an Israelite and of a place in Palestine; Shual.

- as Asherite, son of Zophah (noun proper masculine)
- a district in Benjamin probably north of Michmash (noun proper locative)
