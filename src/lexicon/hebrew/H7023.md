# H7023 קִיר / קיר

## qîyr

_(keer | keer | keer)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or קִר; (Isaiah 22:5), or (feminine) קִירָה; from [[H6979]]; a wall (as built in a trench); [[lexicon-key|+]] mason, side, town, [[lexicon-key|×]] very, wall.

- wall, side
    - wall (of house or chamber)
    - the sides (of the altar)
