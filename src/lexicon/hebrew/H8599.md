# H8599 תַּפּוּחַ / תפוח

## Tappûwach

_(tap-poo'-akh | tahp-POO-ah | ta-POO-ak)_

### Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; the apple city; the same as [[H8598]]; Tappuach, the name of two places in Palestine; Tappuah.

- a Judaite of the family of Caleb, son of Hebron (noun proper masculine)
- a town in the lowland of Judah (noun proper locative)
- a town on the border in Ephraim (noun proper locative)
- a territory within the boundaries of Manasseh (noun proper locative)
