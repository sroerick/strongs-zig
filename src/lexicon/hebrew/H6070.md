# H6070 עַנְתֹתִיָּה / ענתתיה

## ʻAnthôthîyâh

_(an-tho-thee-yaw' | an-toh-tih-YAW | an-toh-tee-YA)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; Jehovah's answer; from the same as [[H6068]] and [[H3050]]; answers of Jah; Anthothijah, an Israelite; Antothijah.

- a Benjamite, one of the sons of Jeroham
