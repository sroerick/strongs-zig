# H4775 מָרַד / מרד

## mârad

_(maw-rad' | maw-RAHD | ma-RAHD)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to rebel; rebel(-lious).

- to rebel, revolt, be rebellious
    - (Qal) to rebel, revolt
        - against human king
        - against God
        - against light (poetic)
