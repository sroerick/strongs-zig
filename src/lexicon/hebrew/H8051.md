# H8051 שַׁמּוּעַ / שמוע

## Shammûwaʻ

_(sham-moo'-ah | shahm-MOO-ah | sha-MOO-ah)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; renowned; from [[H8074]]; renowned; Shammua, the name of four Israelites; Shammua, Shammuah.

- son of Zaccur and the spy from the tribe of Reuben
- son of David by his wife Bathsheba
    - also 'Shammuah' and 'Shimea'
- a Levite, son of Galal and father of Abda who lived in Jerusalem after the return from exile in the time of Nehemiah
    - also 'Shemaiah'
- a priest, head of the family of Bilgah in the time of Joiakim after the return from exile
