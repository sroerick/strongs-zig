# H7912 שְׁכַח / שכח

## shᵉkach

_(shek-akh' | sheh-KA | sheh-HAHK)_

### Definition

verb; corresponding to [[H7911]] through the idea of disclosure of a covered or forgotten thing; to discover (literally or figuratively); find.

- to find
    - (Aphel) to find
    - (Ithpeal) to be found
