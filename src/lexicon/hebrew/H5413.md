# H5413 נָתַךְ / נתך

## nâthak

_(naw-thak' | naw-TAHK | na-TAHK)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to flow forth (literally or figuratively); by implication, to liquify; drop, gather (together), melt, pour (forth, out).

- to pour out or forth, drop (or rain), be poured, be poured out, be melted, be molten
    - (Qal) to pour forth
    - (Niphal) to be poured, be poured out
    - (Hiphil) to pour out, melt
    - (Hophal) to be melted
