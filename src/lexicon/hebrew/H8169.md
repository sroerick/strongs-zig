# H8169 שַׁעַלְבִים / שעלבים

## Shaʻalbîym

_(shah-al-beem' | sha-al-BEEM | sha-al-VEEM)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; place of foxes; or שַׁעֲלַבִּין; plural from [[H7776]]; fox-holes; Shaalbim or Shaalabbin, a place in Palestine; Shaalabbin, Shaalbim.

- a town in Dan occupied by the Amorites
