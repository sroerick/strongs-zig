# H8032 שִׁלְשׁוֹם / שלשום

## shilshôwm

_(shil-shome' | shil-SHOME | sheel-SHOME)_

### Definition

adverb; or שִׁלְשֹׁם; from the same as [[H8028]]; trebly, i.e., (in time) day before yesterday; [[lexicon-key|+]] before (that time, -time), excellent things (from the margin), [[lexicon-key|+]] heretofore, three days, [[lexicon-key|+]] time past.

- day before yesterday, three days ago
    - an idiom for 'heretofore in times past'
