# H5310 נָפַץ / נפץ

## nâphats

_(naw-fats' | naw-PAHTS | na-FAHTS)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to dash to pieces, or scatter; be beaten in sunder, break (in pieces), broken, dash (in pieces), cause to be discharged, dispersed, be overspread, scatter.

- to shatter, break, dash, beat in pieces
    - (Qal)
        - to shatter
        - shattering (infinitive)
    - (Piel) to dash to pieces
    - (Pual) to pulverise
- to scatter, disperse, overspread, be scattered
    - (Qal)
        - to be scattered
        - dispersed (participle)
