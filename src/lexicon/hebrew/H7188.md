# H7188 קָשַׁח / קשח

## qâshach

_(kaw-shakh' | kaw-SHA | ka-SHAHK)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to be (causatively, make) unfeeling; harden.

- to make hard, treat hardly, treat severely
    - (Hiphil)
        - to make hard, make stubborn
        - to treat hardly, treat roughly
