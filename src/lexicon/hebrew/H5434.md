# H5434 סְבָא / סבא

## Çᵉbâʼ

_(seb-aw' | seh-BAW | seh-VA)_

### Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; drink thou; of foreign origin; Seba, a son of Cush, and the country settled by him; Seba.

- a son of Cush (noun proper masculine)
- a nation south of Palestine, perhaps Ethiopia (noun proper locative)
