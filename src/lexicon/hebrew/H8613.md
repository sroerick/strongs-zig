# H8613 תׇּפְתֶּה / תפתה

## Tophteh

_(tof-teh' | tope-TEH | tofe-TEH)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; place of fire; probably a form of [[H8612]]; Tophteh, a place of cremation; Tophet.

- a place in the southeast end of the valley of the son of Hinnom south of Jerusalem
    - same as [[H8612]]
