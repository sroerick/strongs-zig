# H6195 עׇרְמָה / ערמה

## ʻormâh

_(or-maw' | ore-MAW | ore-MA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; feminine of [[H6193]]; trickery; or (in a good sense) discretion; guile, prudence, subtilty, wilily, wisdom.

- shrewdness, craftiness, prudence
