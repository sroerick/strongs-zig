# H2487 חֲלִיפָה / חליפה

## chălîyphâh

_(khal-ee-faw' | huh-lee-PAW | huh-lee-FA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from [[H2498]]; alternation; change, course.

- a change, change (of garments), replacement
    - change (of raiment)
    - relays
    - relief (from death)
    - changing, varying (course of life)
