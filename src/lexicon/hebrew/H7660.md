# H7660 שָׁבַץ / שבץ

## shâbats

_(shaw-bats' | shaw-BAHTS | sha-VAHTS)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to interweave (colored) threads in squares; by implication (of reticulation) to inchase gems in gold; embroider, set.

- to weave in checkered or plaited work or pattern (meaning probable)
    - (Piel) to weave, plait
    - (Pual) inwoven, set (participle)
