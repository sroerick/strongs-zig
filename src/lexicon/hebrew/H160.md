# H160 אַהֲבָה / אהבה

## ʼahăbâh

_(a-hab-aw | ah-huh-BAW | ah-huh-VA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; feminine of [[H158]] and meaning the same; love; love.

- love
    - human love for human object
        - of man toward man
        - of man toward himself
        - between man and woman
        - sexual desire
- God's love to His people
