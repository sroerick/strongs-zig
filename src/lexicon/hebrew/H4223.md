# H4223 מְחָא / מחא

## mᵉchâʼ

_(mekh-aw' | meh-HAW | meh-HA)_

### Definition

verb; corresponding to [[H4222]]; to strike in pieces; also to arrest; specifically to impale; hang, smite, stay.

- to strike, smite, kill
    - (Peal) to strike
    - (Pael) to hinder
    - (Ithpeal) to allow to be stricken
