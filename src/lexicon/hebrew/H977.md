# H977 בָּחַר / בחר

## bâchar

_(baw-khar' | baw-HAHR | ba-HAHR)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to try, i.e., (by implication) select; acceptable, appoint, choose (choice), excellent, join, be rather, require.

- to choose, elect, decide for
    - (Qal) to choose
    - (Niphal) to be chosen
    - (Pual) to be chosen, selected
