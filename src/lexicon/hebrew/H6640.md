# H6640 צְבוּ / צבו

## tsᵉbûw

_(tseb-oo' | tseh-BOO | tseh-VOO)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H6634]]; properly, will; concretely, an affair (as a matter of determination); purpose.

- thing, anything, matter
    - apparently thing desired
    - as a matter of determination
