# H7219 רֹאשׁ / ראש

## rôʼsh

_(roshe | rohsh | rohsh)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or רוֹשׁ; (Deuteronomy 32:32), apparently the same as [[H7218]]; a poisonous plant, probably the poppy (from its conspicuous head); generally poison (even of serpents); gall, hemlock, poison, venom.

- gall, venom, bitter, poisonous
