# H6901 קָבַל / קבל

## qâbal

_(kaw-bal' | kaw-BAHL | ka-VAHL)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to admit, i.e., take (literally or figuratively); choose, (take) hold, receive, (under-)take.

- to take, receive, be before
    - (Piel)
        - to take, choose, receive
        - to accept
        - to accept, assume
    - (Hiphil)
        - to show oppositeness
        - to correspond, receive one to the other
