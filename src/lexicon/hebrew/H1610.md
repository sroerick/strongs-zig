# H1610 גַּף / גף

## gaph

_(gaf | ɡahp | ɡahf)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from an unused root meaning to arch; the back; by extension the body or self; [[lexicon-key|+]] highest places, himself.

- body, self (only in phrase, eg by himself)
- height, elevation
