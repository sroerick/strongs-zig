## mâdôwn

_(maw-dohn' | maw-DONE | ma-DONE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H1777]]; a contest or quarrel; brawling, contention(-ous), discord, strife.

- strife, contention
    - strife, contention
    - object of contention
