# H932 בֹּהַן / בהן

## Bôhan

_(bo'han | boh-HAHN | boh-HAHN)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; thumb; an orthographical variation of [[H931]]; thumb, Bohan, an Israelite; Bohan.

- a Reubenite who set a boundary stone between Judah and Benjamin
- ([[lexicon-key|BDB]]) a mark of division between Judah and Benjamin
