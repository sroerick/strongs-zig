# H197 אוּלָם / אולם

## ʼûwlâm

_(oo-lawm' | oo-LAWM | oo-LAHM)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or (shortened), אֻלָם ; from [[H481]] (in the sense of tying); a vestibule (as bound to the building); porch.

- porch
    - in Solomon's temple
    - in Solomon's palace
    - in temple of Ezekiel's vision
