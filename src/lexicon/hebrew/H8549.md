# H8549 תָּמִים / תמים

## tâmîym

_(taw-meem' | taw-MEEM | ta-MEEM)_

### Definition

adjective; from [[H8552]]; entire (literally, figuratively or morally); also (as noun) integrity, truth; without blemish, complete, full, perfect, sincerely(-ity), sound, without spot, undefiled, upright(-ly), whole.

- complete, whole, entire, sound
    - complete, whole, entire
    - whole, sound, healthful
    - complete, entire (of time)
    - sound, wholesome, unimpaired, innocent, having integrity
    - what is complete or entirely in accord with truth and fact (neuter adjective/substantive)
