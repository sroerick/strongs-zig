# H6828 צָפוֹן / צפון

## tsâphôwn

_(tsaw-fone' | tsaw-PONE | tsa-FONE)_

### Definition

feminine noun; or צָפֹן; from [[H6845]]; properly, hidden, i.e., dark; used only of the north as a quarter (gloomy and unknown); north(-ern, -ward) (side, wind).

- north (of direction), northward
    - north
    - northward
