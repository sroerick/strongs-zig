# H8313 שָׂרַף / שרף

## sâraph

_(saw-raf' | saw-RAHP | sa-RAHF)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to be (causatively, set) on fire; (cause to, make a) burn(-ing, up) kindle, [[lexicon-key|×]] utterly.

- to burn
    - (Qal) to burn
    - (Niphal) to be burned
    - (Piel) burner, burning (participle)
    - (Pual) to be burnt up, be burned
