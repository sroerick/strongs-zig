# H7701 שֹׁד / שד

## shôd

_(shode | shode | shode)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or שׁוֹד; (Job 5:21), from [[H7736]]; violence, ravage; desolation, destruction, oppression, robbery, spoil(-ed, -er, -ing), wasting.

- havoc, violence, destruction, devastation, ruin
    - violence, havoc (as social sin)
    - devastation, ruin
