# H5271 נָעוּר / נעור

## nâʻûwr

_(naw-oor' | naw-OOR | na-OOR)_

### Definition

feminine noun; or נָעֻר; and (feminine) נְעֻרָה; properly, passive participle from [[H5288]] as denominative; (only in plural collective or emphatic form) youth, the state (juvenility) or the persons (young people); childhood, youth.

- youth, early life
