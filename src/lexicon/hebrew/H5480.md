# H5480 סוּךְ / סוך

## çûwk

_(sook | sook | sook)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to smear over (with oil), i.e., anoint; anoint (self), [[lexicon-key|×]] at all.

- to anoint, pour in anointing
    - (Qal) to anoint
        - to anoint oneself
        - to anoint (another)
        - to be poured
    - (Hiphil) to anoint
