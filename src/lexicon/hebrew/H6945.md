# H6945 קָדֵשׁ / קדש

## qâdêsh

_(kaw-dashe' | kaw-DAYSH | ka-DAYSH)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H6942]]; a (quasi) sacred person, i.e., (technically) a (male) devotee (by prostitution) to licentious idolatry; sodomite, unclean.

- male temple prostitute
