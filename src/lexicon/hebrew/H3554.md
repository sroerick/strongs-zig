# H3554 כָּוָה / כוה

## kâvâh

_(kaw-vaw' | kaw-WAW | ka-VA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to prick or penetrate; hence, to blister (as smarting or eating into); burn.

- to burn, scorch, brand
    - (Niphal) to be burned, be scorched
