# H8585 תְּעָלָה / תעלה

## tᵉʻâlâh

_(teh-aw-law' | teh-aw-LAW | teh-ah-LA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from [[H5927]]; a bandage or plaster (as placed upon a wound); conduit, cured, healing, little river, trench, watercourse.

- conduit, water course, trench
- healing (of new flesh and skin forming over wound)
    - ([[lexicon-key|CLBL]]) bandage, plaister (for healing of wound)
