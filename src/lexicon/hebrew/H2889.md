# H2889 טָהוֹר / טהור

## ṭâhôwr

_(taw-hore' | taw-HORE | ta-HORE)_

### Definition

adjective; or טָהֹר; from [[H2891]]; pure (in a physical, chemical, ceremonial or moral sense); clean, fair, pure(-ness).

- pure, clean
    - clean (ceremonially - of animals)
    - pure (physically)
    - pure, clean (morally, ethically)
