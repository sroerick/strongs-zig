# H5740 עֵדֶר / עדר

## ʻÊder

_(ay'-der | ay-DER | ay-DER)_

### Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; a flock; the same as [[H5739]]; Eder, the name of two places in Palestine; Edar, Eder.

- a Merarite Levite in the time of David (noun proper masculine)
- a town in the extreme south of Judah on the border of Edom; site unknown (noun proper locative)
