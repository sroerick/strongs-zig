# H2436 חֵיק / חיק

## chêyq

_(khake | hake | hake)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or חֵק; and חוֹק; from an unused root, apparently meaning to inclose; the bosom (literally or figuratively); bosom, bottom, lap, midst, within.

- bosom, hollow, bottom, midst
