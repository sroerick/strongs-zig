# H2427 חִיל / חיל

## chîyl

_(kheel | heel | heel)_

### Definition

masculine noun; and (feminine) חִילָה; from [[H2342]]; a throe (expectant of childbirth); pain, pang, sorrow.

- pain, agony, sorrow, a writhing, anguish
    - writhing (of fear)
    - anguish
