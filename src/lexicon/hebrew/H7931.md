# H7931 שָׁכַן / שכן

## shâkan

_(shaw-kan' | shaw-KAHN | sha-HAHN)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root (apparently akin (by transmission) to [[H7901]] through the idea of lodging; compare [[H5531]], [[H7925]]); to reside or permanently stay (literally or figuratively); abide, continue, (cause to, make to) dwell(-er), have habitation, inhabit, lay, place, (cause to) remain, rest, set (up).

- to settle down, abide, dwell, tabernacle, reside
    - (Qal)
        - to settle down to abide
        - to abide, dwell, reside
    - (Piel)
        - to make settle down, establish
        - to make or cause to dwell
    - (Hiphil)
        - to lay, place, set, establish, settle, fix
        - to cause to dwell or abide
