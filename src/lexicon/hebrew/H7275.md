# H7275 רָגַם / רגם

## râgam

_(raw-gam' | raw-ɡAHM | ra-ɡAHM)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root (compare [[H7263]], [[H7321]], [[H7551]]); to cast together (stones), i.e., to lapidate; [[lexicon-key|×]] certainly, stone.

- to stone, slay or kill by stoning
    - (Qal) to stone
