# H6509 פָּרָה / פרה

## pârâh

_(paw-raw' | paw-RAW | pa-RA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to bear fruit (literally or figuratively); bear, bring forth (fruit), (be, cause to be, make) fruitful, grow, increase.

- to bear fruit, be fruitful, branch off
    - (Qal) to bear fruit, be fruitful
    - (Hiphil)
        - to cause to bear fruit
        - to make fruitful
        - to show fruitfulness, bear fruit
