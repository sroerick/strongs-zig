# H5835 עֲזָרָה / עזרה

## ʻăzârâh

_(az-aw-raw' | uh-dzaw-RAW | uh-za-RA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from [[H5826]] in its original meaning of surrounding; an inclosure; also a border; court, settle.

- enclosure
    - ledge (surrounding Ezekiel's altar)
    - court (outer one of temple)
