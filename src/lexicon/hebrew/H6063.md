# H6063 עָנֵר / ענר

## ʻÂnêr

_(aw-nare' | aw-NARE | ah-NARE)_

### Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; boy; probably for [[H5288]]; Aner, a Amorite, also a place in Palestine; Aner.

- one of the Amorite chiefs who aided Abraham in the pursuit of the 4 invading kings (noun proper masculine)
- a Levitical city west of the Jordan in Manasseh allotted to the Kohathite Levites (noun proper locative)
