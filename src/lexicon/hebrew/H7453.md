# H7453 רֵעַ / רע

## rêaʻ

_(ray'-ah | RAY-ah | RAY-ah)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or רֵיעַ; from [[H7462]]; an associate (more or less close); brother, companion, fellow, friend, husband, lover, neighbour, [[lexicon-key|×]] (an-)other.

- friend, companion, fellow, another person
    - friend, intimate
    - fellow, fellow-citizen, another person (weaker sense)
    - other, another (reciprocal phrase)
