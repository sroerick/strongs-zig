# H6170 עֲרוּגָה / ערוגה

## ʻărûwgâh

_(ar-oo-gaw' | uh-roo-ɡAW | uh-roo-ɡA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; or עֲרֻגָה; feminine passive participle of [[H6165]]; something piled up (as if (figuratively) raised by mental aspiration), i.e., a paterre; bed, furrow.

- garden terrace or bed
