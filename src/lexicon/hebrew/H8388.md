# H8388 תָּאַר / תאר

## tâʼar

_(taw-ar' | taw-AR | ta-AR)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to delineate; reflexive to extend; be drawn, mark out, (Rimmon-)methoar (by union with [[H7417]]).

- (Qal) to be drawn, incline, delineate, extend
    - meaning dubious
- (Piel) to draw in outline, trace out
