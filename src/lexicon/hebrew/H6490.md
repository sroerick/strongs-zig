# H6490 פִּקּוּד / פקוד

## piqqûwd

_(pik-kood' | pik-KOOD | pee-KOOD)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or פִּקֻּד; from [[H6485]]; properly, appointed, i.e., a mandate (of God; plural only, collectively, for the Law); commandment, precept, statute.

- precept, statute
