# H5198 נָטָף / נטף

## nâṭâph

_(naw-tawf' | naw-TAWP | na-TAHF)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H5197]]; a drop; specifically, an aromatic gum (probably stacte); drop, stacte.

- drop
- gum, drops of stacte
    - an aromatic gum resin of a shrub used in incense
