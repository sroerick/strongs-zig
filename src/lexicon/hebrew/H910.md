# H910 בָּדָד / בדד

## bâdâd

_(baw-dawd' | baw-DAWD | ba-DAHD)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H909]]; separate; adverb, separately; alone, desolate, only, solitary.

- isolation, withdrawal, separation (noun masculine)
    - an isolated city
- alone (adverb)
    - security (figuratively)
