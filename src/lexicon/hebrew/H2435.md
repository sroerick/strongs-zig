# H2435 חִיצוֹן / חיצון

## chîytsôwn

_(khee-tsone' | hee-TSONE | hee-TSONE)_

### Definition

adjective; from [[H2434]]; properly, the (outer) wall side; hence, exterior; figuratively, secular (as opposed to sacred); outer, outward, utter, without.

- outer, external, outward
