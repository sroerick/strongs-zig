# H6017 עֲמֹרָה / עמרה

## ʻĂmôrâh

_(am-o-raw' | uh-moh-RAW | uh-moh-RA)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; submersion; from [[H6014]]; a (ruined) heap; Amorah, a place in Palestine; Gomorrah.

- the twin-city in evil with Sodom, both destroyed in judgment by God with fire from heaven
    - of iniquity (figuratively)
