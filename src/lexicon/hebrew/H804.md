# H804 אַשּׁוּר / אשור

## ʼAshshûwr

_(ash-shoor' | ash-SHOOR | ah-SHOOR)_

### Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; a step; or אַשֻּׁר; apparently from [[H833]] (in the sense of successful); Ashshur, the second son of Shem; also his descendants and the country occupied by them (i.e., Assyria), its region and its empire; Asshur, Assur, Assyria, Assyrians.

- the second son of Shem, eponymous ancestor of the Assyrians (noun proper masculine)
- the people of Assyria (noun proper masculine)
- the nation, Assyria (noun proper locative)
- the land, Assyria or Asshur (noun proper locative)
