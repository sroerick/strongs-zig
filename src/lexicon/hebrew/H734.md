# H734 אֹרַח / ארח

## ʼôrach

_(o'-rakh | oh-RA | oh-RAHK)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H732]]; a well-trodden road (literally or figuratively); also a caravan; manner, path, race, rank, traveller, troop, (by-, high-)way.

- way, path
    - path, road
    - the path, way, passing of life (figuratively)
    - way of living (figuratively)
    - traveller, wayfarer (metonymy)
