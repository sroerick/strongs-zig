# H7679 שָׂגָא / שגא

## sâgâʼ

_(saw-gaw' | saw-ɡAW | sa-ɡA)_

### Definition

verb;  a primitive root; to grow, i.e., (causatively) to enlarge, (figuratively) laud; increase, magnify.

- to increase, grow, magnify, grow great
    - (Hiphil)
        - to make great
        - to magnify, laud
