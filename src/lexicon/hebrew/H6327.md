# H6327 פּוּץ / פוץ

## pûwts

_(poots | poots | poots)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to dash in pieces, literally or figuratively (especially to disperse); break (dash, shake) in (to) pieces, cast (abroad), disperse (selves), drive, retire, scatter (abroad), spread abroad.

- to scatter, be dispersed, be scattered
    - (Qal) to be dispersed, be scattered
    - (Niphal)
        - to be scattered
        - to be spread abroad
    - (Hiphil) to scatter
    - Hithpael) scatter
- (Qal) to flow, overflow
- to break
    - (Polel) to shatter
    - (Pilpel) to dash to pieces
