# H4795 מָרוּק / מרוק

## mârûwq

_(maw-rook' | maw-ROOK | ma-ROOK)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H4838]]; properly, rubbed; but used abstractly, a rubbing (with perfumery); purification.

- scraping, rubbing
    - of year of preparation of girls for the harem; bodily rubbings
