# H863 אִתַּי / אתי

## ʼIttay

_(it-tah'ee | it-TAI | ee-TAI)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; with me; or אִיתַי; from [[H854]]; near; Ittai or Ithai, the name of a Gittite and of an Israelite; Ithai, Ittai.

- a Gittite commander from Gath in David's army
- one of David's 30 mighty warriors, a Benjamite
