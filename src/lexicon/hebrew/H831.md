# H831 אַשְׁקְלוֹן / אשקלון

## ʼAshqᵉlôwn

_(ash-kel-one' | ash-keh-LONE | ash-keh-LONE)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; the fire of infamy, I shall be weighed; probably from [[H8254]] in the sense of weighing-place (i.e., mart); Ashkelon, a place in Palestine; Ashkelon, Askalon.

- a maritime city of the Philistines, southwest of Jerusalem
