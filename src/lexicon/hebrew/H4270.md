# H4270 מַחְסוֹר / מחסור

## machçôwr

_(makh-sore' | ma-SORE | mahk-SORE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or מַחְסֹר; from [[H2637]]; deficiency; hence, impoverishment; lack, need, penury, poor, poverty, want.

- need, poverty, thing needed
    - need, thing needed
    - lack, want
    - need, poverty
