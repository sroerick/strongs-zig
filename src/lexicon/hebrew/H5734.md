# H5734 עַדְנָה / עדנה

## ʻAdnâh

_(ad-naw' | ad-NAW | ad-NA)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; pleasure; from [[H5727]]; pleasure; Adnah, the name of two Israelites; Adnah.

- a Manassite who deserted from Saul and joined David at Ziklag
- a captain in charge of over 300,000 men of Judah in the time of Jehoshaphat
