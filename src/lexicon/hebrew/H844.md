# H844 אַשְׂרִיאֵל / אשריאל

## ʼAsrîyʼêl

_(as-ree-ale' | as-ree-ALE | as-ree-ALE)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; I shall be prince of God; an orthographical variation for [[H840]]; Asriel, the name of two Israelites; Ashriel, Asriel.

- a great-grandson of Manasseh, and son of Gilead
- a son of Manasseh
