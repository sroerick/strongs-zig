# H7802 שׁוּשַׁן עֵדוּת / שושן עדות

## Shûwshan ʻÊdûwth

_(shoo-shan' ay-dooth' | shoo-SHAHN ay-DOOT | shoo-SHAHN ay-DOOT)_

### Definition

masculine noun; lilies; or (plural of former) שׁוֹשַׁנִּים עֵדוּת; from [[H7799]] and [[H5715]]; lily (or trumpet) of assemblage; Shushan-Eduth or Shoshannim-Eduth, the title of a popular song; Shoshannim-Eduth, Shushan-eduth.

- instruction in the title of [Psa 60:1-12]('- - 1-12') and Psalm 80
    - meaning uncertain
