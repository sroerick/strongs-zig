# H1541 גְּלָה / גלה

## gᵉlâh

_(ghel-aw' | ɡeh-LAW | ɡeh-LA)_

### Definition

verb; or גְּלָא; (Aramaic), corresponding to [[H1540]]; to reveal, to carry away; bring over, carry away, reveal.

- to reveal
    - (Peal) to reveal (secrets), be revealed
    - (Aphel) to take into exile
