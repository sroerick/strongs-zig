# H7619 שְׁבוּאֵל / שבואל

## Shᵉbûwʼêl

_(sheb-oo-ale' | sheh-boo-ALE | sheh-voo-ALE)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; captive of God; or שׁוּבָאֵל; from [[H7617]] (abbreviated form) or [[H7725]] and [[H410]]; captive (or returned) of God; Shebuel or Shubael, the name of two Israelites; Shebuel, Shubael.

- son of Gershom and grandson of Moses
- one of the 14 sons of Heman the musician
