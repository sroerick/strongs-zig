# H54 אֶבְיָתָר / אביתר

## ʼEbyâthâr

_(ab-yaw-thawr' | eb-yaw-TAWR | ev-ya-TAHR)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; my father is great; contracted from [[H1]] and [[H3498]]; father of abundance (i.e., liberal); Ebjathar, an Israelite; Abiathar.

- priest, son of Ahitub (Ahimelech), faithful to David, but later rebelled with Adonijah
