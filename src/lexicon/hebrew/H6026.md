# H6026 עָנַג / ענג

## ʻânag

_(aw-nag' | aw-NAHɡ | ah-NAHɡ)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to be soft or pliable, i.e., (figuratively) effeminate or luxurious; delicate(-ness), (have) delight (self), sport self.

- to be soft, be delicate, be dainty
    - (Pual) to be delicate
    - (Hithpael)
        - to be of dainty habit, be pampered
        - to be happy about, take exquisite delight
        - to make merry over, make sport of
