# H8081 שֶׁמֶן / שמן

## shemen

_(sheh'-men | sheh-MEN | sheh-MEN)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H8080]]; grease, especially liquid (as from the olive, often perfumed); figuratively, richness; anointing, [[lexicon-key|×]] fat (things), [[lexicon-key|×]] fruitful, oil(-ed), ointment, olive, [[lexicon-key|+]] pine.

- fat, oil
    - fat, fatness
    - oil, olive oil
        - as staple, medicament or unguent
        - for anointing
    - fat (of fruitful land, valleys) (metaphorically)
