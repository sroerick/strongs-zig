# H1570 גָּלַשׁ / גלש

## gâlash

_(gaw-lash' | ɡaw-LAHSH | ɡa-LAHSH)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; probably to caper (as a goat); appear.

- to sit, sit up, (possibly also) recline
    - (Qal)
        - of flocks of goats
        - of a woman's hair (simile)
