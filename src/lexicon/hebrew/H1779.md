# H1779 דִּין / דין

## dîyn

_(deen | deen | deen)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or (Job 19:29) דּוּן; from [[H1777]]; judgement (the suit, justice, sentence or tribunal); by implication also strife; cause, judgement, plea, strife.

- judgment
    - judgment
    - cause, plea
    - condemnation, judgment
    - dispute, legal suit, strife
    - government
