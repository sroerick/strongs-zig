# H5809 עַזּוּר / עזור

## ʻAzzûwr

_(az-zoor' | adz-ZOOR | ah-ZOOR)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; he that assists; or עַזֻּר; from [[H5826]]; helpful; Azzur, the name of three Israelites; Azur, Azzur.

- a Benjamite of Gibeon, father of the false prophet Hananiah
- father of Jaazaniah, one of the princes against whom Ezekiel was commanded to prophecy
