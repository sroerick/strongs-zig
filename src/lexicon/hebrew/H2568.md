# H2568 חָמֵשׁ / חמש

## châmêsh

_(khaw-maysh' | haw-MAYSH | ha-MAYSH)_

### Definition

noun; masculine חֲמִשָּׁה; a primitive numeral; five; fif(-teen), fifth, five ([[lexicon-key|×]] apiece).

- five
    - five (cardinal number)
    - a multiple of five (with another number)
    - fifth (ordinal number)
