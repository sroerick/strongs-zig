# H5595 סָפָה / ספה

## çâphâh

_(saw-faw' | saw-PAW | sa-FA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to scrape (literally, to shave; but usually figuratively) together (i.e., to accumulate or increase) or away (i.e., to scatter, remove, or ruin; intransitively, to perish); add, augment, consume, destroy, heap, join, perish, put.

- to sweep or snatch away, catch up, destroy, consume
    - (Qal)
        - to be snatched away
        - to sweep or snatch away
    - (Niphal)
        - to be swept away, be destroyed
        - to be caught up, be captured
    - (Hiphil) to catch up, gather
