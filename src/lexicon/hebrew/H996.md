# H996 בֵּין / בין

## bêyn

_(bane | bane | bane)_

### Definition

preposition; (sometimes in the plural masculine or feminine); properly, the constructive form of an otherwise unused noun from [[H995]]; a distinction; but used only as a preposition; between (repeated before each noun, often with other particles); also as a conjunction, either...or; among, asunder, at, be(-tween, -twixt...and), [[lexicon-key|+]] from (the widest), [[lexicon-key|×]] in, out of, whether (it be...or), within.

- between, among, in the midst of (with other preps), from between
