# H5737 עֲדַר / עדר

## ʻădar

_(aw-dar' | uh-DAHR | uh-DAHR)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to arrange, as a battle, a vineyard (to hoe); hence, to muster and so to miss (or find wanting); dig, fail, keep (rank), lack.

- to help
    - (Qal) to help
- to hoe
    - (Niphal) to be hoed
- to be lacking, fail
    - (Niphal)
        - to be lacking
        - to leave lacking
    - (Piel) to leave lacking
