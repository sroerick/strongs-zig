# H8141 שָׁנֶה / שנה

## shâneh

_(shaw-neh' | shaw-NEH | sha-NEH)_

### Definition

feminine noun; (in plural or (feminine) שָׁנָה; from [[H8138]]; a year (as a revolution of time); [[lexicon-key|+]] whole age, [[lexicon-key|×]] long, [[lexicon-key|+]] old, year, [[lexicon-key|×]] yearly.

- year
    - as division of time
    - as measure of time
    - as indication of age
    - a lifetime (of years of life)
