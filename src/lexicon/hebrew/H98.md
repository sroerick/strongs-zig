# H98 אֲגַם / אגם

## ʼăgam

_(ag-am' | uh-ɡAHM | uh-ɡAHM)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from an unused root (meaning to collect as water); a marsh; hence a rush (as growing in swamps); hence a stockade of reeds; pond, pool, standing (water).

- pool, troubled pool
    - troubled or muddy (gloomy) pools, marshes
    - any pool, pond
    - swamp reeds, reeds, rush(es)
