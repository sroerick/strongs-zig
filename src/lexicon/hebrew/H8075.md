# H8075 שְׁמַם / שמם

## shᵉmam

_(shem-am' | sheh-MAHM | sheh-MAHM)_

### Definition

verb; corresponding to [[H8074]]; to stun (or intransitively, grow numb), i.e., devastate or (figuratively) stupefy (both usually in a passive sense); be astonied.

- (Ithpolel) \[[[lexicon-key|CLBL]]\] to be appalled, be stunned
