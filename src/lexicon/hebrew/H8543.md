# H8543 תְּמוֹל / תמול

## tᵉmôwl

_(tem-ole' | teh-MOLE | teh-MOLE)_

### Definition

adverb; or תְּמֹל; probably for [[H865]]; sometimes with [[H8032]]; properly, ago, i.e., a (short or long) time since; especially yesterday, or day before yesterday; [[lexicon-key|+]] before(-time), [[lexicon-key|+]] these (three) days, [[lexicon-key|+]] heretofore, [[lexicon-key|+]] time past, yesterday.

- before, before that time, beforetime, heretofore, of late, of old, these days, time(s) past, yesterday, day before yesterday, recently, formerly
    - yesterday
        - recently, formerly (usual usage) (figuratively)
    - as formerly, so more recently
    - from yesterday, already
