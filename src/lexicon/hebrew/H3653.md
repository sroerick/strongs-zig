# H3653 כֵּן / כן

## kên

_(kane | kane | kane)_

### Definition

masculine noun; the same as [[H3651]], used as a noun; a stand, i.e., pedestal or station; base, estate, foot, office, place, well.

- base, stand, pedestal, office, foot, place, estate
    - base, pedestal
    - office, place
