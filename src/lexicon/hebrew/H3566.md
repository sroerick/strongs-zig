# H3566 כּוֹרֶשׁ / כורש

## Kôwresh

_(ko'-resh | koh-RESH | koh-RESH)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; posses thou the furnace; or (Ezra 1:1 (last time),2) כֹּרֶשׁ; from the Persian; Koresh (or Cyrus), the Persian king; Cyrus.

- the king of Persia and conqueror of Babylon; first ruler of Persia to make a decree allowing the Israelite exiles to return to Jerusalem
