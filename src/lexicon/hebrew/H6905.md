# H6905 קָבָל / קבל

## qâbâl

_(kaw-bawl' | kaw-BAWL | ka-VAHL)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H6901]] in the sense of opposite (see [[H6904]]); the presence, i.e., (adverbially) in front of; before.

- something in front, attacking engine, something before (noun masculine)
    - before
    - siege-engine, battering-ram
- ([[lexicon-key|CLBL]]) before (adverb)
