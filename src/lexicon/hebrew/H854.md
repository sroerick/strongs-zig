# H854 אֵת / את

## ʼêth

_(ayth | ate | ate)_

### Definition

preposition; probably from [[H579]]; properly, nearness (used only as a preposition or an adverb), near; hence, generally, with, by, at, among, etc; against, among, before, by, for, from, in(-to), (out) of, with.

- with, near, together with
    - with, together with
    - with (of relationship)
    - near (of place)
    - with (poss.)
    - from...with, from (with other preposition)


### Note

Often with another prepositional prefix.
