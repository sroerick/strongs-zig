# H7225 רֵאשִׁית / ראשית

## rêʼshîyth

_(ray-sheeth' | ray-SHEET | ray-SHEET)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from the same as [[H7218]]; the first, in place, time, order or rank (specifically, a firstfruit); beginning, chief(-est), first(-fruits) (part, time), principal thing.

- first, beginning, best, chief
    - beginning
    - first
    - chief
    - choice part
