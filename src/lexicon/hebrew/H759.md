# H759 אַרְמוֹן / ארמון

## ʼarmôwn

_(ar-mone' | ar-MONE | ar-MONE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from an unused root (meaning to be elevated); a citadel (from its height); castle, palace.

- citadel, palace, fortress
