# H6004 עָמַם / עמם

## ʻâmam

_(aw-mam' | aw-MAHM | ah-MAHM)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to associate; by implication, to overshadow (by huddling together); become dim, hide.

- to dim, darken, grow dark
    - (Qal) to dim, eclipse, be held dark
    - (Hophal) to be dimmed, grow dark
