# H8646 תֶּרַח / תרח

## Terach

_(teh'-rakh | teh-RA | teh-RAHK)_

### Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; station, delay; of uncertain derivation; Terach, the father of Abraham; also a place in the Desert; Tarah, Terah.

- the father of Abraham (noun proper masculine)
- a station of Israel in the wilderness (noun proper locative)


### Note

Terah means "station," and Tarah means "delay."
