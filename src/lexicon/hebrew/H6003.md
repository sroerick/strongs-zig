# H6003 עֲמָלֵקִי / עמלקי

## ʻĂmâlêqîy

_(am-aw-lay-kee' | uh-maw-lay-KEE | uh-ma-lay-KEE)_

### Definition

adjective; people of lapping; patronymically from [[H6002]]; an Amalekite (or collectively the Amalekites) or descendants of Amalek; Amalekite(-s).

- descendants of Amalek, the grandson of Esau
