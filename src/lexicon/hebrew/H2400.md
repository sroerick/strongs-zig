# H2400 חַטָּא / חטא

## chaṭṭâʼ

_(khat-taw' | haht-TAW | ha-TA)_

### Definition

masculine noun; intensively from [[H2398]]; a criminal, or one accounted guilty; offender, sinful, sinner.

- sinners (noun masculine)
- sinful (adjective)
- exposed to condemnation, reckoned as offenders (adjective)
