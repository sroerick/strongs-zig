# H1926 הָדָר / הדר

## hâdâr

_(haw-dawr' | haw-DAWR | ha-DAHR)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H1921]]; magnificence, i.e., ornament or splendor; beauty, comeliness, excellency, glorious, glory, goodly, honour, majesty.

- ornament, splendour, honour
    - ornament
    - splendour, majesty
    - honour, glory
