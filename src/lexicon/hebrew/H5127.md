# H5127 נוּס / נוס

## nûwç

_(noos | noos | noos)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to flit, i.e., vanish away (subside, escape; causatively, chase, impel, deliver); [[lexicon-key|×]] abate, away, be displayed, (make to) flee (away, -ing), put to flight, [[lexicon-key|×]] hide, lift up a standard.

- to flee, escape
    - (Qal)
        - to flee
        - to escape
        - to take flight, depart, disappear
        - to fly (to the attack) on horseback
    - (Polel) to drive at
    - (Hithpolel) to take flight
    - (Hiphil)
        - to put to flight
        - to drive hastily
        - to cause to disappear, hide
