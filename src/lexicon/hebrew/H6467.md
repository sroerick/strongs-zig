# H6467 פֹּעַל / פעל

## pôʻal

_(po'-al | poh-AL | poh-AL)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H6466]]; an act or work (concretely); act, deed, do, getting, maker, work.

- work, deed, doing
    - deed, thing done
    - work, thing made
    - wages of work
    - acquisition (of treasure)
