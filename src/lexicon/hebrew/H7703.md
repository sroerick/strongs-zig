# H7703 שָׁדַד / שדד

## shâdad

_(shaw-dad' | shaw-DAHD | sha-DAHD)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to be burly, i.e., (figuratively) powerful (passively, impregnable); by implication, to ravage; dead, destroy(-er), oppress, robber, spoil(-er), [[lexicon-key|×]] utterly, (lay) waste.

- to deal violently with, despoil, devastate, ruin, destroy, spoil
    - (Qal)
        - to violently destroy, devastate, despoil, assail
        - devastator, despoiler (participle) (substantive)
    - (Niphal) to be utterly ruined
    - (Piel)
        - to assault
        - to devastate
    - (Pual) to be devastated
    - (Poel) to violently destroy
    - (Hophal) to be devastated
