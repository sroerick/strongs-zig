# H3313 יָפַע / יפע

## yâphaʻ

_(yaw-fah' | yaw-PA | ya-FA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to shine; be light, shew self, (cause to) shine (forth).

- to shine, shine forth or out, cause to shine, send out beams
    - (Hiphil)
        - to shine out, shine forth, send out beams
        - to cause to shine
