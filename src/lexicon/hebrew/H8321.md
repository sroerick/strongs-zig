# H8321 שֹׂרֵק / שרק

## sôrêq

_(so-rake' | soh-RAKE | soh-RAKE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or שׂוֹרֵק; and (feminine) שֹׂרֵקָה; from [[H8319]] in the sense of redness (compare [[H8320]]); a vine stock (properly, one yielding purple grapes, the richest variety); choice(-st, noble) wine.

- choice species of vine, choice grapes


### Note

Compare [[H8291]].
