# H5356 נִקָּיוֹן / נקיון

## niqqâyôwn

_(nik-kaw-yone' | nik-kaw-YONE | nee-ka-YONE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or נִקָּיֹן; from [[H5352]]; clearness (literally or figuratively); cleanness, innocency.

- innocency
    - freedom from guilt, innocency
    - freedom from punishment
    - cleanness of teeth (physical sense)
