# H6202 עָרַף / ערף

## ʻâraph

_(aw-raf' | aw-RAHP | ah-RAHF)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root (identical with through the idea of slopping); properly, to bend downward; but used only as a denominative from [[H6203]]; to break the neck; hence (figuratively) to destroy; that is beheaded, break down, break (cut off, strike off) neck.

- (Qal) to break the neck (of an animal)
