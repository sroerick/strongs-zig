# H6270 עַתְלַי / עתלי

## ʻAthlay

_(ath-lah'ee | at-LAI | at-LAI)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; whom Jehovah afflicts; from an unused root meaning to compress; constringent; Athlai, an Israelite; Athlai.

- one of the sons of Bebai and one who put away his foreign wife in the time of Ezra
