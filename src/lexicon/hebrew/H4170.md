# H4170 מוֹקֵשׁ / מוקש

## môwqêsh

_(mo-kashe' | moh-KAYSH | moh-KAYSH)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or מֹקֵשׁ; from [[H3369]]; a noose (for catching animals) (literally or figuratively); by implication, a hook (for the nose); be ensnared, gin, (is) snare(-d), trap.

- bait, lure, snare
