# H7210 רֳאִי / ראי

## rŏʼîy

_(ro-ee' | roh-EE | roh-EE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H7200]]; sight, whether abstractly (vision) or concretely (a spectacle); gazingstock, look to, (that) see(-th).

- looking, appearance, seeing, sight
    - seeing
    - appearance
    - sight, spectacle
