# H5801 עִזָּבוֹן / עזבון

## ʻizzâbôwn

_(iz-zaw-bone' | idz-zaw-BONE | ee-za-VONE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H5800]] in the sense of letting go (for a price, i.e., selling); trade, i.e., the place (mart) or the payment (revenue); fair, ware.

- wares, goods
