# H6087 עָצַב / עצב

## ʻâtsab

_(aw-tsab' | aw-TSAHB | ah-TSAHV)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to carve, i.e., fabricate or fashion; displease, grieve, hurt, make, be sorry, vex, worship, wrest.

- to hurt, pain, grieve, displease, vex, wrest
    - (Qal) to hurt, pain
    - (Niphal) to be in pain, be pained, be grieved
    - (Piel) to vex, torture
    - (Hiphil) to cause pain
    - (Hithpael) to feel grieved, be vexed
- to shape, fashion, make, form, stretch into shape, ([[lexicon-key|TWOT]]) worship
    - (Piel) to shape, form
    - (Hiphil) to form, copy, fashion
