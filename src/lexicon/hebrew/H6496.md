# H6496 פָּקִיד / פקיד

## pâqîyd

_(paw-keed' | paw-KEED | pa-KEED)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H6485]]; a superintendent (civil, military or religious); which had the charge, governor, office, overseer, (that) was set.

- commissioner, deputy, overseer, officer
