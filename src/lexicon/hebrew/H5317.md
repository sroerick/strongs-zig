# H5317 נֹפֶת / נפת

## nôpheth

_(no'-feth | noh-PET | noh-FET)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H5130]] in the sense of shaking to pieces; a dripping i.e., of honey (from the comb); honeycomb.

- flowing honey, honey from the comb, a dropping down, honey, honeycomb
