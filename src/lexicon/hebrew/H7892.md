# H7892 שִׁיר / שיר

## shîyr

_(sheer | sheer | sheer)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or feminine שִׁירָה; from [[H7891]]; a song; abstractly, singing; musical(-ick), [[lexicon-key|×]] sing(-er, -ing), song.

- song (noun masculine)
    - lyric song
    - religious song
    - song of Levitical choirs
- song (noun feminine)
    - song, ode
