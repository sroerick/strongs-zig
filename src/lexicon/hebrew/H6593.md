# H6593 פִּשְׁתֶּה / פשתה

## pishteh

_(pish-teh' | pish-TEH | peesh-TEH)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from the same as [[H6580]] as in the sense of comminuting; linen (i.e., the thread, as carded); flax, linen.

- flax, linen
    - fibre used as material for garments
