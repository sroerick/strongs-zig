# H5433 סָבָא / סבא

## çâbâʼ

_(saw-baw' | saw-BAW | sa-VA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to quaff to satiety, i.e., become tipsy; drunkard, fill self, Sabean, (wine-)bibber.

- to drink heavily or largely, imbibe
    - (Qal)
        - to imbibe
        - wine-bibber, drunkard (participle) (substantive)
