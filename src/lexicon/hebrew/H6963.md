# H6963 קוֹל / קול

## qôwl

_(kole | kole | kole)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or קֹל; from an unused root meaning to call aloud; a voice or sound; [[lexicon-key|+]] aloud, bleating, crackling, cry ([[lexicon-key|+]] out), fame, lightness, lowing, noise, [[lexicon-key|+]] hold peace, (pro-)claim, proclamation, [[lexicon-key|+]] sing, sound, [[lexicon-key|+]] spark, thunder(-ing), voice, [[lexicon-key|+]] yell.

- voice, sound, noise
    - voice
    - sound (of instrument)
- lightness, frivolity
