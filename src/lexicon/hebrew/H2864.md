# H2864 חָתַר / חתר

## châthar

_(khaw-thar' | haw-TAHR | ha-TAHR)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to force a passage, as by burglary; figuratively, with oars; dig (through), row.

- to dig, row
    - (Qal)
        - to dig (into houses - of burglary)
        - to row (in water)
