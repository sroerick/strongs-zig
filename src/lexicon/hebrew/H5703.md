# H5703 עַד / עד

## ʻad

_(ad | ad | ad)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H5710]]; properly, a (peremptory) terminus, i.e., (by implication) duration, in the sense of advance or perpetuity (substantially as a noun, either with or without a preposition); eternity, ever(-lasting, -more), old, perpetually, [[lexicon-key|+]] world without end.

- perpetuity, for ever, continuing future
    - ancient (of past time)
    - for ever (of future time)
        - of continuous existence
    - for ever (of God's existence)
