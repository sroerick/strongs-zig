# H7155 קִרְיַת חֻצוֹת / קרית חצות

## Qiryath Chutsôwth

_(keer-yath' khoo-tsoth' | kir-YAHT hoo-TSOTE | keer-YAHT hoo-TSOTE)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; city of streets; from [[H7151]] and the feminine plural of [[H2351]]; city of streets; Kirjath-Chutsoth, a place in Moab; Kirjath-huzoth.

- a city in Moab
