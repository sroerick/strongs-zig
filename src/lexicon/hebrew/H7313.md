# H7313 רוּם / רום

## rûwm

_(room | room | room)_

### Definition

verb; corresponding to [[H7311]]; to be high actively, to rise or raise (in various applications, literally or figuratively); extol, lift up (self), set up.

- to rise
    - (Peal) to be lifted up
    - (Aphel) to exalt
    - (Hithpolel) to lift oneself up
