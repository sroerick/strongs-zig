# H5627 סָרָה / סרה

## çârâh

_(saw-raw' | saw-RAW | sa-RA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from [[H5493]]; apostasy, crime; figuratively, remission; [[lexicon-key|×]] continual, rebellion, revolt(-ed), turn away, wrong.

- apostasy, defection, turning aside, withdrawal
    - defection (of moral or legal offenses)
    - apostasy
    - withdrawal (negated)
