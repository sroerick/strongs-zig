# H7966 שִׁלּוּם / שלום

## shillûwm

_(shil-loom' | shil-LOOM | shee-LOOM)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or שִׁלֻּם; from [[H7999]]; a requital, i.e., (secure) retribution, (venal) a fee; recompense, reward.

- requital, reward
    - requital, retribution
    - reward, bribe
