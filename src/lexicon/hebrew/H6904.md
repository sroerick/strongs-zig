# H6904 קֹבֶל / קבל

## qôbel

_(ko'-bel | koh-BEL | koh-VEL)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H6901]] in the sense of confronting (as standing opposite in order to receive); a battering-ram; war.

- something in front, attacking engine, something before
    - before
    - siege-engine, battering-ram
