# H7293 רַהַב / רהב

## rahab

_(rah'-hab | ra-HAHB | ra-HAHV)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H7292]]; bluster(-er); proud, strength.

- pride, blusterer
    - storm, arrogance (but only as names)
        - mythical sea monster
        - emblematic name of Egypt
