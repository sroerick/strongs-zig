# H5874 עֵין־דֹּאר / עיןדאר

## ʻÊyn-Dôʼr

_(ane-dore' | ane dore | ane dore)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; fountain of Dor; or עֵין דּוֹר; or עֵין־דֹּר; from [[H5869]] and [[H1755]]; fountain of dwelling; En-Dor, a place in Palestine; En-dor.

- a place in the territory of Issachar yet possessed by Manasseh; located 4 miles (- 5 kilometers) north of Tabor
    - place of residence of the spiritist consulted by king Saul
