# H5533 סָכַן / סכן

## çâkan

_(saw-kan' | saw-KAHN | sa-HAHN)_

### Definition

verb; probably a denominative from [[H7915]]; properly, to cut, i.e., damage; also to grow (causatively, make) poor; endanger, impoverish.

- to incur danger
    - (Niphal) to endanger oneself
- to be poor
    - (Pual) to be impoverished
