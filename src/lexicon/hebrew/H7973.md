# H7973 שֶׁלַח / שלח

## shelach

_(sheh'-lakh | sheh-LA | sheh-LAHK)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H7971]]; a missile of attack, i.e., spear; also (figuratively) a shoot of growth; i.e., branch; dart, plant, [[lexicon-key|×]] put off, sword, weapon.

- weapon, missile, sprout
    - missile, weapon
    - sprout, shoot
