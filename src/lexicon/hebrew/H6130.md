# H6130 עָקָן / עקן

## ʻÂqân

_(aw-kawn' | aw-KAWN | ah-KAHN)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; sharp-sighted; from an unused root meaning to twist; tortuous; Akan, an Idummaean; Akan.

- son of Ezer, descendant of Seir, and one of the chiefs of the Horites of Edom


### Note

Compare [[H3292]].
