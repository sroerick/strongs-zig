# H8255 שֶׁקֶל / שקל

## sheqel

_(sheh'-kel | sheh-KEL | sheh-KEL)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H8254]]; probably a weight; used as a commercial standard; shekel.

- shekel
    - the chief unit of weight or measure
        - silver - 1/3000 of a talent and equal to 132 grains
        - copper - 1/1500 of a talent and equal to 528 grains
