# H707 אָרַג / ארג

## ʼârag

_(aw-rag' | aw-RAHɡ | ah-RAHɡ)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to plait or weave; weaver(-r).

- to weave
    - (Qal)
        - to weave (cloth)
        - to braid (Samson's hair)
        - weaver (substantive)
        - intrigue (figuratively)
