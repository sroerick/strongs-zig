# H6301 פְּדָהצוּר / פדהצור

## Pᵉdâhtsûwr

_(ped-aw-tsoor' | peh-doh-TSOOR | peh-doh-TSOOR)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; the Rock has ransomed; from [[H6299]] and [[H6697]]; a rock (i.e., God) has ransomed; Pedahtsur, an Israelite; Pedahzur.

- father of Gamaliel who was the chief of the tribe of Manasseh at the time of the exodus
