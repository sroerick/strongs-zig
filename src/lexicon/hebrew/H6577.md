# H6577 פַּרְשַׁנְדָּתָא / פרשנדתא

## Parshandâthâʼ

_(par-shan-daw-thaw' | pahr-shahn-daw-TAW | pahr-shahn-da-TA)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; given by prayer; of Persian origin; Parshandatha, a son of Haman; Parshandatha.

- one of the 10 sons of Haman the enemy of Mordecai and queen Esther
