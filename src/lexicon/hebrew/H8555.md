# H8555 תִּמְנָע / תמנע

## Timnâʻ

_(tim-naw' | tim-NAW | teem-NA)_

### Definition

proper feminine noun, proper masculine noun; restrained; from [[H4513]]; restraint; Timna, the name of two Edomites; Timna, Timnah.

- a chief or duke of Esau and of Edom (noun proper masculine)
- the concubine of Eliphaz, the son of Esau (noun proper feminine)
- daughter of Seir the Horite and sister of Lotan (noun proper feminine)
