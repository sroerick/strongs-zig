# H7887 שִׁילֹה / שילה

## Shîylôh

_(shee-lo' | shee-LOH | shee-LOH)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; place of rest; or שִׁלֹה; or שִׁילוֹ; or שִׁלוֹ; from the same as [[H7886]]; Shiloh, a place in Palestine; Shiloh.

- a city in Ephraim and temporary home of the Ark of the Covenant and the Tabernacle, the place where Samuel grew up
