# H7211 רְאָיָה / ראיה

## Rᵉʼâyâh

_(reh-aw-yaw' | reh-aw-YAW | reh-ah-YA)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; Jehovah has seen; from [[H7200]] and [[H3050]]; Jah has seen; Reajah, the name of three Israelites; Reaia, Reaiah.

- a descendant of Shobal the son of Judah
- a Reubenite, son of Micah and father of Baal
- a progenitor of a family of temple slaves who returned from exile with Zerubbabel
