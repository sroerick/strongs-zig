# H7621 שְׁבוּעָה / שבועה

## shᵉbûwʻâh

_(sheb-oo-aw' | sheh-boo-AW | sheh-voo-AH)_

### Definition

feminine noun; feminine passive participle of [[H7650]]; properly, something sworn, i.e., an oath; curse, oath, [[lexicon-key|×]] sworn.

- oath, curse
    - oath
        - attesting of innocence
        - curse
    - oath (of Jehovah)
