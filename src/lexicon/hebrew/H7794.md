# H7794 שׁוֹר / שור

## shôwr

_(shore | shore | shore)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H7788]]; a bullock (as a traveller); bull(-ock), cow, ox, wall (by mistake for [[H7791]]).

- ox, bull, a head of cattle
    - for plowing, for food, as sacrifice
