# H6738 צֵל / צל

## tsêl

_(tsale | tsale | tsale)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H6751]]; shade, whether literal or figurative; defence, shade(-ow).

- shadow, shade
    - shadow (on dial)
    - shadow, shade (as protection)
    - shadow (symbolic of transitoriness of life)
