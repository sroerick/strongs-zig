# H7096 קָצָה / קצה

## qâtsâh

_(kaw-tsaw' | kaw-TSAW | ka-TSA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to cut off; (figuratively) to destroy; (partially) to scrape off; cut off, cut short, scrape (off).

- to cut off
    - (Qal) to cut off
    - (Piel) to cut off
    - (Hiphil) to scrape, scrape off
