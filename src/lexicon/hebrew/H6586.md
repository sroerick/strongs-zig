# H6586 פָּשַׁע / פשע

## pâshaʻ

_(paw-shah' | paw-SHA | pa-SHA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root (identical with through the idea of expansion); to break away (from just authority), i.e., trespass, apostatize, quarrel; offend, rebel, revolt, transgress(-ion, -or).

- to rebel, transgress, revolt
    - (Qal)
        - to rebel, revolt
        - to transgress
    - (Niphal) to be rebelled against
