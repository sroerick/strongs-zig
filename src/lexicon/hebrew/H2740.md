# H2740 חָרוֹן / חרון

## chârôwn

_(khaw-rone' | haw-RONE | ha-RONE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or (shortened) חָרֹן; from [[H2734]]; a burning of anger; sore displeasure, fierce(-ness), fury, (fierce) wrath(-ful).

- anger, heat, burning (of anger)
    - always used of God's anger
