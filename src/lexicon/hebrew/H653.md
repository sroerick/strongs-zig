# H653 אֲפֵלָה / אפלה

## ʼăphêlâh

_(af-ay-law' | uh-pay-LAW | uh-fay-LA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; feminine of [[H651]]; duskiness, figuratively, misfortune; concrete, concealment; dark, darkness, gloominess, [[lexicon-key|×]] thick.

- darkness, gloominess, calamity
- wickedness (figuratively)
