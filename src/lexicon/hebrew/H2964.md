# H2964 טֶרֶף / טרף

## ṭereph

_(teh'-ref | teh-REP | teh-REF)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H2963]]; something torn, i.e., a fragment, e.g., a fresh leaf, prey, food; leaf, meat, prey, spoil.

- prey, food, leaf
    - prey
    - food
    - leaf
