# H6571 פָּרָשׁ / פרש

## pârâsh

_(paw-rawsh' | paw-ROHSH | pa-ROHSH)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H6567]]; (compare [[H5483]]); a steed (as stretched out to a vehicle, not single nor for mounting ); also (by implication) a driver (in a chariot), i.e., (collectively) cavalry; horseman.

- horse, steed, warhorse
- horseman
