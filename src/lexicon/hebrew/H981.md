# H981 בָּטָא / בטא

## bâṭâʼ

_(baw-taw' | baw-TAW | ba-TA)_

### Definition

verb; or בָּטָה; a primitive root; to babble; hence, to vociferate angrily; pronounce, speak (unadvisedly).

- to speak rashly or angrily, speak thoughtlessly
    - (Qal) one that babbles, speaks rashly (participle)
    - (Piel) to speak rashly, unadvisedly
