# H7662 שְׁבַק / שבק

## shᵉbaq

_(sheb-ak' | sheh-BAHK | sheh-VAHK)_

### Definition

verb; corresponding to the root of [[H7733]]; to quit, i.e., allow to remain; leave, let alone.

- to leave, let alone
    - (Peal) to leave, let alone
    - (Ithpael) to be left
