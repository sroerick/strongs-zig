# H1681 דִּבָּה / דבה

## dibbâh

_(dib-baw' | dib-BAW | dee-BA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from [[H1680]] (in the sense of furtive motion); slander; defaming, evil report, infamy, slander.

- whispering, defamation, evil report
    - whispering
    - defamation, defaming
    - evil report, unfavourable saying
