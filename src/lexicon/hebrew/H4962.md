# H4962 מַת / מת

## math

_(math | maht | maht)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from the same as [[H4970]]; properly, an adult (as of full length); by implication, a man (only in the plural); [[lexicon-key|+]] few, [[lexicon-key|×]] friends, men, persons, [[lexicon-key|×]] small.

- male, man
    - males, men
    - few men (in prose)
        - less emphasis on sex
    - men (poetic)
        - less emphasis on sex
