# H7964 שִׁלּוּחַ / שלוח

## shillûwach

_(shil-loo'-akh | shil-LOO-ah | shee-LOO-ak)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or שִׁלֻּחַ; from [[H7971]]; (only in plural) a dismissal, i.e., (of a wife) divorce (especially the document); also (of a daughter) dower; presents, have sent back.

- sending away, parting gift
    - sending away
    - parting gift
