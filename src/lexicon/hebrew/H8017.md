# H8017 שְׁלֻמִיאֵל / שלמיאל

## Shᵉlumîyʼêl

_(shel-oo-mee-ale' | sheh-loo-mee-ALE | sheh-loo-mee-ALE)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; friend of God; from [[H7965]] and [[H410]]; peace of God; Shelumiel, an Israelite; Shelumiel.

- son of Zurishaddai and prince of the tribe of Simeon at the time of the exodus
