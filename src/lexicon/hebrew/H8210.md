# H8210 שָׁפַךְ / שפך

## shâphak

_(shaw-fak' | shaw-PAHK | sha-FAHK)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to spill forth (blood, a libation, liquid metal; or even a solid, i.e., to mound up); also (figuratively) to expend (life, soul, complaint, money, etc.); intensively, to sprawl out; cast (up), gush out, pour (out), shed(-der) (out), slip.

- to pour, pour out, spill
    - (Qal)
        - to pour, pour out
        - to shed (blood)
        - to pour out (anger or heart) (figuratively)
    - (Niphal) to be poured out, be shed
    - (Pual) to be poured out, be shed
    - (Hithpael)
        - to be poured out
        - to pour out oneself
