# H5689 עֲגַב / עגב

## ʻăgab

_(aw-gab' | uh-ɡAHB | uh-ɡAHV)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to breathe after, i.e., to love (sensually); dote, lover.

- (Qal) to have inordinate affection or lust
    - lust (participle)
    - paramours (participle as subst)
