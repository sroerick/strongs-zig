# H7711 שְׁדֵפָה / שדפה

## shᵉdêphâh

_(shed-ay-faw' | sheh-day-PAW | sheh-day-FA)_

### Definition

noun; or שִׁדָּפוֹן; from [[H7710]]; blight; blasted(-ing).

- blighted or blasted thing, blighted, blasted (noun feminine)
- blight (of crops) (noun masculine)
