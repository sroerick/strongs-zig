# H3913 לָטַשׁ / לטש

## lâṭash

_(law-tash' | law-TAHSH | la-TAHSH)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to hammer out (an edge), i.e., to sharpen; instructer, sharp(-en), whet.

- to sharpen, hammer, whet
    - (Qal)
        - to hammer
        - to sharpen (sword)
        - hammerer (participle)
    - (Pual) to be sharpened
