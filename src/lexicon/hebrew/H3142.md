## Yûwshab Cheçed

_(yoo-shab' kheh'-sed | yoo-SHAHB heh-SED | yoo-SHAHV heh-SED)_

proper masculine noun; whose love is returned; from [[H7725]] and [[H2617]]; kindness will be returned; Jushab-Chesed, an Israelite; Jushab-hesed.
