# H1731 דּוּד / דוד

## dûwd

_(dood | dood | dood)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from the same as [[H1730]]; a pot (for boiling); also (by resemblance of shape) a basket; basket, caldron, kettle, (seething) pot.

- pot, jar, basket, kettle
    - pot, kettle
    - basket, jar
