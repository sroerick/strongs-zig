# H5461 סָגָן / סגן

## çâgân

_(saw-gawn' | saw-ɡAWN | sa-ɡAHN)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from an unused root meaning to superintend; a præfect of a province; prince, ruler.

- ruler, prefect, governor, a subordinate ruler
    - prefects (of Assyria and Babylon)
    - petty rulers or officials
