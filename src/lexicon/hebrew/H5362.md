# H5362 נָקַף / נקף

## nâqaph

_(naw-kaf' | naw-KAHP | na-KAHF)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to strike with more or less violence (beat, fell, corrode); by implication (of attack) to knock together, i.e., surround or circulate; compass (about, -ing), cut down, destroy, go round (about), inclose, round.

- to strike, strike off
    - (Piel) to strike off skin
- to go around, compass, round
    - (Qal) to go around
    - (Hiphil)
        - to go around, surround, encompass, enclose
        - to make the round, complete the circuit
        - to make round, round off
