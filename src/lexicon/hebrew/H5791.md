# H5791 עָוַת / עות

## ʻâvath

_(aw-vath' | aw-WAHT | ah-VAHT)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to wrest; bow self, (make) crooked, falsifying, overthrow, deal perversely, pervert, subvert, turn upside down.

- to be bent, be crooked, bend, make crooked, pervert
    - (Piel)
        - to make crooked, falsify, pervert, subvert
        - to bend, make crooked
    - (Pual) thing bent (participle)
    - (Hithpael) to bend oneself
