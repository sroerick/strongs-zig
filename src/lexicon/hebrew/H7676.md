# H7676 שַׁבָּת / שבת

## shabbâth

_(shab-bawth' | shahb-BAWT | sha-BAHT)_

### Definition

noun; intensive from [[H7673]]; intermission, i.e (specifically) the Sabbath; ([[lexicon-key|+]] every) sabbath.

- Sabbath
    - sabbath
    - day of atonement
    - sabbath year
    - week
    - produce (in sabbath year)
