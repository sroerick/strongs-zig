# H7644 שֶׁבְנָא / שבנא

## Shebnâʼ

_(sheb-naw' | sheb-NAW | shev-NA)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; vigour; or שֶׁבְנָה; from an unused root meaning to grow; growth; Shebna or Shebnah, an Israelite; Shebna, Shebnah.

- a person of high position in the court of king Hezekiah of Judah; subsequently the secretary of Hezekiah
