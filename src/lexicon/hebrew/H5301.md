# H5301 נָפַח / נפח

## nâphach

_(naw-fakh' | naw-PA | na-FAHK)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to puff, in various applications (literally, to inflate, blow hard, scatter, kindle, expire; figuratively, to disesteem); blow, breath, give up, cause to lose (life), seething, snuff.

- to breathe, blow, sniff at, seethe, give up or lose (life)
    - (Qal) to breathe, blow
    - (Pual) to be blown
    - (Hiphil) to cause to breathe out
