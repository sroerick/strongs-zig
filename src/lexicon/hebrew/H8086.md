# H8086 שְׁמַע / שמע

## shᵉmaʻ

_(shem-ah' | sheh-MA | sheh-MA)_

### Definition

verb; corresponding to [[H8085]]; to hear intelligently (often with implication of attention, obedience, etc.; causatively, to tell, etc.); hear, obey.

- to hear
    - (Peal) to hear, have a sense of hearing
    - (Ithpael) to show oneself obedient
