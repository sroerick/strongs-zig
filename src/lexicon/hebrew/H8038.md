# H8038 שֶׁמְאֵבֶר / שמאבר

## Shemʼêber

_(shem-ay'-ber | shem-ay-BER | shem-ay-VER)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; lofty flight; apparently from [[H8034]] and [[H83]]; name of pinion, i.e., illustrious; Shemeber, a king of Zeboim; Shemeber.

- king of Zeboim and an ally of the king of Sodom
