# H5429 סְאָה / סאה

## çᵉʼâh

_(seh-aw' | seh-AW | seh-AH)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from an unused root meaning to define; a seah, or certain measure (as determinative) for grain; measure.

- seah, a measure of flour or grain
    - probably equal to 1/3 ephah
