# H7429 רָמַס / רמס

## râmaç

_(raw-mas' | raw-MAHS | ra-MAHS)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to tread upon (as a potter, in walking or abusively); oppressor, stamp upon, trample (under feet), tread (down, upon).

- to trample
    - (Qal)
        - to trample
        - trampler (participle)
    - (Niphal) to be trampled
