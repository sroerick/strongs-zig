# H5716 עֲדִי / עדי

## ʻădîy

_(ad-ee' | uh-DEE | uh-DEE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H5710]] in the sense of trappings; finery; generally an outfit; specifically, a headstall; [[lexicon-key|×]] excellent, mouth, ornament.

- ornaments
    - ornaments
    - trappings (of horses)
