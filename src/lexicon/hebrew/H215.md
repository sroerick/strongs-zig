# H215 אוֹר / אור

## ʼôwr

_(ore | ore | ore)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to be (causative, make) luminous (literally and metaphorically); [[lexicon-key|×]] break of day, glorious, kindle, (be, give, show) (en-)light(-en, -ened), set on fire, shine.

- to be or become light, shine
    - (Qal)
        - to become light (day)
        - to shine (of the sun)
        - to become bright
    - (Niphal)
        - to be illuminated
        - to become lighted up
    - (Hiphil)
        - to give light, shine (of sun, moon, and stars)
        - to illumine, light up, cause to shine, shine
        - to kindle, light (candle, wood)
        - lighten (of the eyes, his law, etc)
        - to make shine (of the face)
