# H8574 תַּנּוּר / תנור

## tannûwr

_(tan-noor' | tahn-NOOR | ta-NOOR)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H5216]]; a fire-pot; furnace, oven.

- furnace, oven, fire-pot, (portable) stove
    - for cooking
    - of God's wrath, His furnace (figuratively)
    - of hunger, desire for evil
    - fire-pot
