# H6687 צוּף / צוף

## tsûwph

_(tsoof | tsoop | tsoof)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to overflow; (make to over-)flow, swim.

- to flow, overflow, flood, float
    - (Qal) to flood
    - (Hiphil)
        - to cause to flow over
        - to cause to float
