# H7272 רֶגֶל / רגל

## regel

_(reh'-gel | reh-ɡEL | reh-ɡEL)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from [[H7270]]; a foot (as used in walking); by implication, a step; by euphemistically the pudenda; [[lexicon-key|×]] be able to endure, [[lexicon-key|×]] according as, [[lexicon-key|×]] after, [[lexicon-key|×]] coming, [[lexicon-key|×]] follow, (broken-)foot(-ed, -stool), [[lexicon-key|×]] great toe, [[lexicon-key|×]] haunt, [[lexicon-key|×]] journey, leg, [[lexicon-key|+]] piss, [[lexicon-key|+]] possession, time.

- foot
    - foot, leg
    - of God (anthropomorphic)
    - of seraphim, cherubim, idols, animals, table
    - according to the pace of (with preposition)
    - three times (feet, paces)
