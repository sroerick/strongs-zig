# H6397 פְּלוֹנִי / פלוני

## Pᵉlôwnîy

_(pel-o-nee' | peh-loh-NEE | peh-loh-NEE)_

### Definition

adjective; a certain one; patronymically from an unused name (from [[H6395]]) meaning separate; a Pelonite or inhabitant of an unknown Palon; Pelonite.

- the inhabitants of a presently unknown place
