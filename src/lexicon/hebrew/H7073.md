# H7073 קְנַז / קנז

## Qᵉnaz

_(ken-az' | keh-NAHDZ | keh-NAHZ)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; hunter; probably from an unused root meaning to hunt; hunter; Kenaz, the name of an Edomite and of two Israelites; Kenaz.

- son of Eliphaz and grandson of Esau; one of the dukes of Edom
- a brother of Caleb and father of Othniel
