# H7799 שׁוּשַׁן / שושן

## shûwshan

_(shoo-shan' | shoo-SHAHN | shoo-SHAHN)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or שׁוֹשָׁן; or שֹׁשָׁן; and (feminine) שׁוֹשַׁנָּה; from [[H7797]]; a lily (from its whiteness), as a flower of architectural ornament; also a (straight) trumpet (from the tubular shape); lily, Shoshannim.

- lily
    - probably any lily-like flower
    - Shoshannim, in title of Ps. 45
