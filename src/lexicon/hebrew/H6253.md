# H6253 עַשְׁתֹּרֶת / עשתרת

## ʻAshtôreth

_(ash-to'reth | ash-toh-RET | ash-toh-RET)_

### Definition

proper feminine noun; star; probably for [[H6251]]; Ashtoreth, the Phoenician goddess of love (and increase); Ashtoreth.

- the principal female deity of the Phoenicians worshipped in war and fertility
    - also 'Ishtar' of Assyria and 'Astarte' by the Greeks and Romans
