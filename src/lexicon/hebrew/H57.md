# H57 אָבֵל / אבל

## ʼâbêl

_(aw-bale' | aw-BALE | ah-VALE)_

### Definition

adjective; from [[H56]]; lamenting; mourn(-er, -ing).

- mourning
    - for the dead
    - because of calamity
    - of rites of mourning
- mourner (substantive)
    - for the dead
    - for calamity
