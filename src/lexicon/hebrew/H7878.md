# H7878 שִׂיחַ / שיח

## sîyach

_(see'-akh | SEE-ah | SEE-ak)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to ponder, i.e., (by implication) converse (with oneself, and hence, aloud) or (transitively) utter; commune, complain, declare, meditate, muse, pray, speak, talk (with).

- to put forth, mediate, muse, commune, speak, complain, ponder, sing
    - (Qal)
        - to complain
        - to muse, meditate upon, study, ponder
        - to talk, sing, speak
    - (Polel) to meditate, consider, put forth thoughts
