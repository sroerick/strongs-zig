# H819 אַשְׁמָה / אשמה

## ʼashmâh

_(ash-maw' | ash-MAW | ash-MA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; feminine of [[H817]]; guiltiness, a fault, the presentation of a sin-offering; offend, sin, (cause of) trespass(-ing) (offering).

- guiltiness, guilt, offense, sin, wrong-doing
    - doing wrong, committing a trespass or offense
    - becoming guilty, guilt
    - bringing a guilt-offering
