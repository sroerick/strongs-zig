# H8496 תֹּךְ / תך

## tôk

_(toke | toke | toke)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or תּוֹך; (Psalm 72:14), from the same base as [[H8432]] (in the sense of cutting to pieces); oppression; deceit, fraud.

- injury, oppression, oppressor
- ([[lexicon-key|TWOT]]) fraud, deceit, deceitful
