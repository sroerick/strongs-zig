# H5806 עֲזוּבָה / עזובה

## ʻĂzûwbâh

_(az-oo-baw' | uh-dzoo-BAW | uh-zoo-VA)_

### Definition

proper feminine noun; forsaken; the same as [[H5805]]; Azubah, the name of two Israelitesses; Azubah.

- wife of Caleb, the son of Hezron
- daughter of Shilhi, wife of king Asa, and mother of king Jehoshaphat of Judah
