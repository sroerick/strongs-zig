# H5574 סְנוּאָה / סנואה

## Çᵉnûwʼâh

_(sen-oo-aw' | seh-noo-AW | seh-noo-AH)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; bristling; or סְנֻאָה from the same as [[H5570]]; pointed; (used with the article as a proper name) Senuah, the name of two Israelites; Hasenuah (including the art), Senuah.

- a Benjamite
