# H5793 עוּתַי / עותי

## ʻÛwthay

_(oo-thah'-ee | oo-TAI | oo-TAI)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; helpful; from [[H5790]]; succoring; Uthai, the name of two Israelites; Uthai.

- son of Ammihud and a descendant of Pharez of Judah
- one of the sons of Bigvai who returned from exile with the 2nd caravan with Ezra
