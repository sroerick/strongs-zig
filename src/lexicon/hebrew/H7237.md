# H7237 רַבָּה / רבה

## Rabbâh

_(rab-baw' | rahb-BAW | ra-BA)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; great; feminine of [[H7227]]; great; Rabbah, the name of two places in Palestine, East and West; Rabbah, Rabbath.

- the capital city of the Ammonites located east of the Jordan
- a town in Judah; site uncertain
