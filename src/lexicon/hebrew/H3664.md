# H3664 כָּנַס / כנס

## kânaç

_(kaw-nas' | kaw-NAHS | ka-NAHS)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to collect; hence, to enfold; gather (together), heap up, wrap self.

- to gather, collect, wrap
    - (Qal) to gather
    - (Piel) to gather
    - (Hithpael) to gather together, wrap oneself up
