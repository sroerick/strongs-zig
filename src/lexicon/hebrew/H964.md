# H964 בִּזְיוֹתְיָה / בזיותיה

## bizyôwthᵉyâh

_(biz-yo-the-yaw' | bidz-yoh-teh-YAW | beez-yoh-teh-YA)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; contempt of Jehovah; from [[H959]] and [[H3050]]; contempts of Jah; Bizjothjah, a place in Palestine; Bizjothjah.

- a town in southern Judah near Beersheba
