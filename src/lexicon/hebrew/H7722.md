# H7722 שׁוֹא / שוא

## shôwʼ

_(sho | shoh | shoh)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or (feminine) שׁוֹאָה; or שֹׁאָה; from an unused root meaning to rush over; a tempest; by implication, devastation; desolate(-ion), destroy, destruction, storm, wasteness.

- ravage (noun masculine)
- devastation, ruin, waste (noun feminine)
    - devastation, ruin
    - ruin, waste (of land)
