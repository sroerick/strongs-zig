# H7081 קֶסֶם / קסם

## qeçem

_(keh'-sem | keh-SEM | keh-SEM)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H7080]]; a lot; also divination (including its fee), oracle; (reward of) divination, divine sentence, witchcraft.

- divination, witchcraft
    - of the nations, Balaam
    - of false prophets
    - in a good sense (king's lips as oracles)
