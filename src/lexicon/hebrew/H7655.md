# H7655 שִׁבְעָה / שבעה

## shibʻâh

_(shib-aw' | shib-AW | sheev-AH)_

### Definition

feminine noun; corresponding to [[H7651]]; seven (as the sacred full one); also (adverbially) seven times; by implication, a week; by extension, an indefinite number; seven (times).

- seven
