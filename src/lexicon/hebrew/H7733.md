# H7733 שׁוֹבֵק / שובק

## Shôwbêq

_(sho-bake' | shoh-BAKE | shoh-VAKE)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; free; active participle from a primitive root meaning to leave (compare [[H7662]]); forsaking; Shobek, an Israelite; Shobek.

- one of the heads of the people who sealed the covenant with Nehemiah
