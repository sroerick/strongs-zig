# H6260 עַתּוּד / עתוד

## ʻattûwd

_(at-tood' | at-TOOD | ah-TOOD)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or עַתֻּד; from [[H6257]]; prepared, i.e., full grown; spoken only (in plural) of he-goats, or (figuratively) leaders of the people; chief one, (he) goat, ram.

- ram, he-goat, chief one
