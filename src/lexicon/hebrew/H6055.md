# H6055 עֲנַנְיָה / ענניה

## ʻĂnanyâh

_(an-an-yaw' | uh-nahn-YAW | uh-nahn-YA)_

### Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; Jehovah clouds; from [[H6049]] and [[H3050]]; Jah has covered; Ananjah, the name of an Israelite and of a place in Palestine; Ananiah.

- ancestor of Azariah who assisted in rebuilding the city wall in the time of Nehemiah (noun proper masculine)
- a city northwest of Jerusalem to which some Benjamite exiles returned (noun proper locative)
