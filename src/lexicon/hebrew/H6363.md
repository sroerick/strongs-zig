# H6363 פֶּטֶר / פטר

## peṭer

_(peh'-ter | peh-TER | peh-TER)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or פִּטְרָה; from [[H6362]]; a fissure, i.e., (concretely) firstling (as opening the matrix); firstling, openeth, such as open.

- firstborn, firstling, that which separates or first opens
