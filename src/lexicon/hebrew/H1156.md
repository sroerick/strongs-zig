# H1156 בְּעָא / בעא

## bᵉʻâʼ

_(beh-aw' | beh-AW | beh-AH)_

### Definition

verb; or בְּעָה; (Aramaic), corresponding to [[H1158]]; to seek or ask; ask, desire, make (petition), pray, request, seek.

- to ask, seek, request, desire, pray, make petition
    - (Peal)
        - to ask, request
        - to seek (for favour)
