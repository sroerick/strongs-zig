# H6561 פָּרַק / פרק

## pâraq

_(paw-rak' | paw-RAHK | pa-RAHK)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to break off or crunch; figuratively, to deliver; break (off), deliver, redeem, rend (in pieces), tear in pieces.

- to tear apart or away, tear off, break away
    - (Qal) to tear away, snatch, rescue
    - (Piel) to tear off
    - (Hithpael) to tear off from oneself, be broken off, be broken in pieces
