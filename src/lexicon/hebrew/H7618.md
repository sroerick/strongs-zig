# H7618 שְׁבוּ / שבו

## shᵉbûw

_(sheb-oo' | sheh-BOO | sheh-VOO)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from an unused root (probably identical with that of [[H7617]] through the idea of subdivision into flashes or streamers (compare [[H7632]]) meaning to flame; a gem (from its sparkle), probably the agate; agate.

- a precious stone (in the high priest's breastplate)
    - probably an agate
