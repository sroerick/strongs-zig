# H6675 צוֹאָה / צואה

## tsôwʼâh

_(tso-aw' | tsoh-AW | tsoh-AH)_

### Definition

feminine noun; or צֹאָה ; feminine of [[H6674]]; excrement; generally, dirt; figuratively, pollution; dung, filth(-iness).

- filth, excrement
