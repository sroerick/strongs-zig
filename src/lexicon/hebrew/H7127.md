# H7127 קְרֵב / קרב

## qᵉrêb

_(ker-abe' | keh-RABE | keh-RAVE)_

### Definition

verb; corresponding to [[H7126]]; to approach (causatively, bring near) for whatever purpose; approach, come (near, nigh), draw near.

- to approach, come near
    - (Peal) to approach
    - (Pael) to offer, draw near
    - (Aphel) to be summoned
