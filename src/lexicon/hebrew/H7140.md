# H7140 קֶרַח / קרח

## qerach

_(keh'-rakh | keh-RA | keh-RAHK)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or קֹרַח; from [[H7139]]; ice (as if bald, i.e., smooth); hence, hail; by resemblance, rock crystal; crystal, frost, ice.

- frost, ice, ice crystal
    - frost (of night)
    - ice
