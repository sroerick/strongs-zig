# H3715 כְּפִיר / כפיר

## kᵉphîyr

_(kef-eer' | keh-PEER | keh-FEER)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H3722]]; a village (as covered in by walls); also a young lion (perhaps as covered with a mane); (young) lion, village.

- young lion
- village

Compare [[H3723]].
