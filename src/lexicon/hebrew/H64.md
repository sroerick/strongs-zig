# H64 אָבֵל כְּרָמִים / אבל כרמים

## ʼÂbêl Kᵉrâmîym

_(aw-bale' ker-aw-meem' | aw-BALE keh-raw-MEEM | ah-VALE keh-ra-MEEM)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; meadow of the vineyards; from [[H58]] and the plural of [[H3754]]; meadow of vineyards; Abel-Keramim, a place in Palestine; plain of the vineyards.

- a place in Ammon
