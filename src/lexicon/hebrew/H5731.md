# H5731 עֵדֶן / עדן

## ʻÊden

_(ay'-den | ay-DEN | ay-DEN)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; pleasure; the same as [[H5730]] (masculine); Eden, the region of Adam's home; Eden.

- the first habitat of man after the creation; site unknown (noun proper masculine locative)
- a Gershonite Levite, son of Joah in the days of king Hezekiah of Judah (noun proper masculine)
