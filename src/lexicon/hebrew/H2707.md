# H2707 חָקָה / חקה

## châqâh

_(khaw-kaw' | haw-KAW | ha-KA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to carve; by implication, to delineate; also to entrench; carved work, portrayed, set a print.

- to cut, carve, cut in
    - (Pual) carved (participle)
    - (Hithpael) to engrave, cut a limit
