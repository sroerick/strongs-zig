# H895 בַּבֶל / בבל

## Babel

_(baw-bel' | ba-BEL | ba-VEL)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; confusion (by mixing); corresponding to [[H894]]; Babel (i.e., Babylon), including Babylonia and the Babylonian empire; Babylon.

- Babel or Babylon, the ancient site and/or capital of Babylonia (modern Hillah) situated on the Euphrates
