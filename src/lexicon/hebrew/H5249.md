# H5249 נִמְרִים / נמרים

## Nimrîym

_(nim-reem' | nim-REEM | neem-REEM)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; limpid, pure; plural of a masculine corresponding to [[H5247]]; clear waters; Nimrim, a place East of the Jordan; Nimrim.

- a stream or brook in the territory of Moab

Compare [[H1039]].
