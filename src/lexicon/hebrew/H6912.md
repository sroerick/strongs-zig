# H6912 קָבַר / קבר

## qâbar

_(kaw-bar' | kaw-BAHR | ka-VAHR)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to inter; [[lexicon-key|×]] in any wise, bury(-ier).

- to bury
    - (Qal) to bury
    - (Niphal) to be buried
    - (Piel) to bury, bury (in masses)
    - (Pual) to be buried
