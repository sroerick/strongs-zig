# H8567 תָּנָה / תנה

## tânâh

_(taw-naw' | taw-NAW | ta-NA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root (identical with through the idea of attributing honor); to ascribe (praise), i.e., celebrate, commemorate; lament, rehearse.

- (Piel) to recount, rehearse, tell again
