# H6042 עֻנִּי / עני

## ʻUnnîy

_(oon-nee' | oon-NEE | oo-NEE)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; afflicted; from [[H6031]]; afflicted; Unni, the name of two Israelites; Unni.

- a Levite doorkeeper in the time of David
- a Levite concerned in the sacred office after the return from exile in Babylon
