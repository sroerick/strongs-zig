# H5884 עֵין רִמּוֹן / עין רמון

## ʻÊyn Rimmôwn

_(ane rim-mone' | ane rim-MONE | ane ree-MONE)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; fount of the pomegranate; from [[H5869]] and [[H7416]]; fountain of a pomegranate; En-Rimmon, a place in Palestine; En-rimmon.

- one of the places that the returning exiles reinhabited in Judah
