# H7901 שָׁכַב / שכב

## shâkab

_(shaw-kab' | shaw-KAHB | sha-HAHV)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to lie down (for rest, sexual connection, decease or any other purpose); [[lexicon-key|×]] at all, cast down, (lover-)lay (self) (down), (make to) lie (down, down to sleep, still with), lodge, ravish, take rest, sleep, stay.

- to lie down
    - (Qal)
        - to lie, lie down, lie on
        - to lodge
        - to lie (of sexual relations)
        - to lie down (in death)
        - to rest, relax (figuratively)
    - (Niphal) to be lain with (sexually)
    - (Pual) to be lain with (sexually)
    - (Hiphil) to make to lie down
    - (Hophal) to be laid
