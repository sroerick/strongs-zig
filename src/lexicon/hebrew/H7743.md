# H7743 שׁוּחַ / שוח

## shûwach

_(shoo'-akh | SHOO-ah | SHOO-ak)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to sink, literally or figuratively; bow down, incline, humble.

- to sink down, be bowed down, be humble
    - (Qal) to sink down
    - (Hiphil) of depression of the mind
