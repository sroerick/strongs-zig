# H1847 דַּעַת / דעת

## daʻath

_(dah'-ath | da-AT | da-AT)_

### Definition

noun; from [[H3045]]; knowledge; cunning, (ig-)norantly, know(-ledge), (un-)awares (wittingly).

- knowledge
    - knowledge, perception, skill
    - discernment, understanding, wisdom
