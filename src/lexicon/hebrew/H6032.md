# H6032 עֲנָה / ענה

## ʻănâh

_(an-aw' | uh-NAW | uh-NA)_

### Definition

verb; corresponding to [[H6030]]; properly, to eye or (generally) to heed, i.e., pay attention; by implication, to respond; by extension to begin to speak; specifically to sing, shout, testify, announce; answer, speak.

- to answer, respond
    - (Peal)
        - to answer, make reply
        - to respond
