# H7386 רֵיק / ריק

## rêyq

_(rake | rake | rake)_

### Definition

adjective; or (shorter) רֵק; from [[H7324]]; empty; figuratively, worthless; emptied(-ty), vain (fellow, man).

- empty, vain
    - empty (of vessels)
    - empty, idle, worthless (ethically)
