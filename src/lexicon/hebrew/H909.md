# H909 בָּדַד / בדד

## bâdad

_(baw-dad' | baw-DAHD | ba-DAHD)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to divide, i.e., (reflexive) be solitary; alone.

- to withdraw, be separate, be isolated
    - (Qal) an army straggler (participle)
        - of Ephraim (metaphorically)
- ([[lexicon-key|TWOT]]) alone
