# H8052 שְׁמוּעָה / שמועה

## shᵉmûwʻâh

_(sehm-oo-aw' | sheh-moo-AW | sheh-moo-AH)_

### Definition

feminine noun; feminine passive participle of [[H8074]]; something heard, i.e., an announcement; bruit, doctrine, fame, mentioned, news, report, rumor, tidings.

- report, news, rumour
    - report, news, tidings
    - mention
