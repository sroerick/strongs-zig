# H1442 גָּדַף / גדף

## gâdaph

_(gaw-daf' | ɡaw-DAHP | ɡa-DAHF)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to hack (with words), i.e., revile; blaspheme, reproach.

- to revile men, blaspheme God
    - (Piel)
        - to revile (between men)
        - to blaspheme (God)
