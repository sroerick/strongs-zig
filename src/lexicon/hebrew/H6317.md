# H6317 פּוּטִיאֵל / פוטיאל

## Pûwṭîyʼêl

_(poo-tee-ale' | poo-tee-ALE | poo-tee-ALE)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; afflicted of God; from an unused root (probably meaning to disparage) and [[H410]]; contempt of God; Putiel, an Israelite; Putiel.

- father of the wife of Eleazar, the son of Aaron
