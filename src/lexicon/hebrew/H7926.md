# H7926 שְׁכֶם / שכם

## shᵉkem

_(shek-em' | sheh-KEM | sheh-HEM)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H7925]]; the neck (between the shoulders) as the place of burdens; figuratively, the spur of a hill; back, [[lexicon-key|×]] consent, portion, shoulder.

- shoulder, back
    - shoulder, shoulder-blade
    - back (in general)
