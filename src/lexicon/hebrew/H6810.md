# H6810 צָעִיר / צעיר

## tsâʻîyr

_(tsaw-eer' | tsaw-EER | tsa-EER)_

### Definition

adjective; or צָעוֹר; from [[H6819]]; little; (in number) few; (in age) young, (in value) ignoble; least, little (one), small (one), [[lexicon-key|+]] young(-er, -est).

- little, insignificant, young
    - little, insignificant
    - insignificant, mean
    - young, younger, youngest
