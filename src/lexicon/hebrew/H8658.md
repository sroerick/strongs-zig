# H8658 תַּרְשִׁישׁ / תרשיש

## tarshîysh

_(tar-sheesh' | tahr-SHEESH | tahr-SHEESH)_

### Definition

masculine noun; probably of foreign derivation (compare [[H8659]]); a gem, perhaps the topaz; beryl.

- a precious stone or semi-precious gem
    - perhaps a chrysolite, yellow jasper, or other yellow coloured stone
