# H6499 פַּר / פר

## par

_(par | pahr | pahr)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or פָּר; from [[H6565]]; a bullock (apparently as breaking forth in wild strength, or perhaps as dividing the hoof); ([[lexicon-key|+]] young) bull(-ock), calf, ox.

- young bull, steer, bullock
