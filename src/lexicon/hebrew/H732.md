# H732 אָרַח / ארח

## ʼârach

_(aw-rakh' | aw-RA | ah-RAHK)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to travel; go, wayfaring (man).

- to wander, journey, go, keep company with
    - (Qal)
        - to journey, go
        - (participle)
            - wandering, wayfarer, journeying
        - (substantive)
            - wanderer, wayfarer, traveller
