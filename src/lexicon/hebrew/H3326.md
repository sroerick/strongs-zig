# H3326 יָצוּעַ / יצוע

## yâtsûwaʻ

_(yaw-tsoo'-ah | yaw-TSOO-ah | ya-TSOO-ah)_

### Definition

masculine noun; passive participle of [[H3331]]; spread, i.e., a bed; (architecture) an extension, i.e., wing or lean-to (a single story or collectively); bed, chamber, couch.

- couch, bed
