# H5374 נֵרִיָּה / נריה

## Nêrîyâh

_(nay-ree-yaw' | nay-rih-YAW | nay-ree-YA)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; lamp of Jehovah; or נֵרִיָּהוּ; from [[H5216]] and [[H3050]]; light of Jah; Nerijah, an Israelite; Neriah.

- son of Maaseiah and father of Baruch and Seraiah
