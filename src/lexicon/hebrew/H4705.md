# H4705 מִצְעָר / מצער

## mitsʻâr

_(mits-awr' | mits-AWR | meets-AR)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H6819]]; petty (in size or number); adverbially, a short (time); little one (while), small.

- a small thing
    - small thing
    - little while (of time)
