# H8245 שָׁקַד / שקד

## shâqad

_(shaw-kad' | shaw-KAHD | sha-KAHD)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to be alert, i.e., sleepless; hence to be on the lookout (whether for good or ill); hasten, remain, wake, watch (for).

- to wake, watch, awake, be alert
    - (Qal)
        - to keep watch of, be wakeful over
        - to be wakeful, wake (as mourner or sufferer)
