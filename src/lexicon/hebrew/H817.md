# H817 אָשָׁם / אשם

## ʼâshâm

_(aw-shawm' | aw-SHAWM | ah-SHAHM)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H816]]; guilt; by implication, a fault; also a sin-offering; guiltiness, (offering for) sin, trespass (offering).

- guilt, offense, guiltiness
    - offense, trespass, fault
    - guilt, guiltiness
    - compensation (for offense)
    - trespass offering, guilt offering
