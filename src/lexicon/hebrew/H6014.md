# H6014 עָמַר / עמר

## ʻâmar

_(aw-mar' | aw-MAHR | ah-MAHR)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; specifically (as denominative from [[H6016]]); properly, apparently to heap; figuratively, to chastise (as if piling blows); to gather grain; bind sheaves, make merchandise of.

- to bind sheaves
    - (Piel) to gather
- to manipulate, deal tyrannically with
    - (Hithpael) to treat as a slave
