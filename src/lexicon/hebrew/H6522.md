# H6522 פְּרִזִּי / פרזי

## Pᵉrizzîy

_(per-iz-zee' | peh-ridz-ZEE | peh-ree-ZEE)_

### Definition

adjective; belonging to a village; for [[H6521]]; inhabitant of the open country; a Perizzite, one of the Canaanitish tribes; Perizzite.

- a people who inhabited southern Canaan prior to the conquest
