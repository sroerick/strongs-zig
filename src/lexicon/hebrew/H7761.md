# H7761 שׂוּם / שום

## sûwm

_(soom | soom | soom)_

### Definition

verb; corresponding to [[H7760]]; to put (used in a great variety of applications, literal, figurative, inferentially, and elliptically); [[lexicon-key|+]] command, give, lay, make, [[lexicon-key|+]] name, [[lexicon-key|+]] regard, set.

- to set, make, appoint
    - (Peal)
        - to make, make decree, set out (decree)
        - to make, appoint
        - to set, fix
    - (Ithpeal) to be made, be set, be laid
