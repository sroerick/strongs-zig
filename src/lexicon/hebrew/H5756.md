# H5756 עוּז / עוז

## ʻûwz

_(ooz | oodz | ooz)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to be strong; causatively, to strengthen, i.e., (figuratively) to save (by flight); gather (self, self to flee), retire.

- (Hiphil) to take refuge, bring to refuge, seek refuge
