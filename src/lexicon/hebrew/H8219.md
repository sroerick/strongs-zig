# H8219 שְׁפֵלָה / שפלה

## shᵉphêlâh

_(shef-ay-law' | sheh-pay-LAW | sheh-fay-LA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from [[H8213]]; Lowland, i.e., (with the article) the maritime slope of Palestine; low country, (low) plain, vale(-ley).

- lowland, valley
    - lowland
        - strip west of Judean mountains (technical term)
            - the Shephelah
        - strip near coast north of Carmel
