# H63 אָבֵל הַשִּׁטִּים / אבל השטים

## ʼÂbêl hash-Shiṭṭîym

_(aw-bale' hash-shit-teem' | aw-BALE hahsh-shit-TEEM | ah-VALE ha-shee-TEEM)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; meadow of acacias; from [[H58]] and the plural of [[H7848]], with the article inserted; meadow of the acacias; Abel hash-Shittim, a place in Palestine; Abelshittim.

- place in lowlands of Moab
