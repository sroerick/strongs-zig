# H2461 חָלָב / חלב

## châlâb

_(khaw-lawb' | haw-LAWB | ha-LAHV)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from the same as [[H2459]]; milk (as the richness of kine); [[lexicon-key|+]] cheese, milk, sucking.

- milk, sour milk, cheese
    - milk
    - abundance of the land (metaphorically)
    - white (as milk)
