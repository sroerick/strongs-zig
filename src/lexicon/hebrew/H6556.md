# H6556 פֶּרֶץ / פרץ

## perets

_(peh'-rets | peh-RETS | peh-RETS)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H6555]]; a break (literally or figuratively); breach, breaking forth (in), [[lexicon-key|×]] forth, gap.

- breach, gap, bursting forth
    - bursting forth, outburst
    - breach
    - broken wall
    - outburst (figuratively of God's wrath)
