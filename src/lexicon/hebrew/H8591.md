# H8591 תָּעַע / תעע

## tâʻaʻ

_(taw-ah' | taw-AH | ta-AH)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to cheat; by analogy, to maltreat; deceive, misuse.

- to deceive, misuse
    - (Pilpel)
        - to be a mocker, mock
        - mocker (substantive)
    - (Hithpalpel)
        - to ridicule
        - mocking (participle)
