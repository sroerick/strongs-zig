# H7596 שְׁאֵלָה / שאלה

## shᵉʼêlâh

_(sheh-ay-law' | sheh-ay-LAW | sheh-ay-LA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; or שֵׁלָה; (1 Samuel 1:17), from [[H7592]]; a petition; by implication, a loan; loan, petition, request.

- request, thing asked for, demand
    - request, petition
    - thing asked for
