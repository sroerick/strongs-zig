# H1406 גָּג / גג

## gâg

_(gawg | ɡawɡ | ɡahɡ)_

### Definition

masculine noun; probably by reduplication from [[H1342]]; a roof; by analogy, the top of an altar; roof (of the house), (house) top (of the house).

- roof, top, housetop
    - roof (of house)
    - top (of altar of incense)
