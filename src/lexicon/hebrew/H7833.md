# H7833 שָׁחַק / שחק

## shâchaq

_(shaw-khak' | shaw-HAHK | sha-HAHK)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to comminate (by trituration or attrition); beat, wear.

- to rub away, beat fine, pulverise
    - (Qal)
        - to rub away
            - of incense, stones
            - of enemies (figuratively)
