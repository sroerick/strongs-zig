# H66 אַבֵל מַיִם / אבל מים

## ʼAbêl Mayim

_(aw-bale' mah'-yim | ah-BALE ma-YIM | ah-VALE ma-YEEM)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; meadow of waters; from [[H58]] and [[H4325]]; meadow of water; Abel-Majim, a place in Palestine; Abel-maim.

- a city in northern Israel (perhaps Abel Beth Maachah)
