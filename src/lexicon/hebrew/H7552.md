# H7552 רֶקֶם / רקם

## Reqem

_(reh'-kem | reh-KEM | reh-KEM)_

### Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; variegation; from [[H7551]]; versi-color; Rekem, the name of a place in Palestine, also of a Midianite and an Israelite; Rekem.

- one of the 5 kings of Midian killed by the Israelites (noun proper masculine)
- one of the 4 sons of Hebron and the father of Shammai (noun proper masculine)
- a Manassite, son of Machir by Maachah his wife (noun proper masculine)
- a town in Benjamin; site unknown (noun proper locative)
