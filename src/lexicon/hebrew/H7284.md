# H7284 רְגַשׁ / רגש

## rᵉgash

_(reg-ash' | reh-ɡAHSH | reh-ɡAHSH)_

### Definition

verb; corresponding to [[H7283]]; to gather tumultuously; assemble (together).

- to be in tumult
    - (Aphel) to gather in a tumultuous throng, show tumultuousness, come thronging
