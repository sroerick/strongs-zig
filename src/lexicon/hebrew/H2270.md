## châbêr

_(khaw-bare' | haw-BARE | ha-VARE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H2266]]; an associate; companion, fellow, knit together.

- united (adjective)
- associate, fellow, worshippers (noun masculine)
- companion (noun masculine)
