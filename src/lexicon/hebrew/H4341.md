# H4341 מַכְאֹב / מכאב

## makʼôb

_(mak-obe' | mahk-OBE | mahk-OVE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; sometimes מַכְאוֹב; also (feminine Isaiah 53:3) מַכְאֹבָה; from [[H3510]]; anguish or (figuratively) affliction; grief, pain, sorrow.

- pain, sorrow
    - pain (physical)
    - pain (mental)
