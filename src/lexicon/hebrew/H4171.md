# H4171 מוּר / מור

## mûwr

_(moor | moor | moor)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to alter; by implication, to barter, to dispose of; [[lexicon-key|×]] at all, (ex-)change, remove.

- to change, exchange
    - (Niphal) to be changed
    - (Hiphil)
        - to change, alter
        - to exchange
