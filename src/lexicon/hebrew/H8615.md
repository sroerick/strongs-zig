# H8615 תִּקְוָה / תקוה

## tiqvâh

_(tik-vaw' | tik-WAW | teek-VA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from [[H6960]]; (compare [[H6961]]); literally a cord (as an attachment); figuratively, expectancy; expectation(-ted), hope, live, thing that I long for.

- cord
- hope, expectation
    - hope
    - ground of hope
    - things hoped for, outcome
