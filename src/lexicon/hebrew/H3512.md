# H3512 כָּאָה / כאה

## kâʼâh

_(kaw-aw' | kaw-AW | ka-AH)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to despond; causatively, to deject; broken, be grieved, make sad.

- to be sad, be disheartened, be cowed
    - (Niphal) to be disheartened, be cowed
    - (Hiphil) to make sad
