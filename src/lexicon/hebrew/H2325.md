# H2325 חוּב / חוב

## chûwb

_(khoob | hoob | hoov)_

### Definition

verb; also חָיַב; a primitive root; properly, perhaps to tie, i.e., (figuratively and reflexively) to owe, or (by implication) to forfeit; make endanger.

- to be guilty, make guilty
    - (Piel) to endanger
