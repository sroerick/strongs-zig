# H5226 נֵכַח / נכח

## nêkach

_(nay'-kakh | nay-KA | nay-HAHK)_

### Definition

adverb; from an unused root meaning to be straightforward; properly, the fore part; used adverbially, opposite; before, over against.

- be in front of
- in front of, opposite to, in the sight of, before, to the front, right on
- towards the front of, in front of, on behalf of, as far as in front of
