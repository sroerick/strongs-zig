# H5872 עֵין גֶּדִי / עין גדי

## ʻÊyn Gedîy

_(ane geh'-dee | ane ɡeh-DEE | ane ɡeh-DEE)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; fount of the kid; from [[H5869]] and [[H1423]]; fountain of a kid; En-Gedi, a place in Palestine; En-gedi.

- a town in the wilderness of Judah on the western shore of the Dead Sea
