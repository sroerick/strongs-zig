# H3800 כֶּתֶם / כתם

## kethem

_(keh'-them | keh-TEM | keh-TEM)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H3799]]; properly, something carved out, i.e., ore; hence, gold (pure as originally mined); ((most) fine, pure) gold(-en wedge).

- gold, pure gold
