# H6034 עֲנָה / ענה

## ʻĂnâh

_(an-aw' | uh-NAW | uh-NA)_

### Definition

proper feminine noun, proper masculine noun; answer; probably from [[H6030]]; an answer; Anah, the name of two Edomites and one Edomitess; Anah.

- son of Zibeon and father of Aholibamah, a wife of Esau (noun proper masculine)
- a Horite chief (noun proper masculine)
- daughter of Zibeon (noun proper feminine)
