# H8524 תָּלַל / תלל

## tâlal

_(taw-lal' | taw-LAHL | ta-LAHL)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to pile up, i.e., elevate; eminent.

- ([[lexicon-key|CLBL]]) to exalt (verb)
    - (Qal) eminent, exalted (participle)
- ([[lexicon-key|BDB]]) exalted, lofty (adjective)

Compare [[H2048]].
