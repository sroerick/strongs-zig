# H5518 סִיר / סיר

## çîyr

_(seer | seer | seer)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or (feminine) סִירָה; or סִרָה; (Jeremiah 52:18), from a primitive root meaning to boil up; a thorn (as springing up rapidly); by implication, a hook; caldron, fishhook, pan, (wash-)pot, thorn.

- pot
    - pot (household utensil)
    - pot (of temple)
- thorn, hook, brier
    - thorns
    - hooks
