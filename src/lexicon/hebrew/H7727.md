# H7727 שׁוֹבָב / שובב

## Shôwbâb

_(sho-bawb' | shoh-BAWB | shoh-VAHV)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; rebellious; the same as [[H7726]]; rebellious; Shobab, the name of two Israelites; Shobab.

- son of David by Bathsheba
- a Judaite, son of Caleb by his wife Azubah and grandson of Hezron
