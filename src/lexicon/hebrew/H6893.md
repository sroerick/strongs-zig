# H6893 קָאַת / קאת

## qâʼath

_(kaw-ath' | kaw-AT | ka-AT)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from [[H6958]]; probably the pelican (from vomiting); cormorant.

- a ceremonially unclean bird
    - perhaps pelican or cormorant
    - perhaps an extinct bird, exact meaning unknown
