# H7771 שׁוֹעַ / שוע

## shôwaʻ

_(sho'-ah | SHOH-ah | SHOH-ah)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H7768]] in the original sense of freedom; a noble, i.e., liberal, opulent; also (as noun in the derived sense) a halloo; bountiful, crying, rich.

- independent, noble, free, rich, generous (adjective)
- cry (noun masculine)
    - perhaps war-cry, cry for help
