# H5858 עֵיבָל / עיבל

## ʻÊybâl

_(ay-bawl' | ay-BAWL | ay-VAHL)_

### Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; stone, bare mountain; perhaps from an unused root probably meaning to be bald; bare; Ebal, a mountain of Palestine; Ebal.

- a son of Shobal (noun proper masculine)
- mountain of cursing, north of Shechem and opposite Mount Gerizim (noun proper locative)
