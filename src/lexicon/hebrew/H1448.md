# H1448 גְּדֵרָה / גדרה

## gᵉdêrâh

_(ghed-ay-raw' | ɡeh-day-RAW | ɡeh-day-RA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; feminine of [[H1447]]; enclosure (especially for flocks); (sheep-)cote (fold) hedge, wall.

- wall, hedge
- sheepfold (construct with 'sheep')
