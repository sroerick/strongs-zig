# H1277 בָּרִיא / בריא

## bârîyʼ

_(baw-ree' | baw-REE | ba-REE)_

### Definition

adjective; from [[H1254]] (in the sense of [[H1262]]); fatted or plump; fat(-ter) (fleshed), fed, firm, plenteous, rank.

- fat
- ([[lexicon-key|TWOT]]) fat, fatter, fed, firm, plenteous, rank
