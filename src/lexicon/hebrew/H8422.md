# H8422 תּוּבַל / תובל

## Tûwbal

_(too-bal' | too-BAHL | too-VAHL)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; thou shall be brought; or תֻּבַל; probably of foreign derivation; Tubal, a postdiluvian patriarch and his posterity; Tubal.

- son of Japheth and grandson of Noah (noun proper masculine)
- a region in east Asia Minor (noun proper territory)
    - perhaps nearly equal to Cappadocia
