# H8602 תָּפֵל / תפל

## tâphêl

_(taw-fale' | taw-PALE | ta-FALE)_

### Definition

adjective; from an unused root meaning to smear; plaster (as gummy) or slime; (figuratively) frivolity; foolish things, unsavoury, untempered.

- foolish, insipid (adjective)
- ([[lexicon-key|BDB]]) tasteless, unseasoned (adjective)
- whitewash (noun masculine)
    - meaning uncertain
