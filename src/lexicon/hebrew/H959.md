# H959 בָּזָה / בזה

## bâzâh

_(baw-zaw' | baw-DZAW | ba-ZA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to disesteem; despise, disdain, contemn(-ptible), [[lexicon-key|+]] think to scorn, vile person.

- to despise, hold in contempt, disdain
    - (Qal) to despise, regard with contempt
    - (Niphal)
        - to be despised
        - to be despicable
        - to be vile, worthless
    - (Hiphil) to cause to despise
