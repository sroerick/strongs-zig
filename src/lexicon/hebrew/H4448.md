# H4448 מָלַל / מלל

## mâlal

_(maw-lal' | maw-LAHL | ma-LAHL)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to speak (mostly poetical) or say; say, speak, utter.

- to speak, utter, say
    - (Qal) to speak
    - (Piel) to say, utter
