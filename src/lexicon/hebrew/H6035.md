# H6035 עָנָו / ענו

## ʻânâv

_(aw-nawv' | aw-NAW | ah-NAHV)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or (by intermixture with [[H6041]]) עָנָיו; from [[H6031]]; depressed (figuratively), in mind (gentle) or circumstances (needy, especially saintly); humble, lowly, meek, poor.

- poor, humble, afflicted, meek
    - poor, needy
    - poor and weak
    - poor, weak and afflicted
    - humble, lowly, meek


### Note

Compare [[H6041]].
