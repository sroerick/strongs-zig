# H8616 תִּקְוָה / תקוה

## Tiqvâh

_(tik-vaw' | tik-WAW | teek-VA)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; hope; the same as [[H8615]]; Tikvah, the name of two Israelites; Tikvah.

- father of Shallum, the husband of the prophetess Huldah in the time of king Josiah of Judah
- father of Tikvah in the time of Ezra
