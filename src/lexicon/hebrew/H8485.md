# H8485 תֵּימָא / תימא

## Têymâʼ

_(tay-maw' | tay-MAW | tay-MA)_

### Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; desert; or תֵּמָא; probably of foreign derivation; Tema, a son of Ishmael, and the region settled by him; Tema.

- the 9th son of Ishmael (noun proper masculine)
- the land settled by Tema the son of Ishmael (noun proper locative)
