# H60 אֵבֶל / אבל

## ʼêbel

_(ay'-bel | ay-BEL | ay-VEL)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H56]]; lamentation; mourning.

- mourning
    - for the dead
    - for rites of mourning (metaphorically)
    - mourning garb
    - period of mourning
