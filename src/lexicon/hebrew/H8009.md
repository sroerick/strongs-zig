# H8009 שַׂלְמָה / שלמה

## Salmâh

_(sal-maw' | sahl-MAW | sahl-MA)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; garment; the same as [[H8008]]; clothing; Salmah, an Israelite; Salmon.

- father of Boaz, father-in-law of Ruth, and grandfather of David
    - also 'Salmah', 'Salma'


### Note

Compare [[H8012]].
