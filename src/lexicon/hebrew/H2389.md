# H2389 חָזָק / חזק

## châzâq

_(khaw-zawk' | haw-DZAWK | ha-ZAHK)_

### Definition

adjective; from [[H2388]]; strong (usu. in a bad sense, hard, bold, violent); harder, hottest, impudent, loud, mighty, sore, stiff(-hearted), strong(-er).

- strong, stout, mighty
    - strong
        - severe, sharp, hot
        - firm, hard
    - a strong one (substantive)
