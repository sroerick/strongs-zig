# H1881 דָּת / דת

## dâth

_(dawth | dawt | daht)_

### Definition

feminine noun; of uncertain (perhaps foreign) derivation; a royal edict or statute; commandment, commission, decree, law, manner.

- decree, law, edict, regulation, usage
    - decree, edict, commission
    - law, rule
