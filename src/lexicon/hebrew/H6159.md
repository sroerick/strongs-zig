# H6159 עֹרֵב / ערב

## ʻÔrêb

_(o-rabe' | oh-RABE | oh-RAVE)_

### Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; raven; or עוֹרֵב; the same as [[H6158]]; Oreb, the name of a Midianite and of the cliff near the Jordan; Oreb.

- one of the chieftains of the Midianite army defeated by Gideon (noun proper masculine)
- a rock east of the Jordan where Oreb and part of his army fell to the Ephraimites (noun proper locative)
