# H5748 עוּגָב / עוגב

## ʻûwgâb

_(oo-gawb' | oo-ɡAWB | oo-ɡAHV)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or עֻגָּב; from [[H5689]] in the original sense of breathing; a reed-instrument of music; organ.

- a musical instrument
    - perhaps a flute, reed-pipe, or panpipes
