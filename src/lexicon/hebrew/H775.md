# H775 אַרְפַּכְשַׁד / ארפכשד

## ʼArpakshad

_(ar-pak-shad' | ar-pahk-SHAHD | ar-pahk-SHAHD)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; I shall fail as the breast, he cursed the breast-bottle; probably of foreign origin; Arpakshad, a son of Noah; also the region settled by him; Arphaxad.

- third son of Shem
