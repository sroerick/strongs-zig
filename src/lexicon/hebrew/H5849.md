# H5849 עָטַר / עטר

## ʻâṭar

_(aw-tar' | aw-TAHR | ah-TAHR)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to encircle (for attack or protection); especially to crown (literally or figuratively); compass, crown.

- to surround
    - (Qal) to surround
- to crown, give a crown
    - (Piel) to crown
    - (Hiphil) crown-bestower (participle)
