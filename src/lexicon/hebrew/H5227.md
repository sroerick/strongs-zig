# H5227 נֹכַח / נכח

## nôkach

_(no'-kakh | noh-KA | noh-HAHK)_

### Definition

adverb; from the same as [[H5226]]; properly, the front part; used adverbially (especially with preposition), opposite, in front of, forward, in behalf of; (over) against, before, direct(-ly), for, right (on).

- be in front of
- in front of, opposite to, in the sight of, before, to the front, right on
- towards the front of, in front of, on behalf of, as far as in front of
