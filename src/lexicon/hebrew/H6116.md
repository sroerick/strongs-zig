# H6116 עֲצָרָה / עצרה

## ʻătsârâh

_(ats-aw-raw' | uh-tsaw-RAW | uh-tsa-RA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; or עֲצֶרֶת; from [[H6113]]; an assembly, especially on a festival or holiday; (solemn) assembly (meeting).

- assembly, solemn assembly
    - assembly (sacred or festive meeting)
    - assemblage, company, group
