# H5228 נָכֹחַ / נכח

## nâkôach

_(naw-ko'-akh | naw-KOH-ah | na-HOH-ak)_

### Definition

adjective; from the same as [[H5226]]; straightforward, i.e., (figuratively), equitable, correct, or (abstractly), integrity; plain, right, uprightness.

- straight, right, straightness, be in front of
