# H5982 עַמּוּד / עמוד

## ʻammûwd

_(am-mood' | am-MOOD | ah-MOOD)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or עַמֻּד; from [[H5975]]; a column (as standing); also a stand, i.e., platform; [[lexicon-key|×]] apiece, pillar.

- pillar, column
    - pillar
    - column, upright
    - column (of smoke)
