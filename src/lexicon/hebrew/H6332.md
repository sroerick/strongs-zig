# H6332 פּוּר / פור

## Pûwr

_(poor | poor | poor)_

### Definition

masculine noun; lot, piece; also (plural) פּוּרִים; or פֻּרִים; from [[H6331]]; a lot (as by means of a broken piece); Pur, Purim.

- lot
    - a special feast among the post-exilic Jews, to celebrate their deliverance from Haman's destruction through queen Esther's heroic actions
