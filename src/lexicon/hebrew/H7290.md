# H7290 רָדַם / רדם

## râdam

_(raw-dam' | raw-DAHM | ra-DAHM)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to stun, i.e., stupefy (with sleep or death); (be in a deep, cast into a dead, that) (be fast a-)sleep(-er, -eth).

- (Niphal) to be asleep, be unconscious, be in heavy sleep, fall into heavy sleep, be fast asleep
