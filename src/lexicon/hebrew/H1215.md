# H1215 בֶּצַע / בצע

## betsaʻ

_(beh'-tsah | beh-TSA | beh-TSA)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H1214]]; plunder; by extension, gain (usually unjust); covetousness, (dishonest) gain, lucre, profit.

- profit, unjust gain, gain (profit) acquired by violence
