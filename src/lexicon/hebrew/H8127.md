# H8127 שֵׁן / שן

## shên

_(shane | shane | shane)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from [[H8150]]; compare [[H8143]]; a tooth (as sharp); specifically ivory; figuratively, a cliff; crag, [[lexicon-key|×]] forefront, ivory, [[lexicon-key|×]] sharp, tooth.

- tooth, ivory
    - tooth
        - of man, lex talionis, beast
    - tooth, tine (of fork)
    - ivory
        - as material
        - of commerce
    - sharp pointed rock
