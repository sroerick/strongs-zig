# H7981 שְׁלֵט / שלט

## shᵉlêṭ

_(shel-ate' | sheh-LATE | sheh-LATE)_

### Definition

verb; corresponding to [[H7980]]; to rule over; have the mastery, have power, bear rule, be (make) ruler.

- to have power, rule, domineer, rule over
    - (Peal) have power upon or over, rule, fall upon, assault, be ruler
    - (Aphel) make ruler
