# H811 אֶשְׁכּוֹל / אשכול

## ʼeshkôwl

_(esh-kole' | esh-KOLE | esh-KOLE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or אֶשְׁכֹּל; probably prolonged from [[H810]]; a bunch of grapes or other fruit; cluster (of grapes).

- cluster
    - of grapes
    - of flowers (metaphor of lover)
