# H6554 פַּרְפַּר / פרפר

## Parpar

_(par-par' | pahr-PAHR | pahr-PAHR)_

### Definition

proper noun; swift; probably from [[H6565]] in the sense of rushing; rapid; Parpar, a river of Syria; Pharpar.

- a stream in the district of Damascus identified with the modern 'Awaj'; rises on the southeast slopes of Mount Hermon and flows into the southernmost lake of Damascus
