# H1198 בַּעַר / בער

## baʻar

_(bah'-ar | ba-AR | ba-AR)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H1197]]; properly, foot (as consumed); i.e., (by exten.) of cattle brutishness; (concretely) stupid; brutish (person), foolish.

- brutishness, stupidity, brutish (person)
