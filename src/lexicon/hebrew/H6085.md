# H6085 עֶפְרוֹן / עפרון

## ʻEphrôwn

_(ef-rone' | ep-RONE | ef-RONE)_

### Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; fawn-like; from the same as [[H6081]]; fawn-like; Ephron, the name of a Canaanite and of two places in Palestine; Ephron, Ephrain (from the margin).

- a Hittite, son of Zohar and the one from whom Abraham bought the field and cave of Machpelah (noun proper masculine)
- a city on the borders of Benjamin (noun proper locative)
- a mountain on the northern border of Judah (noun proper locative)
