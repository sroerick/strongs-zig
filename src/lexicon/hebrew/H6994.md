# H6994 קָטֹן / קטן

## qâṭôn

_(kaw-tone' | kaw-TONE | ka-TONE)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root (rather denominative from [[H6996]]); to diminish, i.e., be (causatively, make) diminutive or (figuratively) of no account; be a (make) small (thing), be not worthy.

- to be small, be insignificant
    - (Qal) to be insignificant
    - (Hiphil) to make small, reduce significantly
