# H780 אֲרָרַט / אררט

## ʼĂrâraṭ

_(ar-aw-rat' | uh-raw-RAHT | uh-ra-RAHT)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; the curse reversed, precipitation of curse; of foreign origin; Ararat (or rather Armenia); Ararat, Armenia.

- a mountainous region of eastern Armenia, between the river Araxes and the lakes Van and Oroomiah, the site where Noah's ark came to rest
- ([[lexicon-key|TWOT]]) the mountain where Noah's ark came to rest
