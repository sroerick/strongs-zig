# H825 אַשָּׁף / אשף

## ʼashshâph

_(ash-shawf' | ash-SHAWP | ah-SHAHF)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from an unused root (probably meaning to lisp, i.e., practice enchantment); a conjurer; astrologer.

- necromancer, conjurer
- ([[lexicon-key|TWOT]]) astrologer, enchanter, exorcist
