# H8213 שָׁפֵל / שפל

## shâphêl

_(shaw-fale' | shaw-PALE | sha-FALE)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to depress or sink (especially figuratively, to humiliate, intransitive or transitive); abase, bring (cast, put) down, debase, humble (self), be (bring, lay, make, put) low(-er).

- to be or become low, sink, be humbled, be abased
    - (Qal) to be or become low
    - (Hiphil)
        - to lay or bring low, humiliate
        - to set in a lower place, show abasement
        - to make low, sit down
