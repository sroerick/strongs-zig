# H6560 פֶּרֶץ עֻזָּא / פרץ עזא

## Perets ʻUzzâʼ

_(peh'-rets ooz-zaw' | peh-RETS oodz-ZAW | peh-RETS oo-ZA)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; breach of Uzza; from [[H6556]] and [[H5798]]; break of Uzza; Perets-Uzza, a place in Palestine; Perezuzza.

- the place where Uzzah was slain by God for touching the Ark; located close to Jerusalem
