# H433 אֱלוֹהַּ / אלוה

## ʼĕlôwahh

_(el-o'-ah | eh-LOH-ah | ay-LOH-ah)_

### Definition

masculine noun; rarely (shortened) אֱלֹהַּ; probably prolonged (emphatic) from [[H410]]; a deity or the Deity; God, god.

- God
- false god
