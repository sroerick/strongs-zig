# H6106 עֶצֶם / עצם

## ʻetsem

_(eh'tsem | eh-TSEM | eh-TSEM)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from [[H6105]]; a bone (as strong); by extension, the body; figuratively, the substance, i.e., (as pron.) selfsame; body, bone, [[lexicon-key|×]] life, (self-)same, strength, [[lexicon-key|×]] very.

- bone, essence, substance
    - bone
        - body, limbs, members, external body
    - bone (of animal)
    - substance, self
