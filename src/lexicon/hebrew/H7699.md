# H7699 שַׁד / שד

## shad

_(shad | shahd | shahd)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or שֹׁד; probably from [[H7736]] (in its original sense) contracted; the breast of a woman or animal (as bulging); breast, pap, teat.

- breast, bosom, (female) breast
    - breast (of woman)
    - breast (of animal)
    - breast (of both human and animal)
