# H4769 מַרְבֵּץ / מרבץ

## marbêts

_(mar-bates' | mahr-BAYTS | mahr-BAYTS)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H7257]]; a reclining place, i.e., fold (for flocks); couching place, place to lie down.

- place of lying down, resting or dwelling place
    - of wild beasts
