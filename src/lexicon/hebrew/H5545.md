# H5545 סָלַח / סלח

## çâlach

_(saw-lakh' | saw-LA | sa-LAHK)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to forgive; forgive, pardon, spare.

- to forgive, pardon
    - (Qal) to forgive, pardon
    - (Niphal) to be forgiven
