# H6177 עֲרוֹעֵר / ערוער

## ʻĂrôwʻêr

_(ar-o-ayr' | uh-roh-ARE | uh-roh-ARE)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; ruins; or עֲרֹעֵר; or עַרְעוֹר; the same as [[H6176]]; a juniper or stripped; Aroër, the name of three places in or near Palestine; Aroer.

- a city on the north bank of the river Arnon, the southern point of the territory of Sihon the king of the Amorites and later of Reuben; modern 'Arair'
- a city in Ammon near the Jabbok belonging to Gad
- a town in southern Judah
