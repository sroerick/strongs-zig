# H7146 קָרַחַת / קרחת

## qârachath

_(kaw-rakh'-ath | kaw-ra-HAHT | ka-ra-HAHT)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from [[H7139]]; a bald spot (on the back of the head); figuratively, a threadbare spot (on the back side of the cloth); bald head, bare within.

- baldness of head, back baldness, bald spot
