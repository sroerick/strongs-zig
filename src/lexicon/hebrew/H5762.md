# H5762 עֲוִית / עוית

## ʻĂvîyth

_(av-veeth' | uh-WEET | uh-VEET)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; ruins; or perhaps (עַיּוֹת (as if plural of [[H5857]]) (עַיוּת; from [[H5753]]; ruin; Avvith (or Avvoth), a place in Palestine; Avith.

- the city of Hadad Ben-Bedad, one of the kings of Edom
