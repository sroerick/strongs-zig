# H2822 חֹשֶׁךְ / חשך

## chôshek

_(kho-shek' | hoh-SHEK | hoh-SHEK)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H2821]]; the dark; hence (literally) darkness; figuratively, misery, destruction, death, ignorance, sorrow, wickedness; dark(-ness), night, obscurity.

- darkness, obscurity
    - darkness
    - secret place
