# H6679 צוּד / צוד

## tsûwd

_(tsood | tsood | tsood)_

### Definition

verb; a denominative from [[H6718]]; to victual (for a journey); chase, hunt, sore, take (provision).

- to hunt
    - (Qal) to hunt
    - (Poel) to hunt, hunt eagerly or keenly
    - (Hithpael) take provision
