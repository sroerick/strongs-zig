# H3491 יָתוּר / יתור

## yâthûwr

_(yaw-thoor' | yaw-TOOR | ya-TOOR)_

### Definition

verb; passive participle of [[H3498]]; properly, what is left, i.e., (by implication) a gleaning; range.

- to remain over, leave, range over
- ([[lexicon-key|BDB]]) a searching (noun masculine)
