# H7049 קָלַע / קלע

## qâlaʻ

_(kaw-lah' | kaw-LA | ka-LA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to sling; also to carve (as if a circular motion, or into light forms); carve, sling (out).

- to sling, hurl forth
    - (Qal)
        - to sling
        - slinger (participle)
    - (Piel) to sling
- (Qal) to carve
