# H970 בָּחוּר / בחור

## bâchûwr

_(baw-khoor' | baw-HOOR | ba-HOOR)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or בָּחֻר; participle passive of [[H977]]; properly, selected, i.e., a youth (often collective); (choice) young (man), chosen, [[lexicon-key|×]] hole.

- youth, young man
