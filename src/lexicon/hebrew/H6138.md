# H6138 עֶקְרוֹן / עקרון

## ʻEqrôwn

_(ek-rone' | ek-RONE | ek-RONE)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; emigration, torn up by the roots; from [[H6131]]; eradication; Ekron, a place in Palestine; Ekron.

- the most northerly of the 5 principal cities of the Philistines; located in the lowlands of Judah and later given to Dan
