# H827 אַשְׁפָּה / אשפה

## ʼashpâh

_(ash-paw' | ash-PAW | ash-PA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; perhaps (feminine) from the same as [[H825]] (in the sense of covering); a quiver or arrow-case; quiver.

- quiver (for arrows)
    - of home, of God's instruments (figuratively)
