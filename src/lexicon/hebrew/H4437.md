# H4437 מַלְכוּ / מלכו

## malkûw

_(mal-koo' | mahl-KOO | mahl-HOO)_

### Definition

feminine noun; corresponding to [[H4438]]; dominion (abstractly or concretely); kingdom, kingly, realm, reign.

- royalty, reign, kingdom
    - royalty, kingship, kingly authority
    - kingdom
    - realm (of territory)
    - reign (of time)
