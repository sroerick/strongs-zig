# H5707 עֵד / עד

## ʻêd

_(ayd | ade | ade)_

### Definition

masculine noun; contracted from [[H5749]]; concretely, a witness; abstractly, testimony; specifically, a recorder, i.e., prince; witness.

- witness
    - witness, testimony, evidence (of things)
    - witness (of people)
