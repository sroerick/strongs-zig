# H6551 פַּרְעֹשׁ / פרעש

## Parʻôsh

_(par-oshe' | pahr-OHSH | pahr-OHSH)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; flea; the same as [[H6550]]; Parosh, the name of our Israelite; Parosh, Pharosh.

- ancestor of a family who returned from exile with Zerubbabel and a 2nd group who returned with Ezra
- a leader of the people who signed the covenant with Nehemiah
