# H3468 יֶשַׁע / ישע

## yeshaʻ

_(yeh'-shah | yeh-SHA | yeh-SHA)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or יֵשַׁע; from [[H3467]]; liberty, deliverance, prosperity; safety, salvation, saving.

- deliverance, salvation, rescue, safety, welfare
    - safety, welfare, prosperity
    - salvation
    - victory
