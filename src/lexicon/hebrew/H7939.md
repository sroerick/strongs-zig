# H7939 שָׂכָר / שכר

## sâkâr

_(saw-kawr' | saw-KAWR | sa-HAHR)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H7936]]; payment of contract; concretely, salary, fare, maintenance; by implication, compensation, benefit; hire, price, reward(-ed), wages, worth.

- hire, wages
    - wages
    - reward, pay
    - fare, fee, passage-money
