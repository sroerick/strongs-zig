# H8130 שָׂנֵא / שנא

## sânêʼ

_(saw-nay' | saw-NAY | sa-NAY)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to hate (personally); enemy, foe, (be) hate(-ful, -r), odious, [[lexicon-key|×]] utterly.

- to hate, be hateful
    - (Qal) to hate
        - of man
        - of God
        - hater, one hating, enemy (participle) (substantive)
    - (Niphal) to be hated
    - (Piel) hater (participle)
        - of persons, nations, God, wisdom
