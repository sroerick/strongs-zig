# H3489 יָתֵד / יתד

## yâthêd

_(yaw-thade' | yaw-TADE | ya-TADE)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from an unused root meaning to pin through or fast; a peg; nail, paddle, pin, stake.

- pin, stake, peg, nail
    - pin, peg, tent pin, tent stake
    - nail, pin (figuratively)
    - pin (used in weaving)
