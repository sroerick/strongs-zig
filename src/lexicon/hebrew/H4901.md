# H4901 מֶשֶׁךְ / משך

## meshek

_(meh'shek | meh-SHEK | meh-SHEK)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H4900]]; a sowing; also a possession; precious, price.

- a drawing, drawing up, drawing up a trail
    - the drawing up, fishing up, securing, acquiring
    - a trail (of seed)
