## Yᵉrîyʼêl

_(yer-ee-ale' | yeh-ree-ALE | yeh-ree-ALE)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; taught by God; from [[H3384]] and [[H410]]; thrown of God; Jeriel, an Israelite; Jeriel.

- one of the 6 heads of the house of Tola of the tribe of Issachar
