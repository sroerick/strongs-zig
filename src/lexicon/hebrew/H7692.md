# H7692 שִׁגָּיוֹן / שגיון

## shiggâyôwn

_(shig-gaw-yone' | shig-gaw-YONE | shee-ɡa-YONE)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; or שִׁגָּיֹנָה; from [[H7686]]; properly, aberration, i.e., (technically) a dithyramb or rambling poem; Shiggaion, Shigionoth.
