# H8354 שָׁתָה / שתה

## shâthâh

_(shaw-thaw' | shaw-TAW | sha-TA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to imbibe (literally or figuratively); [[lexicon-key|×]] assuredly, banquet, [[lexicon-key|×]] certainly, drink(-er, -ing), drunk, [[lexicon-key|×]] drunkard, surely.

- to drink
    - (Qal)
        - to drink
            - of drinking cup of God's wrath, of slaughter, of wicked deeds (figuratively)
        - to feast
    - (Niphal) to be drunk


### Note

Prop. intensive of [[H8248]].
