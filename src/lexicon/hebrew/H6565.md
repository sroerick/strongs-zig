# H6565 פָּרַר / פרר

## pârar

_(paw-rar' | paw-RAHR | pa-RAHR)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to break up (usually figuratively), i.e., to violate, frustrate; [[lexicon-key|×]] any ways, break (asunder), cast off, cause to cease, [[lexicon-key|×]] clean, defeat, disannul, disappoint, dissolve, divide, make of none effect, fail, frustrate, bring (come) to nought, [[lexicon-key|×]] utterly, make void.

- to break, frustrate
    - (Hiphil)
        - to break, violate
        - to frustrate, make ineffectual
    - (Hophal)
        - to be frustrated
        - to be broken
        - to break
    - (Pilpel) to break to bits, shatter
- to split, divide
    - (Qal) to split, crack through
    - (Poel) to break apart
    - (Hithpoel) to be split, be cracked through
