# H6252 עַשְׁתָּרוֹת / עשתרות

## ʻAshtârôwth

_(ash-taw-roth' | ash-taw-ROTE | ash-ta-ROTE)_

### Definition

proper feminine noun; star; or עַשְׁתָּרֹת; plural of [[H6251]]; Ashtaroth, the name of a Sidonian deity, and of a place East of the Jordan; Asharoth, Astaroth.

- false goddesses in the Canaanite religion, usually related to fertility cult (noun proper feminine deity)
- a city in Bashan east of the Jordan given to Manasseh (noun proper locative)
