# H8662 תַּרְתָּק / תרתק

## Tartâq

_(tar-tawk' | tahr-TAWK | tahr-TAHK)_

### Definition

proper noun; prince of darkness; of foreign derivation; Tartak, a deity of the Avvites; Tartak.

- one of the deities of the Avite people of Samaria
    - according to tradition, worshipped under the form of an ass
