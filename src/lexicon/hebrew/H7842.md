# H7842 שַׁחֲרַיִם / שחרים

## Shachărayim

_(shakh-ar-ah'-yim | sha-huh-ra-YIM | sha-huh-ra-YEEM)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; double dawn; dual of [[H7837]]; double dawn; Shacharajim, an Israelite; Shaharaim.

- a Benjamite, father of Jobab, Zibia, Mesha, Malcham, Jeuz, Shachia, Mirma, Abitub, and Elpaal
