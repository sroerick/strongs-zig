_(ker-ee-yoth' | keh-rih-YOTE | keh-ree-YOTE)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; cities; plural of [[H7151]]; buildings; Kerioth, the name of two places in Palestine; Kerioth, Kirioth.

- a town in the southern district of Judah
