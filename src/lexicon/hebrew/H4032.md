# H4032 מָגוֹר / מגור

## mâgôwr

_(maw-gore' | maw-ɡORE | ma-ɡORE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or (Lamentations 2:22) מָגוּר; from [[H1481]] in the sense of fearing; a fright (objective or subjective); fear, terror.

- fear, terror
