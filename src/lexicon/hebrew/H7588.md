# H7588 שָׁאוֹן / שאון

## shâʼôwn

_(shaw-one' | shaw-ONE | sha-ONE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H7582]]; uproar (as of rushing); by implication, destruction; [[lexicon-key|×]] horrible, noise, pomp, rushing, tumult, [[lexicon-key|×]] tumultuous.

- roar, din, crash, uproar
    - roar (of water)
    - uproar (of revellers)
