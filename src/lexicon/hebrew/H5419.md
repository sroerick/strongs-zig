# H5419 נְתַן־מֶלֶךְ / נתןמלך

## Nᵉthan-Melek

_(neth-an' meh'-lek | neh-TAHN meh-LEK | neh-TAHN meh-LEK)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; the gift of the king; from [[H5414]] and [[H4428]]; given of (the) king; Nethan-Melek, an Israelite; Nathanmelech.

- an official of Judah in the time of king Josiah
