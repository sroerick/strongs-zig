# H7287 רָדָה / רדה

## râdâh

_(raw-daw' | raw-DAW | ra-DA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to tread down, i.e., subjugate; specifically, to crumble off; (come to, make to) have dominion, prevail against, reign, (bear, make to) rule,(-r) (over), take.

- to rule, have dominion, dominate, tread down
    - (Qal) to have dominion, rule, subjugate
    - (Hiphil) to cause to dominate
- to scrape out
    - (Qal) to scrape, scrape out
