# H7613 שְׂאֵת / שאת

## sᵉʼêth

_(seh-ayth' | seh-ATE | seh-ATE)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from [[H5375]]; an elevation or leprous scab; figuratively, elation or cheerfulness; exaltation in rank or character; be accepted, dignity, excellency, highness, raise up self, rising.

- elevation, exaltation, dignity, swelling, uprising
    - dignity, exaltation, loftiness
    - swelling
    - uprising
