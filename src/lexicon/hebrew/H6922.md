# H6922 קַדִּישׁ / קדיש

## qaddîysh

_(kad-deesh' | kahd-DEESH | ka-DEESH)_

### Definition

adjective; corresponding to [[H6918]]; sacred (ceremonially or morally); (as noun) God (by eminence), an angel, a saint, a sanctuary; holy (One), saint.

- holy, separate
- ([[lexicon-key|TWOT]]) angels, saints
