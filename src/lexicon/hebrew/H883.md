# H883 בְּאֵר לַחַי רֹאִי / באר לחי ראי

## Bᵉʼêr la-Chay Rôʼîy

_(be-ayr' lakh-ah'ee ro-ee' | beh-ARE la-HAI roh-EE | beh-ARE la-HAI roh-EE)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; well of the Living One seeing me; from [[H875]] and [[H2416]] (with prefix) and [[H7203]]; well of a living (One) my Seer; Beer-Lachai-Roi, a place in the Desert; Beer-lahai-roi.

- a well west of Kadesh, south of Israel
