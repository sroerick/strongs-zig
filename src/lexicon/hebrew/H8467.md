_(tekh-in-naw' | teh-hin-NAW | teh-hee-NA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from [[H2603]]; graciousness; causatively, entreaty; favour, grace, supplication.

- favour, supplication, supplication for favour
    - favour
    - supplication for favour
