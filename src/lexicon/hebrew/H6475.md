# H6475 פָּצָה / פצה

## pâtsâh

_(paw-tsaw' | paw-TSAW | pa-TSA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to rend, i.e., open (especially the mouth); deliver, gape, open, rid, utter.

- to part, open, separate, set free
    - (Qal)
        - to open (mouth), utter
        - to snatch away, set free
