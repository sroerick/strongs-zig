# H8071 שִׂמְלָה / שמלה

## simlâh

_(sim-law' | sim-LAW | seem-LA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; perhaps by permutation for the feminine of [[H5566]] (through the idea of a cover assuming the shape of the object beneath); a dress, especially a mantle; apparel, cloth(-es, -ing), garment, raiment.

- wrapper, mantle, covering garment, garments, clothes, raiment, a cloth


### Note

Compare [[H8008]].
