# H4195 מוֹתָר / מותר

## môwthâr

_(mo-thar' | moh-TAWR | moh-TAHR)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H3498]]; literally gain; figuratively, superiority; plenteousness, preeminence, profit.

- pre-eminence, abundance, profit, superiority
    - abundance, plenty
    - pre-eminence, superiority
