# H6318 פּוֹטִיפַר / פוטיפר

## Pôwṭîyphar

_(po-tee-far' | poh-tee-PAHR | poh-tee-FAHR)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; belonging to the sun; of Egyptian derivation; Potiphar, an Egyptian; Potiphar.

- an officer of Pharaoh, chief of the executioners, and the master to whom Joseph was sold as a slave
