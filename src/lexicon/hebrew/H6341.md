# H6341 פַּח / פח

## pach

_(pakh | pa | pahk)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H6351]]; a (metallic) sheet (as pounded thin); gin, (thin) plate, snare.

- bird trap, trap, snare
    - bird trap (literal)
    - of calamities, plots, source or agent of calamity (figuratively)
- plate (of metal)
