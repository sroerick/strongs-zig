# H5401 נָשַׁק / נשק

## nâshaq

_(naw-shak' | naw-SHAHK | na-SHAHK)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root (identical with [[H5400]], through the idea of fastening up; compare [[H2388]], [[H2836]]); to kiss, literally or figuratively (touch); also (as a mode of attachment), to equip with weapons; armed (men), rule, kiss, that touched.

- to put together, kiss
    - (Qal) to kiss
    - (Piel) to kiss
    - (Hiphil) to touch gently
- to handle, be equipped with
    - (Qal) to be equipped
