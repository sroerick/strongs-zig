# H8394 תָּבוּן / תבון

## tâbûwn

_(taw-boon' | taw-BOON | ta-VOON)_

### Definition

masculine noun; and (feminine) תְּבוּנָה; or תּוֹבֻנָה; from [[H995]]; intelligence; by implication, an argument; by extension, caprice; discretion, reason, skilfulness, understanding, wisdom.

- understanding, intelligence
    - the act of understanding
        - skill
    - the faculty of understanding
        - intelligence, understanding, insight
    - the object of knowledge
    - teacher (personification)
