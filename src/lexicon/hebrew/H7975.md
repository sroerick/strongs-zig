# H7975 שִׁלֹחַ / שלחה

## Shilôach

_(shee-lo'-akh | shih-LOH-ah | shee-LOH-ak)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; sent; or (in imitation of [[H7974]]) שֶׁלַח; (Nehemiah 3:15), from [[H7971]]; rill; Shiloach, a fountain of Jerusalem; Shiloah, Siloah.

- a fountain just southeast of Jerusalem
