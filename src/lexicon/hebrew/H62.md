# H62 אָבֵל בֵּית־מֲעַכָה / אבל ביתמעכה

## ʼÂbêl Bêyth-Măʻakâh

_(aw-bale' bayth ma-akaw' | aw-BALE bate muh-ah-KAW | ah-VALE bate muh-ah-HA)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; meadow of the house of Maachah; from [[H58]] and [[H1004]] and [[H4601]]; meadow of Beth-Maakah; Abel of Beth-maakah, a place in Palestine; Abel-beth-maachah, Abel of Beth-maachah.

- city in northern Israel near Beth Maachah
