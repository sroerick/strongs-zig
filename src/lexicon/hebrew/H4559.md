# H4559 מִסְפֶּרֶת / מספרת

## Miçpereth

_(mis-peh'-reth | mis-peh-RET | mees-peh-RET)_

### Definition

proper feminine noun; number; feminine of [[H4437]]; enumeration; Mispereth, an Israelite; Mispereth.
