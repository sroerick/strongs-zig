# H399 אֲכַל / אכל

## ʼăkal

_(ak-al' | uh-KAHL | uh-HAHL)_

### Definition

verb; corresponding to [[H398]]; to eat; [[lexicon-key|+]] accuse, devour, eat.

- to eat, devour
    - (Peal)
        - to eat (of beasts)
        - to devour
        - eat their pieces (in a phrase, that is, slander them)
