# H8336 שֵׁשׁ / שש

## shêsh

_(shaysh | shaysh | shaysh)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or (for alliteration with [[H4897]]) שְׁשִׁי; for [[H7893]]; bleached stuff, i.e., white linen or (by analogy) marble; [[lexicon-key|×]] blue, fine (twined) linen, marble, silk.

- something bleached white, byssus, linen, fine linen
- alabaster, similar stone, marble
