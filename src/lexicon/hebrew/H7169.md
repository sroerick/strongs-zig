# H7169 קָרַץ / קרץ

## qârats

_(kaw-rats' | kaw-RAHTS | ka-RAHTS)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to pinch, i.e., (partially) to bite the lips, blink the eyes (as a gesture of malice), or (fully) to squeeze off (a piece of clay in order to mould a vessel from it); form, move, wink.

- to narrow, form, nip, pinch, squeeze, wink, purse
    - (Qal) to pinch
    - (Pual) to be nipped or pinched off, be formed
