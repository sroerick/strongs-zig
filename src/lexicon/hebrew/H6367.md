# H6367 פִּי הַחִירֹת / פי החרת

## Pîy ha-Chîyrôth

_(pee hah-khee-roth' | pee ha-hee-ROTE | pee ha-hee-ROTE)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; place where sedge grows; from [[H6310]] and the feminine plural of a noun (from the same root as [[H2356]]), with the article interpolated; mouth of the gorges; Pi-ha-Chiroth, a place in Egypt; Pi-hahiroth.

- the 3rd encampment of the Israelites after leaving Goshen in Egypt and the last one before crossing the Red Sea


### Note

In Numbers 14:19 without 'Pi-'.
