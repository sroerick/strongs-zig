# H7133 קׇרְבָּן / קרבן

## qorbân

_(kor-bawn' | kore-BAWN | kore-BAHN)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or קֻרְבָּן; from [[H7126]]; something brought near the altar, i.e., a sacrificial present; oblation, that is offered, offering.

- offering, oblation
