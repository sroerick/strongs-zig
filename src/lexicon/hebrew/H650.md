# H650 אָפִיק / אפיק

## ʼâphîyq

_(aw-feek' | aw-PEEK | ah-FEEK)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H622]]; properly, containing, i.e., a tube; also a bed or valley of a stream; also a strong thing or a hero; brook, channel, mighty, river, [[lexicon-key|+]] scale, stream, strong piece.

- channel
- ravine
- of hollow bones (figuratively)
