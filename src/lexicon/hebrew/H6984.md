# H6984 קוּשָׁיָהוּ / קושיהו

## qûwshâyâhûw

_(koo-shaw-yaw'-hoo | koo-shaw-yaw-HOO | koo-sha-ya-HOO)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; bow of Jehovah; from the passive participle of [[H6983]] and [[H3050]]; entrapped of Jah; Kushajah, an Israelite; Kushaiah.

- father of Ethan the Merarite
    - same as 'Kish' or 'Kishi'
