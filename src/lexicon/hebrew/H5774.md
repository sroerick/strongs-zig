# H5774 עוּף / עוף

## ʻûwph

_(oof | oop | oof)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to cover (with wings or obscurity); hence (as denominative from [[H5775]]); to fly; also (by implication of dimness) to faint (from the darkness of swooning); brandish, be (wax) faint, flee away, fly (away), [[lexicon-key|×]] set, shine forth, weary.

- to fly, fly about, fly away (verb)
    - (Qal)
        - to fly, hover
        - to fly away
    - (Hiphil) to cause to fly, light upon
    - (Polel)
        - to fly about or to and fro
        - to cause to fly to and fro, brandish
    - (Hithpolel) to fly away
- (Qal) to cover, be dark (verb)
- gloom (noun feminine)
