# H899 בֶּגֶד / בגד

## beged

_(behg'-ed | beh-ɡED | beh-ɡED)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H898]]; a covering, i.e., clothing; apparel, cloth(-es, -ing), garment, lap, rag, raiment, robe, [[lexicon-key|×]] very (treacherously), vesture, wardrobe.

- treachery, deceit
- ([[lexicon-key|CLBL]]) garment, clothing (used indiscriminately)
