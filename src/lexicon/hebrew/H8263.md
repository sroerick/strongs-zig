# H8263 שֶׁקֶץ / שקץ

## sheqets

_(sheh'-kets | sheh-KETS | sheh-KETS)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H8262]]; filth, i.e., (figuratively and specifically) an idolatrous object; abominable(-tion).

- detestable thing or idol, an unclean thing, an abomination, detestation
