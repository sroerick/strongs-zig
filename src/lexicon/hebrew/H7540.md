# H7540 רָקַד / רקד

## râqad

_(raw-kad' | raw-KAHD | ra-KAHD)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to stamp, i.e., to spring about (wildly or for joy); dance, jump, leap, skip.

- to skip about
    - (Qal) to skip about
    - (Piel) to dance, leap
    - (Hiphil) to make to skip
