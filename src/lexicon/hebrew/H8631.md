# H8631 תְּקֵף / תקף

## tᵉqêph

_(tek-afe' | teh-KAPE | teh-KAFE)_

### Definition

verb; corresponding to [[H8630]]; to become (causatively, make) mighty or (figuratively) obstinate; make firm, harden, be(-come) strong.

- to grow strong, be hardened
    - (Peal)
        - to grow strong, be hardened
        - to grow arrogant (figuratively)
    - (Pael) to make strong, make firm, make stringent
