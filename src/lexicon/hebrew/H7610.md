# H7610 שְׁאָר יָשׁוּב / שאר ישוב

## Shᵉʼâr Yâshûwb

_(sheh-awr'yaw-shoob' | sheh-AWR yaw-SHOOB | sheh-AR ya-SHOOV)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; a remnant shall return; from [[H7605]] and [[H7725]]; a remnant will return; Shear-Jashub, the symbolic name of one of Isaiah's sons; Shear-jashub.

- a symbolical name for the son of Isaiah the prophet
