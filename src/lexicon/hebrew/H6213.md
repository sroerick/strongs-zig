# H6213 עָשָׂה / עשה

## ʻâsâh

_(aw-saw' | aw-SAW | ah-SA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to do or make, in the broadest sense and widest application; accomplish, advance, appoint, apt, be at, become, bear, bestow, bring forth, bruise, be busy, [[lexicon-key|×]] certainly, have the charge of, commit, deal (with), deck, [[lexicon-key|+]] displease, do, (ready) dress(-ed), (put in) execute(-ion), exercise, fashion, [[lexicon-key|+]] feast, (fight-)ing man, [[lexicon-key|+]] finish, fit, fly, follow, fulfill, furnish, gather, get, go about, govern, grant, great, [[lexicon-key|+]] hinder, hold (a feast), [[lexicon-key|×]] indeed, [[lexicon-key|+]] be industrious, [[lexicon-key|+]] journey, keep, labour, maintain, make, be meet, observe, be occupied, offer, [[lexicon-key|+]] officer, pare, bring (come) to pass, perform, pracise, prepare, procure, provide, put, requite, [[lexicon-key|×]] sacrifice, serve, set, shew, [[lexicon-key|×]] sin, spend, [[lexicon-key|×]] surely, take, [[lexicon-key|×]] thoroughly, trim, [[lexicon-key|×]] very, [[lexicon-key|+]] vex, be (warr-)ior, work(-man), yield, use.

- to do, fashion, accomplish, make
    - (Qal)
        - to do, work, make, produce
            - to do
            - to work
            - to deal (with)
            - to act, act with effect, effect
        - to make
            - to make
            - to produce
            - to prepare
            - to make (an offering)
            - to attend to, put in order
            - to observe, celebrate
            - to acquire (property)
            - to appoint, ordain, institute
            - to bring about
            - to use
            - to spend, pass
    - (Niphal)
        - to be done
        - to be made
        - to be produced
        - to be offered
        - to be observed
        - to be used
    - (Pual) to be made
- (Piel) to press, squeeze
