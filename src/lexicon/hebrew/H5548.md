# H5548 סַלְכָה / סלכה

## Çalkâh

_(sal-kaw' | sahl-KAW | sahl-HA)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; migration; from an unused root. meaning to walk; walking; Salcah, a place East of the Jordan; Salcah, Salchah.

- a town or district at the extreme eastern limit of Bashan and allocated to the tribe of Gad; modern 'Sulkhad' which is 56 miles
