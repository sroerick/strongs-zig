# H5360 נְקָמָה / נקמה

## nᵉqâmâh

_(nek-aw-maw' | neh-kaw-MAW | neh-ka-MA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; feminine of [[H5359]]; avengement, whether the act of the passion; [[lexicon-key|+]] avenge, revenge(-ing), vengeance.

- vengeance
    - vengeance (by God, by Israel, by enemies of Israel)
