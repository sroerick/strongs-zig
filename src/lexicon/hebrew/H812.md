# H812 אֶשְׁכֹּל / אשכל

## ʼEshkôl

_(esh-kole' | esh-KOLE | esh-KOLE)_

### Definition

proper noun; cluster; the same as [[H811]]; Eshcol, the name of an Amorite, also of a valley in Palestine; Eshcol.

- an area of Hebron, the valley of Eshcol
- an Amorite, the brother of Mamre, dwelling in Hebron
