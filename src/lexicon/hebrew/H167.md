# H167 אָהַל / אהל

## ʼâhal

_(aw-hal' | aw-HAHL | ah-HAHL)_

### Definition

verb; a denominative from [[H168]]; to tent; pitch (remove) a tent.

- to pitch a tent, to move a tent
    - (Qal) pitch or remove a tent
    - (Piel) to pitch one's tent
