# H8232 שְׁפַר / שפר

## shᵉphar

_(shef-ar' | sheh-PAHR | sheh-FAHR)_

### Definition

verb; corresponding to [[H8231]]; to be beautiful; be acceptable, please, [[lexicon-key|+]] think good.

- to be fair, be seemly, seem good, seem pleasing
    - (Peal) to seem good, be acceptable
