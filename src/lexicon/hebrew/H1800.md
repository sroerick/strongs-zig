# H1800 דַּל / דל

## dal

_(dal | dahl | dahl)_

### Definition

adjective; from [[H1809]]; properly, dangling, i.e., (by implication) weak or thin; lean, needy, poor (man), weaker.

- low, poor, weak, thin, one who is low
