# H2426 חֵיל / חיל

## chêyl

_(khale | hale | hale)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or (shortened) חֵל; a collateral form of [[H2428]]; an army; also (by analogy,) an intrenchment; army, bulwark, host, [[lexicon-key|+]] poor, rampart, trench, wall.

- rampart, fortress, wall
    - rampart
    - fortress
