# H8067 שְׁמִינִית / שמינית

## shᵉmîynîyth

_(shem-ee-neeth' | sheh-mee-NEET | sheh-mee-NEET)_

### Definition

feminine noun; feminine of [[H8066]]; probably an eight-stringed lyre; Sheminith.

- Sheminith
    - perhaps an eight-stringed musical instrument
    - perhaps a musical notation, i.e. an octave
