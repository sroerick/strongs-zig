# H7549 רָקִיעַ / רקיע

## râqîyaʻ

_(raw-kee'-ah | raw-KEE-ah | ra-KEE-ah)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H7554]]; properly, an expanse, i.e., the firmament or (apparently) visible arch of the sky; firmament.

- extended surface (solid), expanse, firmament
    - expanse (flat as base, support)
    - firmament (of vault of heaven supporting waters above)
        - considered by Hebrews as solid and supporting 'waters' above
