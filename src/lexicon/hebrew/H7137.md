# H7137 קָרֶה / קרה

## qâreh

_(kaw-reh' | kaw-REH | ka-REH)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H7136]]; an (unfortunate) occurrence, i.e., some accidental (ceremonial) disqualification; uncleanness that chanceth.

- chance, accident
    - of nocturnal pollution requiring temporary exodus from camp
