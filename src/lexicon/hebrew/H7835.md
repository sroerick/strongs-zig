# H7835 שָׁחַר / שחר

## shâchar

_(shaw-khar' | shaw-HAHR | sha-HAHR)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root (identical with [[H7836]] through the idea of the duskiness of early dawn); to be dim or dark (in color); be black.

- to be black
    - (Qal) to be black (of skin)
