# H2881 טָבַל / טבל

## ṭâbal

_(taw-bal' | taw-BAHL | ta-VAHL)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to dip, to immerse; dip, plunge.

- to dip, dip into, plunge
    - (Qal)
        - to dip in or into
        - to dip oneself
    - (Niphal) to be dipped
