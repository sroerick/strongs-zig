# H5769 עוֹלָם / עולם

## ʻôwlâm

_(o-lawm' | oh-LAWM | oh-LAHM)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or עֹלָם; from [[H5956]]; properly, concealed, i.e., the vanishing point; generally, time out of mind (past or future), i.e., (practically) eternity; frequentatively, adverbial (especially with prepositional prefix) always; alway(-s), ancient (time), any more, continuance, eternal, (for) (n-)ever(-lasting, -more) (of old), lasting, long (time), (of) old (time), perpetual, at any time, (beginning of the) world ([[lexicon-key|+]] without end).

- long duration, antiquity, futurity, for ever, ever, everlasting, evermore, perpetual, old, ancient, world
    - ancient time, long time (of past)
    - (of future)
        - for ever, always
        - continuous existence, perpetual
        - everlasting, indefinite or unending future, eternity

Compare [[H5331]], [[H5703]].
