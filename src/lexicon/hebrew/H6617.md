# H6617 פָּתַל / פתל

## pâthal

_(paw-thal' | paw-TAHL | pa-TAHL)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to twine, i.e., (literally) to struggle or (figuratively) be (morally) tortuous; (shew self) froward, shew self unsavoury, wrestle.

- to twist
    - (Niphal)
        - to be twisted
        - to wrestle
    - (Hithpael) to be twisted
