# H931 בֹּהֶן / בהן

## bôhen

_(bo'-hen | boh-HEN | boh-HEN)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from an unused root apparently meaning to be thick; the thumb of the hand or great toe of the foot; thumb, great toe.

- thumb, great (big) toe (always used of both together)
