# H7883 שִׁיחוֹר / שיחור

## Shîychôwr

_(shee-khore' | shee-HORE | shee-HORE)_

### Definition

proper feminine noun; dark; or שִׁחוֹר; or שִׁחֹר; probably from [[H7835]]; dark, i.e., turbid; Shichor, a stream of Egypt; Shihor, Sihor.

- a river or canal on east border of Egypt and a branch of the Nile
