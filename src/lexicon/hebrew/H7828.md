# H7828 שַׁחַף / שחף

## shachaph

_(shakh'-af | sha-HAHP | sha-HAHF)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from an unused root meaning to peel, i.e., emaciate; the gull (as thin); cuckoo.

- a ceremonially unclean bird
    - cuckow, gull, seagull, sea-mew
    - maybe an extinct bird, exact meaning unknown
