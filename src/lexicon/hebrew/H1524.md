# H1524 גִּיל / גיל

## gîyl

_(gheel | ɡeel | ɡeel)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H1523]]; a revolution (of time, i.e., an age); also joy; [[lexicon-key|×]] exceedingly, gladness, [[lexicon-key|×]] greatly, joy, rejoice(-ing), sort.

- a rejoicing
- a circle, age
