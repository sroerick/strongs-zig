# H5886 עֵין תַּנִּים / עין תנים

## ʻÊyn Tannîym

_(ane tan-neem' | ane tahn-NEEM | ane ta-NEEM)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; fountain of the jackal; from [[H5869]] and the plural of [[H8565]]; fountain of jackals; En-Tannim, a pool near Jerusalem; dragon well.

- a spring near Jerusalem
