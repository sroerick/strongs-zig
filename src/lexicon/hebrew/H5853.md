# H5853 עַטְרוֹת אַדָּר / עטרות אדר

## ʻAṭrôwth ʼAddâr

_(at-roth' ad-dawr' | at-ROTE ad-DAWR | at-ROTE ah-DAHR)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; crowns of glory; from the same as [[H5852]] and [[H146]]; crowns of Addar; Atroth-Addar, a place in Palestine; Ataroth-adar(-addar).

- a Gadite town on the west border of Benjamin
