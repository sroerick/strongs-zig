# H5676 עֵבֶר / עבר

## ʻêber

_(ay'-ber | ay-BER | ay-VER)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H5674]]; properly, a region across; but used only adverbially (with or without a preposition) on the opposite side (especially of the Jordan; ususally meaning the east); [[lexicon-key|×]] against, beyond, by, [[lexicon-key|×]] from, over, passage, quarter, (other, this) side, straight.

- region beyond or across, side
    - region across or beyond
    - side, opposite side
