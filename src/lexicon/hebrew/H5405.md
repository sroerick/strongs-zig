# H5405 נָשַׁת / נשת

## nâshath

_(naw-shath' | naw-SHAHT | na-SHAHT)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to eliminate, i.e., (intransitively) to dry up; fail.

- to be dry, be parched
    - (Qal) to be dry, be parched
    - (Niphal) to be dried up
