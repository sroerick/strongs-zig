# H5525 סֻכִּי / סכי

## Çukkîy

_(sook-kee' | sook-KEE | soo-KEE)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; booth dwellers; patrial from an unknown name (perhaps [[H5520]]); a Sukkite, or inhabitant of some place near Egypt (i.e., hut-dwellers); Sukkiims.

- a people who supplied warriors for the Egyptian army under Shishak
