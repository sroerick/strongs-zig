# H74 אַבְנֵר / אבנר

## ʼAbnêr

_(ab-nare' | ab-NARE | av-NARE)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; my father is a lamp; or (fully) אֲבִינֵר ; from [[H1]] and [[H5216]]; father of light (i.e., enlightening); Abner, an Israelite; Abner.

- Saul's cousin and army captain, treacherously slain by Joab
