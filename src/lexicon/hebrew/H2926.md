# H2926 טָלַל / טלל

## ṭâlal

_(taw-lal' | taw-LAHL | ta-LAHL)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to strew over, i.e., (by implication) to cover in or plate (with beams); cover.

- (Piel) to cover with a roof, cover over, roof
