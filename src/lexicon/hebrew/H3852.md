# H3852 לֶהָבָה / להבה

## lehâbâh

_(leh-aw-baw' | leh-haw-BAW | leh-ha-VA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; or לַהֶבֶת; feminine of [[H3851]], and meaning the same; flame; flame(-ming), head (of a spear).

- flame
- tip of weapon, point, head of spear
