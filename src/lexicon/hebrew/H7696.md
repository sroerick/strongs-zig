# H7696 שָׁגַע / שגע

## shâgaʻ

_(shaw-gah' | shaw-ɡA | sha-ɡA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to rave through insanity; (be, play the) mad (man).

- to be mad
    - (Pual)
        - to be mad
        - to be maddened (participle)
    - (Hithpael) to show madness
