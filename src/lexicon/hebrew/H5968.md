# H5968 עָלַף / עלף

## ʻâlaph

_(aw-laf' | aw-LAHP | ah-LAHF)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to veil or cover; figuratively, to be languid; faint, overlaid, wrap self.

- to cover
    - (Pual) covered, encrusted (participle)
    - (Hithpael) to enwrap oneself, disguise oneself, faint
