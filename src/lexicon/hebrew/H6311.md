# H6311 פֹּה / פה

## pôh

_(po | poh | poh)_

### Definition

adverb; or פֹּא; (Job 38:11), or פּוֹ; probably from a primitive inseparable particle 'p' (of demonstrative force) and [[H1931]]; this place (French ici), i.e., here or hence; here, hither, the one (other, this, that) side.

- here, from here, hither
    - here
    - hither
