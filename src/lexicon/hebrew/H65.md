# H65 אָבֵל מְחוֹלָה / אבל מחולה

## ʼÂbêl Mᵉchôwlâh

_(aw-bale' mekh-o-law' | aw-BALE meh-hoh-LAW | ah-VALE meh-hoh-LA)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; meadow of dancing; from [[H58]] and [[H4246]]; meadow of dancing; Abel-Mecholah, a place in Palestine; Abel-meholah.

- a city of Issachar, birthplace of Elisha
