# H982 בָּטַח / בטח

## bâṭach

_(baw-takh' | baw-TA | ba-TAHK)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to hie for refuge (but not so precipitately as H2620); figuratively, to trust, be confident or sure; be bold (confident, secure, sure), careless (one, woman), put confidence, (make to) hope, (put, make to) trust.

- to trust
    - (Qal)
        - to trust, trust in
        - to have confidence, be confident
        - to be bold
        - to be secure
    - (Hiphil)
        - to cause to trust, make secure
- ([[lexicon-key|TWOT]]) to feel safe, be careless
