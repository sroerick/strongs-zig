# H6735 צִיר / ציר

## tsîyr

_(tseer | tseer | tseer)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H6696]]; a throe (as a phys. or mental pressure); ambassador, hinge, messenger, pain, pang, sorrow.

- envoy, messenger
- pivot of door, hinge
- pang, distress

Compare [[H6736]].
