# H7017 קֵינִי / קיני

## Qêynîy

_(kay-nee' | kay-NEE | kay-NEE)_

### Definition

adjective; smiths; or קִינִי; (1 Chronicles 2:55), patronymic from [[H7014]]; a Kenite or member of the tribe of Kajin; Kenite.

- the tribe from which the father-in-law of Moses was a member and which lived in the area between southern Palestine and the mountains of Sinai
