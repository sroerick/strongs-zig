# H1281 בָּרִיחַ / בריח

## bârîyach

_(baw-ree'-akh | baw-REE-ah | ba-REE-ak)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or בָּרִחַ; (shortened) from [[H1272]]; a fugitive, i.e., the serpent (as fleeing), and the constellation by that name; crooked, noble, piercing.

- fleeing (adjective)
- fugitive (substantive)
