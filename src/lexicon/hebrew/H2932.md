# H2932 טֻמְאָה / טמאה

## ṭumʼâh

_(toom-aw' | toom-AW | toom-AH)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from [[H2930]]; religious impurity; filthiness, unclean(-ness).

- uncleanness
    - sexual
    - of filthy mass
    - ethical and religious
    - ritual
    - local (of nations)
