# H5380 נָשַׁב / נשב

## nâshab

_(naw-shab' | naw-SHAHB | na-SHAHV)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to blow; by implication, to disperse; (cause to) blow, drive away.

- to blow
    - (Qal) to blow
    - (Hiphil)
        - to cause to blow
        - to disperse, to drive away
