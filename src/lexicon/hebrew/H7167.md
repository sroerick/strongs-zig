# H7167 קָרַע / קרע

## qâraʻ

_(kaw-rah' | kaw-RA | ka-RA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to rend, literally or figuratively (revile, paint the eyes, as if enlarging them); cut out, rend, [[lexicon-key|×]] surely, tear.

- to tear, tear in pieces
    - (Qal)
        - to tear, rend
        - to tear away or out
        - to tear, rend asunder
            - to make wide or large (of eyes)
            - to rend open (of heavens)
        - to tear, rend (of wild beasts)
    - (Niphal) to be rent, be split asunder
