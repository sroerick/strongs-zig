# H6299 פָּדָה / פדה

## pâdâh

_(paw-daw' | paw-DAW | pa-DA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to sever, i.e., ransom; generally to release, preserve; [[lexicon-key|×]] at all, deliver, [[lexicon-key|×]] by any means, ransom, (that are to be, let be) redeem(-ed), rescue, [[lexicon-key|×]] surely.

- to ransom, redeem, rescue, deliver
    - (Qal) to ransom
    - (Niphal) to be ransomed
    - (Hiphil) to allow one to be ransomed
    - (Hophal) redeemed
