# H1788 דִּישֹׁן / דישן

## dîyshôn

_(dee-shone' | dee-SHONE | dee-SHONE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H1758]]; the leaper, i.e., an antelope; pygarg.

- a clean animal
    - pygarg, a kind of antelope or gazelle
    - perhaps mountain goat
    - perhaps an extinct animal, exact meaning unknown
