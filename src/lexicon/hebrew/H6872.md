# H6872 צְרוֹר / צרור

## tsᵉrôwr

_(tser-ore' | tseh-RORE | tseh-RORE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or (shorter) צְרֹר; from [[H6887]]; a parcel (as packed up); also a kernel or particle (as if a package); bag, [[lexicon-key|×]] bendeth, bundle, least grain, small stone.

- bundle, parcel, pouch, bag (as packed)
- pebble
