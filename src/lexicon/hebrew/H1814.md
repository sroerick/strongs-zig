# H1814 דָּלַק / דלק

## dâlaq

_(daw-lak' | daw-LAHK | da-LAHK)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to flame (literally or figuratively); burning, chase, inflame, kindle, persecute(-or), pursue hotly.

- to burn, hotly pursue
    - (Qal)
        - to burn
        - to hotly pursue
    - (Hiphil)
        - to kindle
        - to inflame
