# H5128 נוּעַ / נוע

## nûwaʻ

_(noo'-ah | NOO-ah | NOO-ah)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to waver, in a great variety of applications, literally and figuratively (as subjoined); continually, fugitive, [[lexicon-key|×]] make, to (go) up and down, be gone away, (be) move(-able, -d), be promoted, reel, remove, scatter, set, shake, sift, stagger, to and fro, be vagabond, wag, (make) wander (up and down).

- to quiver, totter, shake, reel, stagger, wander, move, sift, make move, wave, waver, tremble
    - (Qal)
        - to wave, quiver, vibrate, swing, stagger, tremble, be unstable
        - to totter, go tottering
            - vagabond (participle)
    - (Niphal) to be tossed about or around
    - (Hiphil)
        - to toss about
        - to shake, cause to totter
        - to shake, disturb
        - to cause to wander
