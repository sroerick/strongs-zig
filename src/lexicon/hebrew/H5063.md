# H5063 נֶגֶף / נגף

## negeph

_(neh'-ghef | neh-ɡEP | neh-ɡEF)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H5062]]; a trip (of the foot); figuratively, an infliction (of disease); plague, stumbling.

- blow, striking, plague
    - blow, plague (fatal)
    - striking
