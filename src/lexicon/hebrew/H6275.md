# H6275 עָתַק / עתק

## ʻâthaq

_(aw-thak' | aw-TAHK | ah-TAHK)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to remove (intransitive or transitive) figuratively, to grow old; specifically, to transcribe; copy out, leave off, become (wax) old, remove.

- to move, proceed, advance, move on, become old, be removed
    - (Qal)
        - to move
        - to advance (in years), grow old and weak
    - (Hiphil)
        - to move forward, proceed, move on
        - to remove
        - to transcribe
