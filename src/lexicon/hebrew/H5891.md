# H5891 עֵיפָה / עיפה

## ʻÊyphâh

_(ay-faw' | ay-PAW | ay-FA)_

### Definition

proper feminine noun, proper masculine noun; gloomy; the same as [[H5890]]; Ephah, the name of a son of Midian, and of the region settled by him; also of an Israelite and of an Israelitess; Ephah.

- a son of Midian (noun proper masculine)
- a Judaite, son of Jahdai (noun proper masculine)
- a concubine of Caleb in the line of Judah (noun proper feminine)
