# H7684 שְׁגָגָה / שגגה

_(sheg-aw-gaw' | sheh-ɡaw-ɡAW | sheh-ɡa-ɡA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from [[H7683]]; a mistake or inadvertent transgression; error, ignorance, at unawares; unwittingly.

- sin, sin of error or inadvertence, inadvertent sin
