# H809 אֲשִׁישָׁה / אשישה

## ʼăshîyshâh

_(ash-ee-shaw' | uh-shee-SHAW | uh-shee-SHA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; feminine of [[H808]]; something closely pressed together, i.e., a cake of raisins or other comfits; flagon.

- raisin-cake, used in sacrificial feasts
    - consistently translated in AV as "flagon of wine"
