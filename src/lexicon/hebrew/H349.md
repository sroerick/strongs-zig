# H349 אֵיךְ / איך

## ʼêyk

_(ake | ake | ake)_

### Definition

adverb; also אֵיכָה ; and אֵיכָכָה ; prolonged from [[H335]]; how? or how!; also where; how, what.

- how? (interrogative adverb)
- how! (in lamentation) (interjection)
- expression of satisfaction (interjection)
