# H2179 זָנַב / זנב

## zânab

_(zaw-nab' | dzaw-NAHB | za-NAHV)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root meaning to wag; used only as a denominative from [[H2180]]; to curtail, i.e., cut off the rear; smite the hindmost.

- to cut off
    - (Piel) to attack the rear, smite the tail
