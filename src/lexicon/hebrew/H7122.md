# H7122 קָרָא / קרא

## qârâʼ

_(kaw-raw' | kaw-RAW | ka-RA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to encounter, whether accidentally or in a hostile manner; befall, (by) chance, (cause to) come (upon), fall out, happen, meet.

- to encounter, befall, meet
    - (Qal)
        - to meet, encounter
        - to befall (figuratively)
    - (Niphal) to meet, meet unexpectedly
    - (Hiphil) to cause to meet
