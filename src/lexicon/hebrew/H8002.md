# H8002 שֶׁלֶם / שלם

## shelem

_(sheh'-lem | sheh-LEM | sheh-LEM)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H7999]]; properly, requital, i.e., a (voluntary) sacrifice in thanks; peace offering.

- peace offering, requital, sacrifice for alliance or friendship
    - voluntary sacrifice of thanks
