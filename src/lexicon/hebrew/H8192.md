# H8192 שָׁפָה / שפה

## shâphâh

_(shaw-faw' | shaw-PAW | sha-FA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to abrade, i.e., bare; high, stick out.

- to sweep bare, scrape
    - (Niphal) to be wind-swept, be bare, be scraped barren (by wind)
    - (Pual) to be bare, be laid bare
