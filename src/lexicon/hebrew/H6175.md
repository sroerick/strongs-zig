## ʻârûwm

_(aw-room' | aw-ROOM | ah-ROOM)_

### Definition

adjective; passive participle of [[H6191]]; cunning (usually in a bad sense); crafty, prudent, subtil.

- subtle, shrewd, crafty, sly, sensible
    - crafty
