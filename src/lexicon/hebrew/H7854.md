# H7854 שָׂטָן / שטן

## sâṭân

_(saw-tawn' | saw-TAWN | sa-TAHN)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H7853]]; an opponent; especially (with the article prefixed) Satan, the arch-enemy of good; adversary, Satan, withstand.

- adversary, one who withstands
    - adversary (in general - personal or national)
- superhuman adversary
    - Satan (as noun proper)
