# H7079 קְנָת / קנת

## Qᵉnâth

_(ken-awth' | keh-NAWT | keh-NAHT)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; possession; from [[H7069]]; possession; Kenath, a place East of the Jordan; Kenath.

- a city in Gilead east of the Jordan taken possession of by a certain Nobah and renamed in his own honour
