# H6921 קָדִים / קדים

## qâdîym

_(kaw-deem' | kaw-DEEM | ka-DEEM)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or קָדִם; from [[H6923]]; the fore or front part; hence (by orientation) the East (often adverbially, eastward, for brevity the east wind); east(-ward, wind).

- east, east wind
    - east (of direction)
    - east wind
