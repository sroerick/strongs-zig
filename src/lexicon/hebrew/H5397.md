# H5397 נְשָׁמָה / נשמה

## nᵉshâmâh

_(nesh-aw-maw' | neh-shaw-MAW | neh-sha-MA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from [[H5395]]; a puff, i.e., wind, angry or vital breath, divine inspiration, intellect. or (concretely) an animal; blast, (that) breath(-eth), inspiration, soul, spirit.

- breath, spirit
    - breath (of God)
    - breath (of man)
    - every breathing thing
    - spirit (of man)
