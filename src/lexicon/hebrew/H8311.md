# H8311 שָׂרַע / שרע

## sâraʻ

_(saw-rah' | saw-RA | sa-RA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to prolong, i.e., (reflex) be deformed by excess of members; stretch out self, (have any) superfluous thing.

- to extend, stretch out
    - (Qal) extended (participle)
    - (Hithpael) to stretch oneself
