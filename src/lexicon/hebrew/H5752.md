# H5752 עוֹדֵד / עודד

## ʻÔwdêd

_(o-dade' | oh-DADE | oh-DADE)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; restorer; or עֹדֵד; from [[H5749]]; reiteration; Oded, the name of two Israelites; Oded.

- father of Azariah the prophet in the reign of king Asa of Judah
- a prophet of Jehovah in Samaria at the time of Pekah's invasion of Judah
