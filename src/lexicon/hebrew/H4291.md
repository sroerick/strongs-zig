# H4291 מְטָא / מטא

## mᵉṭâʼ

_(met-aw' | meh-TAW | meh-TA)_

### Definition

verb; or מְטָה; (Aramaic), apparently corresponding to [[H4672]] in the intransitive sense of being found present; to arrive, extend or happen; come, reach.

- to reach, come upon, attain
    - (Peal)
        - to reach, come to
        - to reach, extend
        - to come upon
