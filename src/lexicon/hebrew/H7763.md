# H7763 שׁוֹמֵר / שומר

## Shôwmêr

_(sho-mare' | shoh-MARE | shoh-MARE)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; keeper; or שֹׁמֵר; active participle of [[H8104]]; keeper; Shomer, the name of two Israelites; Shomer.

- son of Jehozabad or Shimrith the Moabitess and one of the conspirators who slew king Joash of Judah
- an Asherite, son of Heber
    - also 'Shemer'
