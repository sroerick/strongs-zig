# H6953 קֹהֶלֶת / קהלת

## qôheleth

_(ko-heh'-leth | koh-heh-LET | koh-heh-LET)_

### Definition

masculine noun; feminine of active participle from [[H6950]]; a (female) assembler (i.e., lecturer); abstractly, preaching (used as a 'nom de plume', Koheleth); preacher.

- collector (of sentences), preacher, public speaker, speaker in an assembly, Qoheleth
