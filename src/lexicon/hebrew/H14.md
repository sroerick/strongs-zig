# H14 אָבָה / אבה

## ʼâbâh

_(aw-baw' | aw-BAW | ah-VA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to breathe after, i.e., (figuratively) to be acquiescent; consent, rest content will, be willing.

- to be willing, consent
    - (Qal)
        - to be willing
        - to consent, yield to, accept
        - to desire
