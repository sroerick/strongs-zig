# H2873 טָבַח / טבח

## ṭâbach

_(taw-bakh' | taw-BA | ta-VAHK)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to slaughter (animals or men); kill, (make) slaughter, slay.

- to slaughter, slay, butcher, kill ruthlessly
    - (Qal)
        - to slaughter, butcher
        - to slay, kill ruthlessly (figuratively)
