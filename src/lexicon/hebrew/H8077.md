# H8077 שְׁמָמָה / שממה

## shᵉmâmâh

_(shem-aw-maw' | sheh-maw-MAW | sheh-ma-MA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; or שִׁמָמָה; feminine of [[H8076]]; devastation; figuratively, astonishment; (laid, [[lexicon-key|×]] most) desolate(-ion), waste.

- devastation, waste, desolation
