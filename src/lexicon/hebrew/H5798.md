# H5798 עֻזָּא / עזא

## ʻUzzâʼ

_(ooz-zaw' | oodz-ZAW | oo-ZA)_

### Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; strength; or עֻזָּה; feminine of [[H5797]]; strength; Uzza or Uzzah, the name of five Israelites; Uzza, Uzzah.

- a man slain by Jehovah for touching the sacred ark (noun proper masculine)
- a Benjamite of the sons of Ehud (noun proper masculine)
- ancestor of a family of temple slaves who returned from exile with (noun proper masculine)
- a Merarite Levite (noun proper masculine)
- the garden where kings Manasseh and Amon of Judah were buried (noun proper locative)
