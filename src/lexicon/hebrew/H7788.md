# H7788 שׁוּר / שור

## shûwr

_(shoor | shoor | shoor)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to turn, i.e., travel about (as a harlot or a merchant); go, singular See also [[H7891]].

- (Qal) to travel, journey, go
    - traveller (participle)
    - ([[lexicon-key|BDB]]) meaning dubious
