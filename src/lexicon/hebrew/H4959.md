# H4959 מָשַׁשׁ / משש

## mâshash

_(maw-shash' | maw-SHAHSH | ma-SHAHSH)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to feel of; by implication, to grope; feel, grope, search.

- to feel, grope
    - (Qal) to feel, grope
    - (Piel) to feel over or through, grope
        - to feel through
        - to grope
    - (Hiphil) to feel
