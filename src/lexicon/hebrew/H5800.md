# H5800 עָזַב / עזב

## ʻâzab

_(aw-zab' | aw-DZAHB | ah-ZAHV)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to loosen, i.e., relinquish, permit, etc; commit self, fail, forsake, fortify, help, leave (destitute, off), refuse, [[lexicon-key|×]] surely.

- to leave, loose, forsake
    - (Qal) to leave
        - to depart from, leave behind, leave, let alone
        - to leave, abandon, forsake, neglect, apostatise
        - to let loose, set free, let go, free
    - (Niphal)
        - to be left to
        - to be forsaken
    - (Pual) to be deserted
- to restore, repair
    - (Qal) to repair
