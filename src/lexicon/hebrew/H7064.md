# H7064 קֵן / קן

## qên

_(kane | kane | kane)_

### Definition

masculine noun; contracted from [[H7077]]; a nest (as fixed), sometimes including the nestlings; figuratively, a chamber or dwelling; nest, room.

- nest
    - nest (of bird)
    - cells (like nests in Noah's ark)
