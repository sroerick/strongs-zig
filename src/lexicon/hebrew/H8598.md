# H8598 תַּפּוּחַ / תפוח

## tappûwach

_(tap-poo'-akh | tahp-POO-ah | ta-POO-ak)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H5301]]; an apple (from its fragrance), i.e., the fruit or the tree (probably including others of the pome order, as the quince, the orange, etc.); apple (tree).

- apple, apple tree
    - apple tree
    - apples
