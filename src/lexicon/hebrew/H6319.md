# H6319 פּוֹטִי פֶרַע / פוטי פרע

## Pôwṭîy Pheraʻ

_(po-tee feh'-rah | poh-TEE peh-RA | poh-TEE feh-RA)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; he whom the Ra gave; of Egyptian derivation; Poti-Phera, an Egyptian; Poti-pherah.

- an Egyptian, priest of On, father of Asenath, the wife whom Pharaoh gave to Joseph
