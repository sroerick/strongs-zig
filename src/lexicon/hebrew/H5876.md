# H5876 עֵין חַדָּה / עין חדה

## ʻÊyn Chaddâh

_(ane khad-daw' | ane hahd-DAW | ane ha-DA)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; swift fountain; from [[H5869]] and the feminine of a derivative from [[H2300]]; fountain of sharpness; En-Chaddah, a place in Palestine; En-haddah.

- one of the cities on the border of Issachar
