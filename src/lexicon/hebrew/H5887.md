# H5887 עֵין תַּפּוּחַ / עין תפוח

## ʻÊyn Tappûwach

_(ane tap-poo'-akh | ane tahp-POO-ah | ane ta-POO-ak)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; fountain of the apple-city; from [[H5869]] and [[H8598]]; fountain of an apple-tree; En-Tappuach, a place in Palestine; En-tappuah.

- a place on the border between Ephraim and Manasseh
