# H6909 קַבְצְאֵל / קבצאל

## Qabtsᵉʼêl

_(kab-tseh-ale' | kahb-tseh-ALE | kahv-tseh-ALE)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; God gathers; from [[H6908]] and [[H410]]; God has gathered; Kabtseel, a place in Palestine; Kabzeel.

- the most remote city of Judah; located in southern Judah on the border of Edom


### Note

Compare [[H3343]].
