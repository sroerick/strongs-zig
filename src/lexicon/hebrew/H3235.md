# H3235 יָמַר / ימר

## yâmar

_(yaw-mar' | yaw-MAHR | ya-MAHR)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to exchange; by implication, to change places; boast selves, change.

- to exchange
    - (Hiphil) to exchange
    - (Hithpael) to enter into, be changed into
- ([[lexicon-key|TWOT]]) to promise
