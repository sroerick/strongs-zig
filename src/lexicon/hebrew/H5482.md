# H5482 סְוֵנֵה / סונה

## Çᵉvênêh

_(sev-ay-nay' | seh-way-NAY | seh-vay-NAY)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; her veiling(?); (rather to be written סְוֵנָה; for סְוֵן; i.e to Seven); of Egyptian derivation; Seven, a place in Upper Egypt; Syene.

- a town of Egypt on the frontier of Cush or Ethiopia
