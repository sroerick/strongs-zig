# H92 אֲגֻדָּה / אגדה

## ʼăguddâh

_(ag-ood-daw' | uh-ɡood-DAW | uh-ɡoo-DA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; feminine passive participle of an unused root (meaning to bind); a band, bundle, knot, or arch; bunch, burden, troop.

- band, binding
    - cords, bands, thongs (metaphorical of slavery)
    - bunch of hyssop
    - band of men, troops
    - vault (of the heavens), firmament (binding earth to the heavens)
