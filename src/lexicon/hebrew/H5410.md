# H5410 נָתִיב / נתיב

## nâthîyb

_(naw-theeb' | naw-TEEB | na-TEEV)_

### Definition

noun; or (feminine) נְתִיבָה; or נְתִבָה; (Jeremiah 6:16), from an unused root meaning to tramp; a (beaten) track; path(-way), [[lexicon-key|×]] travel(-ler), way.

- trodden with the feet, path, pathway
- path, pathway, traveller
