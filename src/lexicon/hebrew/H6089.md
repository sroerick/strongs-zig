# H6089 עֶצֶב / עצב

## ʻetseb

_(eh'-tseb | eh-TSEB | eh-TSEV)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H6087]]; an earthen vessel; usually (painful) toil; also a pang (whether of body or mind); grievous, idol, labor, sorrow.

- pain, hurt, toil, sorrow, labour, hardship
    - pain
    - hurt, offense
    - toil, hardship
- vessel, creation, object
- ([[lexicon-key|TWOT]]) idol
