## pîylegesh

_(pee-leh'-ghesh | pee-leh-ɡESH | pee-leh-ɡESH)_

### Definition

feminine noun; or פִּלֶגֶשׁ; of uncertain derivation; a concubine; also (masculine) a paramour; concubine, paramour.

- concubine, paramour
