# H5678 עֶבְרָה / עברה

## ʻebrâh

_(eb-raw' | eb-RAW | ev-RA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; feminine of [[H5676]]; an outburst of passion; anger, rage, wrath.

- outpouring, overflow, excess, fury, wrath, arrogance
    - overflow, excess, outburst
    - arrogance
    - overflowing rage or fury
