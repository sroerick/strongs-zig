# H929 בְּהֵמָה / בהמה

## bᵉhêmâh

_(be-hay-maw' | beh-hay-MAW | beh-hay-MA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from an unused root (probably meaning to be mute); properly, a dumb beast; especially any large quadruped or animal (often collective); beast, cattle.

- beast, cattle, animal
    - beasts (collective of all animals)
    - cattle, livestock (of domestic animals)
    - wild beasts
