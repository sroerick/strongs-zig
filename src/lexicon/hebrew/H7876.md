# H7876 שָׁיָה / שיה

## shâyâh

_(shaw-yaw' | shaw-YAW | sha-YA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to keep in memory; be unmindful.

- (Qal) to forget, deprive


### Note

Render [[Deuteronomy 32#18|Deuteronomy 32:18]], "A Rock bore thee, thou must recollect; and (yet) thou hast forgotten," etc.
