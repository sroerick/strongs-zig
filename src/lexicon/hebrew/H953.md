# H953 בּוֹר / בור

## bôwr

_(bore | bore | bore)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H952]] (in the sense of [[H877]]); a pit hole (especially one used as a cistern or a prison); cistern, dungeon, fountain, pit, well.

- pit, well, cistern
