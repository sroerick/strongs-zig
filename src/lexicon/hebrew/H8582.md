# H8582 תָּעָה / תעה

## tâʻâh

_(taw-aw' | taw-AW | ta-AH)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to vacillate, i.e., reel or stray (literally or figuratively); also causative of both; (cause to) go astray, deceive, dissemble, (cause to, make to) err, pant, seduce, (make to) stagger, (cause to) wander, be out of the way.

- to err, wander, go astray, stagger
    - (Qal) to err
        - to wander about (physically)
        - of intoxication
        - of sin (ethically)
        - wandering (of the mind)
    - (Niphal)
        - to be made to wander about, be made to stagger (drunkard)
        - to be led astray (ethically)
    - (Hiphil) to cause to wander
        - to cause to wander about (physically)
        - to cause to wander (of intoxication)
        - to cause to err, mislead (mentally and morally)
