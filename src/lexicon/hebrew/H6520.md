# H6520 פְּרָזוֹן / פרזון

## pᵉrâzôwn

_(per-aw-zone' | peh-raw-DZONE | peh-ra-ZONE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from the same as [[H6518]]; magistracy, i.e., leadership (also concretely, chieftains); village.

- rural population, rustics, rural people, people of unwalled villages
    - meaning dubious
