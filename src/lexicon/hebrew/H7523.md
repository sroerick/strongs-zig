# H7523 רָצַח / רצח

## râtsach

_(raw-tsakh' | raw-TSA | ra-TSAHK)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to dash in pieces, i.e., kill (a human being), especially to murder; put to death, kill, (man-)slay(-er), murder(-er).

- to murder, slay, kill
    - (Qal) to murder, slay
        - premeditated
        - accidental
        - as avenger
        - slayer (intentional) (participle)
    - (Niphal) to be slain
    - (Piel)
        - to murder, assassinate
        - murderer, assassin (participle) (substantive)
    - (Pual) to be killed
