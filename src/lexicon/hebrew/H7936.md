# H7936 שָׂכַר / שכר

## sâkar

_(saw-kar' | saw-KAHR | sa-HAHR)_

### Definition

verb; or (by permutation) סָכַר; (Ezra 4:5), a primitive root (apparently akin (by prosthesis) to [[H3739]] through the idea of temporary purchase; compare [[H7937]]); to hire; earn wages, hire (out self), reward, [[lexicon-key|×]] surely.

- to hire
    - (Qal) to hire
    - (Niphal) to hire oneself out
    - (Hithpael) to earn wages
