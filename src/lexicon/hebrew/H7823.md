# H7823 שָׁחִיס / שחיס

## shâchîyç

_(shaw-khece' | shaw-HEES | sha-HEES)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or סָחִישׁ; from an unused root apparently meaning to sprout; aftergrowth; (that) which springeth of the same.

- volunteer grain
    - grain that shoots up of itself the second year, without replanting
