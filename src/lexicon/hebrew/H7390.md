# H7390 רַךְ / רך

## rak

_(rak | rahk | rahk)_

### Definition

adjective; from [[H7401]]; tender (literally or figuratively); by implication, weak; faint((-hearted), soft, tender(-hearted) (one), weak.

- tender, soft, delicate, weak
    - tender, delicate (of flesh)
    - weak of heart, timid
    - soft (of words)
        - gentle words (substantive)
