# H1899 הֶגֶה / הגה

## hegeh

_(heh'-geh | heh-ɡEH | heh-ɡEH)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H1897]]; a muttering (in sighing, thought, or as thunder); mourning, sound, tale.

- a rumbling, growling, moaning
    - a rumbling, growling
    - a moaning
    - a sigh, moan, sighing
