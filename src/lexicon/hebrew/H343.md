# H343 אֵיד / איד

## ʼêyd

_(ade | ade | ade)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from the same as [[H181]] (in the sense of bending down); oppression; by implication misfortune, ruin; calamity, destruction.

- distress, burden, calamity
    - burden (of the righteous)
    - calamity (of nation)
    - disaster (of wicked)
    - day of calamity
