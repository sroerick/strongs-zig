# H7992 שְׁלִישִׁי / שלישי

## shᵉlîyshîy

_(shel-ee-shee' | sheh-lee-SHEE | sheh-lee-SHEE)_

### Definition

adjective; ordinal from [[H7969]]; third; feminine a third (part); by extension, a third (day, year or time); specifically, a third-story cell); third (part, rank, time), three (years old).

- third, one third, third part, third time
    - ordinal number
