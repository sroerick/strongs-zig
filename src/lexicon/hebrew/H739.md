# H739 אֲרִיאֵל / אריאל

## ʼărîyʼêl

_(ar-ee-ale' | uh-ree-ALE | uh-ree-ALE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or אֲרִאֵל; from [[H738]] and [[H410]]; lion of God; i.e., heroic; lionlike men.

- meaning uncertain
    - ([[lexicon-key|CLBL]]) possibly lion-like
    - ([[lexicon-key|BDB]]) possibly two sons of Ariel of Moab


### Note

The meaning is considered uncertain.
