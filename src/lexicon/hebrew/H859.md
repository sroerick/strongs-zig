# H859 אַתָּה / אתה

## ʼattâh

_(at-taw' | at-TAW | ah-TA)_

### Definition

personal pronoun; or (shortened); אַתָּ; or אַת; feminine (irregular) sometimes אַתִּי; plural masculine אַתֶּם; feminine אַתֶּן; or אַתֵּנָה; or אַתֵּנָּה; a primitive pronoun of the second person; thou and thee, or (plural) ye and you; thee, thou, ye, you.

- you (second person singular masculine)
