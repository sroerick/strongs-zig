# H738 אֲרִי / ארי

## ʼărîy

_(ar-ee' | uh-REE | uh-REE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or (prolonged) אַרְיֵה; from [[H717]] (in the sense of violence); a lion; (young) lion, [[lexicon-key|+]] pierce (from the margin).

- lion
    - pictures or images of lions
