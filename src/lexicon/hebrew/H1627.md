# H1627 גָּרוֹן / גרון

## gârôwn

_(gaw-rone' | ɡaw-RONE | ɡa-RONE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or (shortened) גָּרֹן; from [[H1641]]; (compare [[H1621]]); the throat (as roughened by swallowing); [[lexicon-key|×]] aloud, mouth, neck, throat.

- neck, throat
    - neck
    - throat
        - of open sepulchre (figuratively)
