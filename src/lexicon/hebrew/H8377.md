# H8377 תְּאוֹ / תאו

## tᵉʼôw

_(teh-o' | teh-OH | teh-OH)_

### Definition

masculine noun; and תּוֹא; (the original form), from [[H8376]]; a species of antelope (probably from the white stripe on the cheek); wild bull (ox).

- wild ox, antelope, oryx
    - perhaps an extinct animal, exact meaning unknown
