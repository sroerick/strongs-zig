# H5483 סוּס / סוס

## çûwç

_(soos | soos | soos)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or סֻס; from an unused root meaning to skip (properly, for joy); a horse (as leaping); crane, horse(-back, -hoof).

- swallow, swift
- horse
    - chariot horses

Compare [[H6571]].
