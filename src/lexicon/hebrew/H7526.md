# H7526 רְצִין / רצין

## Rᵉtsîyn

_(rets-een' | reh-TSEEN | reh-TSEEN)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; firm; probably for [[H7522]]; Retsin, the name of a Syrian and of an Israelite; Rezin.

- king of Damascus during the reigns of kings Jotham and Ahaz of Judah
- the progenitor of a family of temple slaves who returned from captivity with Zerubbabel
