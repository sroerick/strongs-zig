# H6166 עֲרָד / ערד

## ʻĂrâd

_(ar-awd' | uh-RAWD | uh-RAHD)_

### Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; a wild ass; from an unused root meaning to sequester itself; fugitive; Arad, the name of a Canaanite and an Israelite; Arad.

- a Benjamite, son of Beriah, who drove out the inhabitants of Gath (noun proper masculine)
- a royal city of the Canaanites north of the wilderness of Judah (noun proper locative)
