# H6946 קָדֵשׁ / קדש

## Qâdêsh

_(kaw-dashe' | kaw-DAYSH | ka-DAYSH)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; holy; the same as [[H6945]]; sanctuary; Kadesh, a place in the Desert; Kadesh.

- a city in the extreme south of Judah
    - same as 'Kedesh' and 'Kadesh-barnea'


### Note

Compare [[H6947]].
