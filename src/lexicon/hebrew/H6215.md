# H6215 עֵשָׂו / עשו

## ʻÊsâv

_(ay-sawv' | ay-SAW | ay-SAHV)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; hairy; apparently a form of the passive participle of [[H6213]] in the original sense of handling; rough (i.e., sensibly felt); Esav, a son of Isaac, including his posterity; Esau.

- eldest son of Isaac and Rebecca and twin brother of Jacob; sold the birthright for food when he was hungry and the divine blessing went to Jacob; progenitor of the Arab peoples
