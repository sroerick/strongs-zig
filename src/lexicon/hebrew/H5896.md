# H5896 עִירָא / עירא

## ʻÎyrâʼ

_(ee-raw' | ee-RAW | ee-RA)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; watchful of a city; from [[H5782]]; wakefulness; Ira, the name of three Israelites; Ira.

- the Jairite, one of David's great officers
- one of David's mighty warriors
- a Tekoite, son of Ikkesh, and another of David's mighty warriors
