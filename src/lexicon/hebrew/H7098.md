# H7098 קָצָה / קצה

## qâtsâh

_(kaw-tsaw' | kaw-TSAW | ka-TSA)_

### Definition

noun; feminine of [[H7097]]; (used like [[H7097]]); a termination; coast, corner, (selv-)edge, lowest, (uttermost) participle.

- end, extremity
    - end
    - from the whole of, from among (of what is included between extremities)
