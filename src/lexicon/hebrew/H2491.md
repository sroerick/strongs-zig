# H2491 חָלָל / חלל

## châlâl

_(khaw-lawl' | haw-LAWL | ha-LAHL)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H2490]]; pierced (especially to death); figuratively, polluted; kill, profane, slain (man), [[lexicon-key|×]] slew, (deadly) wounded.

- slain, fatally wounded, pierced (noun masculine)
    - pierced, fatally wounded
    - slain
- ([[lexicon-key|CLBL]]) profaned (adjective)
    - defiled, profaned (by divorce)
