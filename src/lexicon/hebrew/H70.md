# H70 אֹבֶן / אבן

## ʼôben

_(o' ben | oh-BEN | oh-VEN)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from the same as [[H68]]; a pair of stones (only dual); a potter's wheel or a midwife's stool (consisting alike of two horizontal disks with a support between); wheel, stool.

- wheel, disc
    - potter's wheel
    - bearing-stool, midwife's stool
