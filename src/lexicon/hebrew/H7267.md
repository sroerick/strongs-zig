# H7267 רֹגֶז / רגז

## rôgez

_(ro'-ghez | roh-ɡEDZ | roh-ɡEZ)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H7264]]; commotion, restlessness (of a horse), crash (of thunder), disquiet, anger; fear, noise, rage, trouble(-ing), wrath.

- agitation, excitement, raging, trouble, turmoil, trembling
    - turmoil, disquiet, raging
    - trembling, trepidation
