# H6249 עַשְׁתֵּי / עשתי

## ʻashtêy

_(ash-tay' | ash-TAY | ash-TAY)_

### Definition

noun; apparently masculine plural construction of [[H6247]] in the sense of an afterthought (used only in connection with [[H6240]] in lieu of [[H259]]); eleven or (ordinal) eleventh; eleven(-th).

- one, eleven, eleventh
