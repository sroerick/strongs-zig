# H8364 שֻׁתַלְחִי / שתלחי

## Shuthalchîy

_(shoo-thal-kee' | shoo-tahl-HEE | shoo-tahl-HEE)_

### Definition

adjective; freshly appointed; patronymically from [[H7803]]; a Shuthalchite (collectively) or descendants of Shuthelach; Shuthalhites.

- descendants of Shuthelah, son of Ephraim, and of his son of the same name
