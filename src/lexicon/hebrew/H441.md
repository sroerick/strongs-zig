# H441 אַלּוּף / אלוף

## ʼallûwph

_(al-loof' | al-LOOP | ah-LOOF)_

### Definition

masculine adjective; or (shortened) אַלֻּף; from [[H502]]; familiar; a friend, also gentle; and so, a chieftain (as notable, like neat cattle); captain, duke, (chief) friend, governor, guide, ox.

- tame, docile
- friend, intimate
- chief
