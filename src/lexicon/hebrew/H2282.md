# H2282 חַג / חג

## chag

_(khag | hahɡ | hahɡ)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or חָג; from [[H2287]]; a festival, or a victim therefor; (solemn) feast (day), sacrifice, solemnity.

- festival, feast, festival-gathering, pilgrim-feast
    - feast
    - festival sacrifice
