# H1060 בְּכוֹר / בכור

## bᵉkôwr

_(bek-ore' | beh-KORE | beh-HORE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H1069]]; first-born; hence, chief; eldest (son), firstborn(-ling).

- firstborn, firstling
    - of men and women
    - of animals
    - noun of relation (figuratively)
