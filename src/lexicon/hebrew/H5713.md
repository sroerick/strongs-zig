# H5713 עֵדָה / עדה

## ʻêdâh

_(ay-daw' | ay-DAW | ay-DA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; feminine of [[H5707]] in its technical sense; testimony; testimony, witness.

- testimony, witness
    - always plural and always of laws as divine testimonies


### Note

Compare [[H5712]].
