# H2251 חָבַט / חבט

## châbaṭ

_(khaw-bat' | haw-BAHT | ha-VAHT)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to knock out or off; beat (off, out), thresh.

- to beat, beat out, beat off, thresh
    - (Qal)
        - to beat out
        - to beat off
    - (Niphal) to be beaten out
