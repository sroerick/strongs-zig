# H5030 נָבִיא / נביא

## nâbîyʼ

_(naw-bee' | naw-BEE | na-VEE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H5012]]; a prophet or (generally) inspired man; prophecy, that prophesy, prophet.

- spokesman, speaker, prophet
    - prophet
    - false prophet
    - heathen prophet
