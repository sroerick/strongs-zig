# H7803 שׁוּתֶלַח / שותלח

## Shûwthelach

_(shoo-theh'-lakh | shoo-teh-LA | shoo-teh-LAHK)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; noise of breaking; probably from [[H7582]] and the same as [[H8520]]; crash of breakage; Shuthelach, the name of two Israelites; Shuthelah.

- son of Ephraim, ancestor of Joshua, and progenitor of family of Ephraim
