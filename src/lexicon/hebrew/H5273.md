# H5273 נָעִים / נעים

## nâʻîym

_(naw-eem' | naw-EEM | na-EEM)_

### Definition

adjective; from [[H5276]]; delightful (objective or subjective, literal or figurative); pleasant(-ure), sweet.

- pleasant, delightful, sweet, lovely, agreeable
    - delightful
    - lovely, beautiful (physical)
- singing, sweetly sounding, musical
