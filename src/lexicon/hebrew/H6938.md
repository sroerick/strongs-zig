# H6938 קֵדָר / קדר

## Qêdâr

_(kay-dawr' | kay-DAWR | kay-DAHR)_

### Definition

proper noun; dark; from [[H6937]]; dusky (of the skin or the tent); Kedar, a son of Ishmael; also (collectively) Bedouin (as his descendants or representatives); Kedar.

- a son of Ishmael (noun proper masculine)
- the descendants of Kedar (noun proper people)
