# H8504 תְּכֵלֶת / תכלת

## tᵉkêleth

_(tek-ay'-leth | teh-kay-LET | teh-hay-LET)_

### Definition

feminine noun; probably for [[H7827]]; the cerulean mussel, i.e., the color (violet) obtained therefrom or stuff dyed therewith; blue.

- violet, violet stuff
    - violet thread
    - violet stuff or fabric
- ([[lexicon-key|TWOT]]) blue (covering spectrum from brilliant red through deep purple)
