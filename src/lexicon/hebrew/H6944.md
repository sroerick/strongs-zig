# H6944 קֹדֶשׁ / קדש

## qôdesh

_(ko'-desh | koh-DESH | koh-DESH)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H6942]]; a sacred place or thing; rarely abstract, sanctity; consecrated (thing), dedicated (thing), hallowed (thing), holiness, ([[lexicon-key|×]] most) holy ([[lexicon-key|×]] day, portion, thing), saint, sanctuary.

- apartness, holiness, sacredness, separateness
    - apartness, sacredness, holiness
        - of God
        - of places
        - of things
    - set-apartness, separateness
