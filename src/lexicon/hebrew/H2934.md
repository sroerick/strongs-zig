# H2934 טָמַן / טמן

## ṭâman

_(taw-man' | taw-MAHN | ta-MAHN)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to hide (by covering over); hide, lay privily, in secret.

- to hide, conceal, bury
    - (Qal)
        - to hide
        - hiding, concealing, secretly laying (participle)
        - darkness (participle)
    - (Niphal) to hide oneself
    - (Hiphil) to hide
