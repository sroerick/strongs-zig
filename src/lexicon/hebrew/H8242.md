# H8242 שַׂק / שק

## saq

_(sak | sahk | sahk)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H8264]]; properly, a mesh (as allowing a liquid to run through), i.e., coarse loose cloth or sacking (used in mourning and for bagging); hence, a bag (for grain, etc.); sack(-cloth, -clothes).

- mesh, sackcloth, sack, sacking
    - sack (for grain)
    - sackcloth
        - worn in mourning or humiliation
        - same material spread out to lie on
