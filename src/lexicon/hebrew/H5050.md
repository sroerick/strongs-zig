# H5050 נָגַהּ / נגה

## nâgahh

_(naw-gah' | naw-ɡA | na-ɡA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to glitter; causatively, to illuminate; (en-)lighten, (cause to) shine.

- to shine
    - (Qal) to shine
    - (Hiphil)
        - to enlighten
        - to cause to shine
