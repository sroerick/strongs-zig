# H6491 פָּקַח / פקח

## pâqach

_(paw-kakh' | paw-KA | pa-KAHK)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to open (the senses, especially the eyes); figuratively, to be observant; open.

- to open (the eyes)
    - (Qal)
        - to open (the eyes)
        - to open (the ears)
    - (Niphal) to be opened
