# H8562 תַּמְרוּק / תמרוק

## tamrûwq

_(tam-rook' | tahm-ROOK | tahm-ROOK)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or תַּמְרֻק; or תַּמְרִיק; from [[H4838]]; properly, a scouring, i.e., soap or perfumery for the bath; figuratively, a detergent; [[lexicon-key|×]] cleanse, (thing for) purification, (thing for) purifying.

- a scraping, rubbing
- ([[lexicon-key|CLBL]]) remedy (for an injury)
