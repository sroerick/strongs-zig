# H4268 מַחֲסֶה / מחסה

## machăçeh

_(makh-as-eh' | ma-huh-SEH | ma-huh-SEH)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or מַחְסֶה; from [[H2620]]; a shelter (literally or figuratively); hope, (place of) refuge, shelter, trust.

- refuge, shelter
    - from rain or storm, from danger
    - of falsehood
