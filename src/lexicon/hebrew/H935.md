# H935 בּוֹא / בוא

## bôwʼ

_(bo | boh | boh)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to go or come (in a wide variety of applications); abide, apply, attain, [[lexicon-key|×]] be, befall, [[lexicon-key|+]] besiege, bring (forth, in, into, to pass), call, carry, [[lexicon-key|×]] certainly, (cause, let, thing for) to come (against, in, out, upon, to pass), depart, [[lexicon-key|×]] doubtless again, [[lexicon-key|+]] eat, [[lexicon-key|+]] employ, (cause to) enter (in, into, -tering, -trance, -try), be fallen, fetch, [[lexicon-key|+]] follow, get, give, go (down, in, to war), grant, [[lexicon-key|+]] have, [[lexicon-key|×]] indeed, (in-)vade, lead, lift (up), mention, pull in, put, resort, run (down), send, set, [[lexicon-key|×]] (well) stricken (in age), [[lexicon-key|×]] surely, take (in), way.

- to go in, enter, come, go, come in
    - (Qal)
        - to enter, come in
        - to come
            - to come with
            - to come upon, fall or light upon, attack (enemy)
            - to come to pass
        - to attain to
        - to be enumerated
        - to go
    - (Hiphil)
        - to lead in
        - to carry in
        - to bring in, cause to come in, gather, cause to come, bring near, bring against, bring upon
        - to bring to pass
    - (Hophal)
        - to be brought, brought in
        - to be introduced, be put
