# H4806 מְרִיא / מריא

## mᵉrîyʼ

_(mer-ee' | meh-REE | meh-REE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H4754]] in the sense of grossness, through the idea of domineering (compare [[H4756]]); stall-fed; often (as noun) a beeve; fat (fed) beast (cattle, -ling).

- well-fed, fatling
