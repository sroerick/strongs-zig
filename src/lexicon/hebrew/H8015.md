# H8015 שְׁלֹמִי / שלמי

## Shᵉlômîy

_(shel-o-mee' | sheh-loh-MEE | sheh-loh-MEE)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; peaceful; from [[H7965]]; peaceable; Shelomi, an Israelite; Shelomi.

- an Asherite, father of Ahihud, the leader of the tribe of Asher at the time of the division of the land of Canaan
