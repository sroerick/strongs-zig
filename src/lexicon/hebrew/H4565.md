# H4565 מִסְתָּר / מסתר

## miçtâr

_(mis-tawr' | mis-TAWR | mees-TAHR)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H5641]]; properly, a concealer, i.e., a covert; secret(-ly) (place).

- secret place, hiding place
    - secret place
    - hiding place
        - for protection
        - for perpetration of crime
