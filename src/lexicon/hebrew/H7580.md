# H7580 שָׁאַג / שאג

## shâʼag

_(shaw-ag' | shaw-Aɡ | sha-Aɡ)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to rumble or moan; [[lexicon-key|×]] mightily, roar.

- (Qal) to roar
    - of lion, conqueror, Jehovah, cry of distress
