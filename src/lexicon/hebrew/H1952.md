# H1952 הוֹן / הון

## hôwn

_(hone | hone | hone)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from the same as [[H1951]] in the sense of [[H202]]; wealth; by implication, enough; enough, for nought, riches, substance, wealth.

- wealth, riches, substance (noun masculine)
    - wealth
    - price, high value
- enough!, sufficiency (interjection)
