# H1739 דָּוֶה / דוה

## dâveh

_(daw-veh' | daw-WEH | da-VEH)_

### Definition

adjective; from [[H1738]]; sick (especially in menstruation); faint, menstruous cloth, she that is sick, having sickness.

- faint, unwell
    - faint, weak
    - unwell, menstruous
