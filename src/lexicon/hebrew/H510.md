# H510 אַלְקוּם / אלקום

## ʼalqûwm

_(al-koom' | al-KOOM | al-KOOM)_

### Definition

masculine noun; probably from [[H408]] and [[H6965]]; a non-rising (i.e., resistlessness); no rising up.

- ([[lexicon-key|TWOT]]) (negative adverb) no, not, nor, neither
- ([[lexicon-key|BDB]]) band of soldiers
- ([[lexicon-key|CLBL]]) no rebellion, no uprising (literally)
