# H7033 קָלָה / קלה

## qâlâh

_(kaw-law' | kaw-LAW | ka-LA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root (rather identical with [[H7034]] through the idea of shrinkage by heat); to toast, i.e., scorch partially or slowly; dried, loathsome, parch, roast.

- to roast, parch
    - (Qal) to roast
    - (Niphal)
        - to be burned
        - burning (participle)
