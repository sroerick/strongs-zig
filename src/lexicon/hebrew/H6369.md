# H6369 פִּיכֹל / פיכל

## Pîykôl

_(pee-kole' | pee-KOLE | pee-HOLE)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; strong; apparently from [[H6310]] and [[H3605]]; mouth of all; Phichol (Phicol), a Philistine, possibly a title or namesake; Phichol.

- chief captain of the army of king Abimelech of the Philistines of Gerar in the days of Abraham and Isaac
