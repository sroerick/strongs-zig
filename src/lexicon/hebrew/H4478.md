# H4478 מָן / מן

## mân

_(mawn | mawn | mahn)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H4100]]; literally a whatness (so to speak), i.e., manna (so called from the question about it); manna.

- manna
    - the bread from Heaven that fed the Israelites for 40 years of wilderness wanderings
    - means 'What is it?'
