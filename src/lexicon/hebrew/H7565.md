# H7565 רֶשֶׁף / רשף

## resheph

_(reh'-shef | reh-SHEP | reh-SHEF)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H8313]]; a live coal; by analogy lightning; figuratively, an arrow, (as flashing through the air); specifically, fever; arrow, (burning) coal, burning heat, [[lexicon-key|+]] spark, hot thunderbolt.

- flame, firebolt, spark
    - flame
    - fire-bolt
