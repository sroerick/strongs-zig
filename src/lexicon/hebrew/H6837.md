# H6837 צִפְיוֹן / צפיון

## Tsiphyôwn

_(tsif-yone' | tsip-YONE | tseef-YONE)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; lookout; from [[H6822]]; watch-tower; Tsiphjon, an Israelite; Ziphion.

- a son of Gad and progenitor of the family of Zephonites
    - also 'Zephon'


### Note

Compare [[H6827]].
