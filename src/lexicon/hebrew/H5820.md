# H5820 עַזְמָוֶת / עזמות

## ʻAzmâveth

_(az-maw'-veth | adz-maw-WET | az-ma-VET)_

### Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; strong unto death; from [[H5794]] and [[H4194]]; strong one of death; Azmaveth, the name of three Israelites and of a place in Palestine; Azmaveth.

- one of David's mighty warriors (noun proper masculine)
- a descendant of Mephibosheth, the son of Jonathan and grandson of king Saul (noun proper masculine)
- a Benjamite, father of Jeziel and Pelet who were two of David's men who joined him at Ziklag (noun proper masculine)
- overseer of the royal treasures in the reign of David (noun proper masculine)
- a place in Benjamin; also 'Beth-azmeveth' (noun proper locative)
