# H5257 נְסִיךְ / נסיך

## nᵉçîyk

_(nes-eek' | neh-SEEK | neh-SEEK)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H5258]]; properly, something poured out, i.e., a libation; by implication, a prince (as anointed); drink offering, duke, prince(-ipal).

- poured out, libation, molten image, one anointed
    - libation, drink-offering
    - molten image
- prince, anointed one
