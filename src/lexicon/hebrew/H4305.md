# H4305 מָטַר / מטר

## mâṭar

_(maw-tar' | maw-TAHR | ma-TAHR)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to rain; (cause to) rain (upon).

- to rain
    - (Niphal) to be rained on or upon
    - (Hiphil)
        - to rain, send rain
        - to rain hail, send hail
