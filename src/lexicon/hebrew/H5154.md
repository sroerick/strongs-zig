# H5154 נְחוּשָׁה / נחושה

## nᵉchûwshâh

_(nekh-oo-shaw' | neh-hoo-SHAW | neh-hoo-SHA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; or נְחֻשָׁה; feminine of [[H5153]]; copper; brass, steel.

- copper, bronze
    - copper (made from melting copper ore)
    - bronze (made from copper and alloy)

Compare [[H5176]].
