# H7198 קֶשֶׁת / קשת

## qesheth

_(keh'-sheth | keh-SHET | keh-SHET)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from [[H7185]] in the original sense (of [[H6983]]) of bending; a bow, for shooting (hence, figuratively, strength) or the iris; [[lexicon-key|×]] arch(-er), [[lexicon-key|+]] arrow, bow(-man, -shot).

- bow
    - bow (for hunting, battle)
    - bowmen, archers
    - bow (figuratively of might)
    - rainbow
