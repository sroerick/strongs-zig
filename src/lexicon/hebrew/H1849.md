# H1849 דָּפַק / דפק

## dâphaq

_(daw-fak' | daw-PAHK | da-FAHK)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to knock; by analogy, to press severely; beat, knock, overdrive.

- to beat, knock
    - (Qal)
        - to beat, drive severely
        - to knock
    - (Hithpael) to beat violently
