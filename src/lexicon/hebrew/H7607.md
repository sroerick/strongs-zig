# H7607 שְׁאֵר / שאר

## shᵉʼêr

_(sheh-ayr' | sheh-ARE | sheh-ARE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H7604]]; flesh (as swelling out), as living or for food; generally food of any kind; figuratively, kindred by blood; body, flesh, food, (near) kin(-sman, -swoman), near (nigh) (of kin).

- flesh, food, body, near kin, near kinswoman
    - flesh
        - as food
        - for physical power (figuratively)
    - flesh relation, blood relation
    - self
