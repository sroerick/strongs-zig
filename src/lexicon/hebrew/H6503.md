# H6503 פַּרְבָּר / פרבר

## Parbâr

_(par-bawr' | pahr-BAWR | pahr-BAHR)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; open apartment; or פַּרְוָר; of foreign origin; Parbar or Parvar, a quarter of Jerusalem; Parbar, suburb.

- a structure or building attached to the west side of Solomon's temple
