# H4658 מַפֶּלֶת / מפלת

## mappeleth

_(map-peh'-leth | mahp-peh-LET | ma-peh-LET)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from [[H5307]]; fall, i.e., decadence; concretely, a ruin; specifically a carcase; carcase, fall, ruin.

- carcass, ruin, overthrow
    - carcass
    - ruin, overthrow
