# H7251 רָבַע / רבע

## râbaʻ

_(raw-bah' | raw-BA | ra-VA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root (rather identical with [[H7250]] through the idea of sprawling 'at all fours' (or possibly the reverse is the order of deriv.); compare [[H702]]); properly, to be four (sided); used only as denominative of [[H7253]]; to be quadrate; (four-)square(-d).

- to square, be squared
    - (Qal) square (participle)
    - (Pual) square (participle)
