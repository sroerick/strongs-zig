# H1011 בֵּית בִּרְאִי / בית בראי

## Bêyth Birʼîy

_(bayth bir-ee' | bate bir-EE | bate beer-EE)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; house of a creative one; from [[H1004]] and [[H1254]]; house of a creative one; Beth-Biri, a place in Palestine; Bethbirei.

- a place in the territory of Simeon
