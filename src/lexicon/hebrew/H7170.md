# H7170 קְרַץ / קרץ

## qᵉrats

_(ker-ats' | keh-RAHTS | keh-RAHTS)_

### Definition

masculine noun; corresponding to [[H7171]] in the sense of a bit (to 'eat the morsels of' any one, i.e; chew him up (figuratively) by slander; [[lexicon-key|+]] accuse.

- piece
    - denounce, slander, accuse maliciously, chew on (idiom)
