# H4263 מַחְמָל / מחמל

## machmâl

_(makh-mawl' | ma-MAWL | mahk-MAHL)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H2550]]; (by paronomasia with [[H4261]]) delight; properly, sympathy; pitieth.

- object of compassion or pity, thing pitied
- ([[lexicon-key|TWOT]]) object of deep love
