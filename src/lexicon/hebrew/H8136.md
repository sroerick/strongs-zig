# H8136 שִׁנְאָן / שנאן

## shinʼân

_(shin-awn' | shin-AWN | sheen-AN)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H8132]]; change, i.e., repetition; [[lexicon-key|×]] angels.

- repetition
    - repetitions, twice-told repetition, twice ten thousands, thousands of repetitions, twice-told thousands
        - myriads (in effect)
