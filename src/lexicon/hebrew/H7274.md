## Rôgᵉlîym

_(ro-gel-eem' | roh-ɡeh-LEEM | roh-ɡeh-LEEM)_

### Definition

proper locative noun; place of fullers; plural of active participle of [[H7270]]; fullers (as tramping the cloth in washing); Rogelim, a place East of the Jordan; Rogelim.

- a place in the highlands east of the Jordan
