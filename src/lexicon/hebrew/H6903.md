# H6903 קְבֵל / קבל

## qᵉbêl

_(keb-ale' | keh-BALE | keh-VALE)_

### Definition

conjunction; or קֳבֵל; (Aramaic), (corresponding to [[H6905]]; (adverbially) in front of; usually (with other particles) on account of, so as, since, hence; [[lexicon-key|+]] according to, [[lexicon-key|+]] as, [[lexicon-key|+]] because, before, [[lexicon-key|+]] for this cause, [[lexicon-key|+]] forasmuch as, [[lexicon-key|+]] by this means, over against, by reason of, [[lexicon-key|+]] that, [[lexicon-key|+]] therefore, [[lexicon-key|+]] though, [[lexicon-key|+]] wherefore.

- front (substantive)
- in front of, before, because of, in view of, by reason of, because of this, therefore (preposition)
- because that, inasmuch as, although, according as, before (conjunction)
- accordingly, then (adverb)
