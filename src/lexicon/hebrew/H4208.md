# H4208 מַזָּלָה / מזלה

## mazzâlâh

_(maz-zaw-law' | mahdz-zaw-LAW | ma-za-LA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; apparently from [[H5140]] in the sense of raining; a constellation, i.e., Zodiacal sign (perhaps as affecting the weather); planet.

- constellations
    - signs of zodiac (maybe)


### Note

Compare [[H4216]].
