# H6265 עֲתָיָה / עתיה

## ʻĂthâyâh

_(ath-aw-yaw' | uh-taw-YAW | uh-ta-YA)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; Jehovah has helped; from [[H5790]] and [[H3050]]; Jah has helped; Athajah, an Israelite; Athaiah.

- a descendant of Judah through Perez; dwelt at Jerusalem after the return from exile in Babylon
