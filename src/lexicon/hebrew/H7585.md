# H7585 שְׁאוֹל / שאול

## shᵉʼôwl

_(sheh-ole' | sheh-OLE | sheh-OLE)_

### Definition

feminine noun; or שְׁאֹל; from [[H7592]]; hades or the world of the dead (as if a subterranean retreat), including its accessories and inmates; grave, hell, pit.

- sheol, underworld, grave, hell, pit
    - the underworld
    - Sheol - the OT designation for the abode of the dead
        - place of no return
        - without praise of God
        - wicked sent there for punishment
        - righteous not abandoned to it
        - of the place of exile (figuratively)
        - of extreme degradation in sin
