# H7250 רָבַע / רבע

## râbaʻ

_(raw-bah' | raw-BA | ra-VA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to squat or lie out flat, i.e., (specifically) in copulation; let gender, lie down.

- to lie stretched out, lie down
    - (Qal)
        - to lie down
            - for repose
            - for copulation (of woman with beast)
    - (Hiphil) to cause to lay down (of cattle breeding)
