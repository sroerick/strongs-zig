# H7651 שֶׁבַע / שבע

## shebaʻ

_(sheh'-bah | sheh-BA | sheh-VA)_

### Definition

noun; or (masculine) (שִׁבְעָה); from [[H7650]]; a primitive cardinal number; seven (as the sacred full one); also (adverbially) seven times; by implication, a week; by extension, an indefinite number; ([[lexicon-key|+]] by) seven(-fold),-s, (-teen, -teenth), -th, times).

- seven (cardinal number)
    - as ordinal number
    - in combination - 17, 700 etc


### Note

Compare [[H7658]].
