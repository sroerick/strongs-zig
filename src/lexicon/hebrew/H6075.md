# H6075 עָפַל / עפל

## ʻâphal

_(aw-fal' | aw-PAHL | ah-FAHL)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to swell; figuratively, be elated; be lifted up, presume.

- to lift up, swell, be lifted up
    - (Pual) to swell
    - (Hiphil) to be bold, be swelled
- to presume, be heedless
    - (Hiphil) to be heedless, show heedlessness
