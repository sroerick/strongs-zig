# H5475 סוֹד / סוד

## çôwd

_(sode | sode | sode)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H3245]]; a session, i.e., company of persons (in close deliberation); by implication, intimacy, consultation, a secret; assembly, consel, inward, secret (counsel).

- council, counsel, assembly
    - council (of familiar conversation)
        - divan, circle (of familiar friends)
        - assembly, company
    - counsel
        - counsel (itself)
        - secret counsel
        - familiar converse, intimacy (with God)
