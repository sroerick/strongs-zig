# H973 בָּחַל / בחל

## bâchal

_(baw-khal' | baw-HAHL | ba-HAHL)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to loath; abhor, get hastily (from the margin for [[H926]]).

- to loathe, abhor, feel loathing
    - (Qal)
        - to loathe
        - to gain by greed, gain hastily
    - (Pual) obtained by greed
