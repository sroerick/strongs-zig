# H6030 עָנָה / ענה

## ʻânâh

_(aw-naw' | aw-NAW | ah-NA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to eye or (generally) to heed, i.e., pay attention; by implication, to respond; by extension to begin to speak; specifically to sing, shout, testify, announce; give account, afflict (by mistake for [[H6031]]), (cause to, give) answer, bring low (by mistake for [[H6031]]), cry, hear, Leannoth, lift up, say, [[lexicon-key|×]] scholar, (give a) shout, sing (together by course), speak, testify, utter, (bear) witness.

- to answer, respond, testify, speak, shout
    - (Qal)
        - to answer, respond to
        - to testify, respond as a witness
    - (Niphal)
        - to make answer
        - to be answered, receive answer
- (Qal) to sing, utter tunefully
- (Qal) to dwell
