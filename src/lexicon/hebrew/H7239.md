# H7239 רִבּוֹ / רבו

## ribbôw

_(rib-bo' | rib-BOH | ree-BOH)_

### Definition

feminine noun; from [[H7231]]; or רִבּוֹא from [[H7231]]; a myriad, i.e., indefinitely, large number; great things, ten, eighteen, forty, [[lexicon-key|+]] sixscore, [[lexicon-key|+]] threescore, [[lexicon-key|×]] twenty, (twenty) thousand(-s).

- ten thousand, myriad
