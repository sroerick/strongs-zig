# H6021 עֲמָשָׂא / עמשא

## ʻĂmâsâʼ

_(am-aw-saw' | uh-maw-SAW | uh-ma-SA)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; burden; from [[H6006]]; burden; Amasa, the name of two Israelites; Amasa.

- son of Ithra or Jether by Abigail the sister of David and general of the army of Absalom
- son of Hadlai and a prince of Ephraim in the reign of king Ahaz
