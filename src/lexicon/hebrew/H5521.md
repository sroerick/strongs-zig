# H5521 סֻכָּה / סכה

## çukkâh

_(sook-kaw' | sook-KAW | soo-KA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; feminine of [[H5520]]; a hut or lair; booth, cottage, covert, pavilion, tabernacle, tent.

- thicket, covert, booth
    - thicket
    - booth (rude or temporary shelter)
