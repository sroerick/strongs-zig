# H8154 שָׁסָה / שסה

## shâçâh

_(shaw-saw' | shaw-SAW | sha-SA)_

### Definition

verb; or שָׁשָׂה; (Isaiah 10:13), a primitive root; to plunder; destroyer, rob, spoil(-er).

- to spoil, plunder, take spoil
    - (Qal)
        - to plunder
        - plunderers (participle)
    - (Poel) to plunder
