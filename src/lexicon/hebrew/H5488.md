# H5488 סוּף / סוף

## çûwph

_(soof | soop | soof)_

### Definition

masculine noun; probably of Egyptian origin; a reed, especially the papyrus; flag, Red (sea), weed.

- reed, rush, water plant
    - rushes
    - sea of rushes
        - of Red Sea
        - of arms of Red Sea
        - of Gulf of Suez
        - of sea from straits to Gulf of Akaba

Compare [[H5489]].
