# H5082 נְדִיבָה / נדיבה

## nᵉdîybâh

_(ned-ee-baw' | neh-dee-BAW | neh-dee-VA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; feminine of [[H5081]]; properly, nobility, i.e., reputation; soul.

- nobility, nobleness, noble deeds
    - nobility (of rank), honour
    - noble things
