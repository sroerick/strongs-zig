# H1920 הָדַף / הדף

## hâdaph

_(haw-daf' | haw-DAHP | ha-DAHF)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to push away or down; cast away (out), drive, expel, thrust (away).

- to thrust, push, drive, cast away, cast out, expel, thrust away
    - (Qal)
        - to thrust, push
        - to drive out, thrust out
        - to depose
