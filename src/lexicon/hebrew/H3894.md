# H3894 לָחוּם / לחום

## lâchûwm

_(law-khoom' | law-HOOM | la-HOOM)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or לָחֻם; passive participle of [[H3898]]; properly, eaten, i.e., food; also flesh, i.e., body; while...is eating, flesh.

- intestines, bowels
    - meaning uncertain
- ([[lexicon-key|CLBL]]) food, something eaten
