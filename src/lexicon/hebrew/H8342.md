# H8342 שָׂשׂוֹן / ששון

## sâsôwn

_(saw-sone' | saw-SONE | sa-SONE)_

### Definition

masculine noun; or שָׂשֹׂן; from [[H7797]]; cheerfulness; specifically, welcome; gladness, joy, mirth, rejoicing.

- gladness, joy, exultation, rejoicing
    - exultation, joy
