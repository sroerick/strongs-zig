# H7600 שַׁאֲנָן / שאנן

## shaʼănân

_(shah-an-awn' | sha-uh-NAWN | sha-uh-NAHN)_

### Definition

adjective; from [[H7599]]; secure; in a bad sense, haughty; that is at ease, quiet, tumult.

- at ease, quiet, secure (adjective)
    - at ease, secure
    - at ease, careless, wanton, arrogant
- security, pride, arrogance (substantive)
    - one at ease
    - arrogance

Compare [[H7946]].
