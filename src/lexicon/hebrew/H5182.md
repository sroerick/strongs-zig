# H5182 נְחַת / נחת

## nᵉchath

_(nekh-ath' | neh-HAHT | neh-HAHT)_

### Definition

verb; corresponding to [[H5181]]; to descend; causatively, to bring away, deposit, depose; carry, come down, depose, lay up, place.

- to descend
    - (Peal) to descend
    - (Aphel) to deposit
    - (Hophal) to be deposed, be cast down
