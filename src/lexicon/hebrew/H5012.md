# H5012 נָבָא / נבא

## nâbâʼ

_(naw-baw' | naw-BAW | na-VA)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to prophesy, i.e., speak (or sing) by inspiration (in prediction or simple discourse); prophesy(-ing), make self a prophet.

- to prophesy
    - (Niphal)
        - to prophesy
            - under influence of divine spirit
            - of false prophets
    - (Hithpael)
        - to prophesy
            - under influence of divine spirit
            - of false prophets
