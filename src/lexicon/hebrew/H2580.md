# H2580 חֵן / חן

## chên

_(khane | hane | hane)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H2603]]; graciousness, i.e., subjective (kindness, favor) or objective (beauty); favour, grace(-ious), pleasant, precious, (well-)favoured.

- favour, grace, charm
    - favour, grace, elegance
    - favour, acceptance
