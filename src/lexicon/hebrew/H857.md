# H857 אָתָה / אתה

## ʼâthâh

_(aw-thaw' | aw-TAW | ah-TA)_

### Definition

verb; or אָתָא; a primitive root (collateral to [[H225]] contraction); to arrive; (things to) (be-)come (upon), bring.

- to come, arrive
    - (Qal) to come (of men, time, beasts, calamity)
    - (Hiphil) to bring
