# H7543 רָקַח / רקח

## râqach

_(raw-kakh' | raw-KA | ra-KAHK)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; to perfume; apothecary, compound, make (ointment), prepare, spice.

- to mix, compound
    - (Qal)
        - to mix, compound
        - compounder, mixer, perfumer (participle) (substantive)
    - (Pual) mixed (participle)
    - (Hiphil) to mix
