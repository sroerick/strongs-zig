# H7916 שָׂכִיר / שכיר

## sâkîyr

_(saw-keer' | saw-KEER | sa-HEER)_

### Definition

adjective; from [[H7936]]; a man who is hired by the day or year; hired (man, servant), hireling.

- hired
    - hired (of animal)
    - hireling, hired labourer (substantive)
        - mercenary
