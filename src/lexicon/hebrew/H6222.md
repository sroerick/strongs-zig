# H6222 עֲשָׂיָה / עשיה

## ʻĂsâyâh

_(aw-saw-yaw' | uh-saw-YAW | uh-sa-YA)_

### Definition

proper masculine noun; made by Jehovah; from [[H6213]] and [[H3050]]; Jah has made; Asajah, the name of three or four Israelites; Asaiah.

- a prince of the tribe of Simeon in the time of king Hezekiah of Judah
- a servant of king Josiah of Judah
- a Merarite Levite, chief of his family, in the time of David
