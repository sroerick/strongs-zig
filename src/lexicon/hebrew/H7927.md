# H7927 שְׁכֶם / שכם

## Shᵉkem

_(shek-em' | sheh-KEM | sheh-HEM)_

### Definition

proper locative noun, proper masculine noun; back, shoulder; the same as [[H7926]]; ridge; Shekem, a place in Palestine; Shechem.

- son of Hamor, the chieftain of the Hivites at Shechem at the time of Jacob's arrival (noun proper masculine)
- a city in Manasseh; located in a valley between Mount Ebal and Mount Gerizim, 34 miles (54 kilometers) north of Jerusalem and 7 miles (noun proper locative)
