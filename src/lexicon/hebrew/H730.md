# H730 אֶרֶז / ארז

## ʼerez

_(eh-rez' | eh-REDZ | eh-REZ)_

### Definition

masculine noun; from [[H729]]; a cedar tree (from the tenacity of its roots); cedar (tree).

- cedar
    - cedar tree
    - cedar timber, cedar wood (in building)
    - cedar wood (in purifications)
