# H3332 יָצַק / יצק

## yâtsaq

_(yaw-tsak' | yaw-TSAHK | ya-TSAHK)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to pour out (transitive or intransitive); by implication, to melt or cast as metal; by extension, to place firmly, to stiffen or grow hard; cast, cleave fast, be (as) firm, grow, be hard, lay out, molten, overflow, pour (out), run out, set down, stedfast.

- to pour, flow, cast, pour out
    - (Qal)
        - to pour, pour out
        - to cast
        - to flow
    - (Hiphil) to pour, pour out
    - (Hophal)
        - to be poured
        - cast, molten (participle)
        - being firmly established (participle)
