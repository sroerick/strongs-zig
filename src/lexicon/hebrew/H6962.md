# H6962 קוּט / קוט

## qûwṭ

_(koot | koot | koot)_

### Definition

verb; a primitive root; properly, to cut off, i.e., (figuratively) detest; begrieved, loathe self.

- to loathe, be grieved, feel a loathing
    - (Qal) to feel a loathing
    - (Niphal)
        - to feel loathing against self
        - to detest
    - (Hithpolel) to loathe oneself
