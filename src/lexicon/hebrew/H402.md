# H402 אׇכְלָה / אכלה

## ʼoklâh

_(ok-law' | oke-LAW | oke-LA)_

### Definition

feminine noun; feminine of [[H401]]; food; consume, devour, eat, food, meat.

- food
    - food, eating
    - object of devouring, consuming
        - by wild beasts (figurative)
        - in fire
        - of judgment (figurative)
