# G4377 προσφωνέω

## prosphōnéō

_(pros-fo-neh'-o | prose-foh-NEH-oh | prose-foh-NAY-oh)_

### Definition

from [[G4314]] and [[G5455]]; to sound towards, i.e., address, exclaim, summon; call unto, speak (un-)to.

- to call to, to address by calling
- to call to one's self, summon
