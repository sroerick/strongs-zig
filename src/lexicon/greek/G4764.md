# G4764 στρῆνος

## strēnos

_(stray'-nos | STRAY-nose | STRAY-nose)_

### Definition

akin to [[G4731]]; a "straining", "strenuousness" or "strength", i.e., (figuratively) luxury (voluptuousness); delicacy.

- excessive strength which longs to break forth, over strength
- luxury
- eager desire
