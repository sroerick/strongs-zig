# G1199 δεσμόν

## desmón

_(des-mon' | theh-SMONE | thay-SMONE)_

### Definition

neuter and masculine respectively from [[G1210]]; a band, i.e., ligament (of the body) or shackle (of a prisoner); figuratively, an impediment or disability; band, bond, chain, string.

- a band or bond
