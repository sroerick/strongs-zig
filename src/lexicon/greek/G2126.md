# G2126 εὐλαβής

## eulabḗs

_(yoo-lab-ace' | ev-la-VASE | ave-la-VASE)_

### Definition

from [[G2095]] and [[G2983]]; taking well (carefully), i.e., circumspect (religiously, pious); devout.

- taking hold well
  - carefully and surely
  - cautiously
- reverencing God, pious, religious
