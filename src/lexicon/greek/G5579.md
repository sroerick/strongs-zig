# G5579 ψεῦδος

## pseûdos

_(psyoo'-dos | PSEV-those | PSAVE-those)_

### Definition

from [[G5574]]; a falsehood; lie, lying.

- a lie
- conscious and intentional falsehood
- in a broad sense, whatever is not what it seems to be
  - of perverse, impious, deceitful precepts
