# G4914 συνήθεια

## synḗtheia

_(soon-ay'-thi-ah | soon-A-thee-ah | syoon-A-thee-ah)_

### Definition

from a compound of [[G4862]] and [[G2239]]; mutual habituation, i.e., usage; custom.

- intercourse (with one), intimacy
- custom
- a being used to, accustomed
