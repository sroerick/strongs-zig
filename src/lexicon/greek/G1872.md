# G1872 ἐπακολουθέω

## epakolouthéō

_(ep-ak-ol-oo-theh'-o | ep-ah-koh-loo-THEH-oh | ape-ah-koh-loo-THAY-oh)_

### Definition

from [[G1909]] and [[G190]]; to accompany; follow (after).

- to follow (close) upon, follow after
- to tread in one's footsteps i.e., to imitate his example
