# G4391 προϋπάρχω

## proÿpárchō

_(pro-oop-ar'-kho | proh-oo-PAHR-hoh | proh-yoo-PAHR-hoh)_

### Definition

from [[G4253]] and [[G5225]]; to exist before, i.e., (adverbially) to be or do something previously; [[lexicon-key|+]] be before(-time).

- to be before, exist previously

[[G4253]], [[G5225]]
