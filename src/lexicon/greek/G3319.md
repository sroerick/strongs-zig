## mésos

_(mes'-os | MEH-sose | MAY-sose)_

### Definition

from [[G3326]]; middle (as an adjective or (neuter) noun); among, [[lexicon-key|X]] before them, between, [[lexicon-key|+]] forth, mid(-day, -night), midst, way.

- middle
- the midst
- in the midst of, amongst
