# G1421 δυσερμήνευτος

## dysermḗneutos

_(doos-er-mane'-yoo-tos | thoo-ser-MAY-nev-tose | thyoo-sare-MAY-nayf-tose)_

### Definition

from [[G1418]] and a presumed derivative of [[G2059]]; difficult of explanation; hard to be uttered.

- hard to interpret, difficult to explain
