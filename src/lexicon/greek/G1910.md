# G1910 ἐπιβαίνω

## epibaínō

_(ep-ee-bah'-ee-no | eh-pee-VEH-noh | ay-pee-VAY-noh)_

### Definition

from [[G1909]] and the base of [[G939]]; to walk upon, i.e., mount, ascend, embark, arrive; come (into), enter into, go abroad, sit upon, take ship.

- to get upon, mount
  - to embark in
  - to go aboard (a ship)
- to set foot in, enter
