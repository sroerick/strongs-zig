# G2009 ἐπιτιμία

## epitimía

_(ep-ee-tee-mee'-ah | eh-pee-tee-MEE-ah | ay-pee-tee-MEE-ah)_

### Definition

from a compound of [[G1909]] and [[G5092]]; properly, esteem, i.e., citizenship; used (in the sense of [[G2008]]) of a penalty; punishment.

- punishment
