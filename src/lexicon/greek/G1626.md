# G1626 ἔκτρωμα

## éktrōma

_(ek'-tro-mah | EK-troh-ma | AKE-troh-ma)_

### Definition

from a comparative of [[G1537]] and τιτρώσκω (to wound); a miscarriage (abortion), i.e., (by analogy) untimely birth; born out of due time.

- an abortion, abortive birth
- an untimely birth
