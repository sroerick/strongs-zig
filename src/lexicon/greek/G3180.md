# G3180 μεθοδεία

## methodeía

_(meth-od-i'-ah | meh-thoh-THEE-ah | may-thoh-THEE-ah)_

### Definition

from a compound of [[G3326]] and [[G3593]] (compare "method"); travelling over, i.e., travesty (trickery); wile, lie in wait.

- cunning arts, deceit, craft, trickery
