# G845 αὐτόπτης

## autóptēs

_(ow-top'-tace | av-TOH-ptase | af-TOH-ptase)_

### Definition

from [[G846]] and [[G3700]]; self-seeing, i.e., an eye-witness; eye-witness.

- seeing with one's own eye, an eye-witness
  - a medical term: autopsy, a detailed examination
