# G1189 δέομαι

## déomai

_(deh'-om-ahee | THEH-oh-meh | THAY-oh-may)_

### Definition

middle voice of [[G1210]]; to beg (as binding oneself), i.e., petition; beseech, pray (to), make request.

- to want, lack
- to desire, long for
- to ask, beg
  - the thing asked for
  - to pray, make supplications

### Note

Compare [[G4441]].
