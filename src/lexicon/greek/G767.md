# G767 ἄσημος

## ásēmos

_(as'-ay-mos | AH-say-mose | AH-say-mose)_

### Definition

from [[G1]] (as a negative particle) and the base of [[G4591]]; unmarked, i.e., (figuratively) ignoble; mean.

- unmarked or unstamped
  - of money
- unknown, of no mark, insignificant, ignoble
