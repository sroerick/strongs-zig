# G4587 σεμνότης

## semnótēs

_(sem-not'-ace | sem-NOH-tase | same-NOH-tase)_

### Definition

from [[G4586]]; venerableness, i.e., probity; gravity, honesty.

- the characteristic of a thing or person which entitles to reverence and respect, dignity, majesty, sanctity
- honor, purity
