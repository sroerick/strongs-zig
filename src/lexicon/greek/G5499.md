# G5499 χειροποίητος

## cheiropoíētos

_(khi-rop-oy'-ay-tos | hee-roh-POO-ay-tose | hee-roh-POO-ay-tose)_

### Definition

from [[G5495]] and a derivative of [[G4160]]; manufactured, i.e., of human construction; made by (make with) hands.

- made by the hands i.e., the skill of men
  - of temples
  - of circumcision
  - of idols
