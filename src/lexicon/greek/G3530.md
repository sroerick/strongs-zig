# G3530 Νικόδημος

## Nikódēmos

_(nik-od'-ay-mos | nee-KOH-thay-mose | nee-KOH-thay-mose)_

### Definition

conqueror; from [[G3534]] and [[G1218]]; victorious among his people; Nicodemus, an Israelite; Nicodemus.

- a member of the Sanhedrin who took the part of Jesus
