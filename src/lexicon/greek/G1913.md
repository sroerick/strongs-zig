# G1913 ἐπιβιβάζω

## epibibázō

_(ep-ee-bee-bad'-zo | eh-pee-vee-VA-zoh | ay-pee-vee-VA-zoh)_

### Definition

from [[G1909]] and a reduplicated derivative of the base of [[G939]] (compare [[G307]]); to cause to mount (an animal); set on.

- to cause to mount
- to place upon
