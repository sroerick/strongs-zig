# G4303 προμαρτύρομαι

## promartýromai

_(prom-ar-too'-rom-ahee | proh-mahr-TOO-roh-meh | proh-mahr-TYOO-roh-may)_

### Definition

from [[G4253]] and [[G3143]]; to be a witness in advance i.e., predict; testify beforehand.

- antetestor
- to testify beforehand
  - to make known, predict
