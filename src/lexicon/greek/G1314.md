# G1314 διαφυλάσσω

## diaphylássō

_(dee-af-oo-las'-so | thee-ah-foo-LAHS-soh | thee-ah-fyoo-LAHS-soh)_

### Definition

from [[G1223]] and [[G5442]]; to guard thoroughly, i.e., protect; keep.

- to guard carefully
  - the LXX used this especially of God's providential care
