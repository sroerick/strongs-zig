# G4942 συνυποκρίνομαι

## synypokrínomai

_(soon-oo-pok-rin'-om-ahee | soon-oo-poh-KREE-noh-meh | syoon-yoo-poh-KREE-noh-may)_

### Definition

from [[G4862]] and [[G5271]]; to act hypocritically in concert with; dissemble with.

- to dissemble with
- to act hypocritically with
