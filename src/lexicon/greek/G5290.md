# G5290 ὑποστρέφω

## hypostréphō

_(hoop-os-tref'-o | hoo-poh-STREH-foh | yoo-poh-STRAY-foh)_

### Definition

from [[G5259]] and [[G4762]]; to turn under (behind), i.e., to return (literally or figuratively); come again, return (again, back again), turn back (again).

- to turn back
  - to turn about
- to return
