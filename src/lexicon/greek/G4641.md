# G4641 σκληροκαρδία

## sklērokardía

_(sklay-rok-ar-dee'-ah | sklay-roh-kahr-THEE-ah | sklay-roh-kahr-THEE-ah)_

### Definition

feminine of a compound of [[G4642]] and [[G2588]]; hard-heartedness, i.e., (specially), destitution of (spiritual) perception; hardness of heart.

- hardness of heart
