# G1837 ἐξηχέομαι

## exēchéomai

_(ex-ay-kheh'-om-ahee | eks-ay-HEH-oh-meh | ayks-ay-HAY-oh-may)_

### Definition

middle voice from [[G1537]] and [[G2278]]; to "echo" forth, i.e., resound (be generally reported); sound forth.

- to sound forth, emit, sound, resound
