# G2169 εὐχαριστία

## eucharistía

_(yoo-khar-is-tee'-ah | ev-ha-ree-STEE-ah | afe-ha-ree-STEE-ah)_

### Definition

from [[G2170]]; gratitude; actively, grateful language (to God, as an act of worship); thankfulness, (giving of) thanks(-giving).

- thankfulness
- the giving of thanks
