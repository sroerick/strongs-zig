# G2155 εὔσπλαγχνος

## eúsplanchnos

_(yoo'-splangkh-nos | EV-splahng-hnose | AFE-splahng-hnose)_

### Definition

from [[G2095]] and [[G4698]]; well compassioned, i.e., sympathetic; pitiful, tender-hearted.

- having strong bowels
- compassionate, tender hearted
