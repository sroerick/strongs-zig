# G1887 ἐπαύριον

## epaúrion

_(ep-ow'-ree-on | ep-EV-ree-one | ape-A-ree-one)_

### Definition

from [[G1909]] and [[G839]]; occurring on the succeeding day, i.e., ([[G2250]] being implied) to-morrow; day following, morrow, next day (after).

- on the morrow, the next day
