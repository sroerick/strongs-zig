# G2547 κἀκεῖθεν

## kakeîthen

_(kak-i'-then | ka-KEE-then | ka-KEE-thane)_

### Definition

from [[G2532]] and [[G1564]]; likewise from that place (or time); and afterward (from) (thence), thence also.

- of place
  - and from thence, and thence
- of time
  - and thereafter, and afterward
