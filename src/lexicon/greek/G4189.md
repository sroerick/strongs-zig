# G4189 πονηρία

## ponēría

_(pon-ay-ree'-ah | poh-nay-REE-ah | poh-nay-REE-ah)_

### Definition

from [[G4190]]; depravity, i.e., (specially), malice; plural (concretely) plots, sins; iniquity, wickedness.

- depravity, iniquity, wickedness
- malice
- evil purposes and desires
