# G4483 ῥέω

## rhéō

_(hreh'-o | REH-oh | RAY-oh)_

### Definition

perhaps akin (or identical) with [[G4482]] (through the idea of pouring forth); to utter, i.e., speak or say; command, make, say, speak (of).

- to pour forth, to utter

### Note

Compare [[G3004]].
