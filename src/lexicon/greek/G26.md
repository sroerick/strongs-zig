# G26 ἀγάπη

## agápē

_(ag-ah'-pay | ah-GA-pay | ah-GA-pay)_

### Definition

from [[G25]]; love, i.e., affection or benevolence; specially (plural) a love-feast; (feast of) charity(-ably), dear, love.

- brotherly love, affection, good will, love, benevolence
- love feasts
