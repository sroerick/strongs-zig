# G4033 περικυκλόω

## perikyklóō

_(per-ee-koo-klo'-o | peh-ree-koo-KLOH-oh | pay-ree-kyoo-KLOH-oh)_

### Definition

from [[G4012]] and [[G2944]]; to encircle all around, i.e., blockade completely; compass round.

- to encircle, compass about
  - of a city (besieged)
