# G4682 σπαράσσω

## sparássō

_(spar-as'-so | spa-RAHS-soh | spa-RAHS-soh)_

### Definition

prolongation from σπαίρω (to grasp; apparently strengthened from [[G4685]] through the idea of spasmodic contraction); to mangle, i.e., convluse with epilepsy; rend, tear.

- to convulse, tear
