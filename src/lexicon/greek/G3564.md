# G3564 Νυμφᾶς

## Nymphâs

_(noom-fas' | noom-FAHS | nyoom-FAHS)_

### Definition

bridegroom; probably contracted for a compound of [[G3565]] and [[G1435]]; nymph-given (i.e., -born); Nymphas, a Christian; Nymphas.

- a wealthy and zealous Christian in Laodicea
