# G753 ἀρχιτέκτων

## architéktōn

_(ar-khee-tek'-tone | ar-hee-TEK-tone | ar-hee-TAKE-tone)_

### Definition

from [[G746]] and [[G5045]]; a chief constructor, i.e., "architect"; masterbuilder.

- a master builder, an architect, the superintendent in the erection of buildings
