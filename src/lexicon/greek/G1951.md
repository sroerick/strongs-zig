# G1951 ἐπιλέγομαι

## epilégomai

_(ep-ee-leg'-om-ahee | eh-pee-LEH-goh-meh | ay-pee-LAY-goh-may)_

### Definition

middle voice from [[G1909]] and [[G3004]]; to surname, select; call, choose.

- to say besides
- to surname
- to choose for
- to choose for one's self
