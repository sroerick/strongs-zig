# G4687 σπείρω

## speírō

_(spi'-ro | SPEE-roh | SPEE-roh)_

### Definition

probably strengthened from [[G4685]] (through the idea of extending); to scatter, i.e., sow (literally or figuratively); sow(-er), receive seed.

- to sow, scatter, seed
- metaphorically of proverbial sayings
