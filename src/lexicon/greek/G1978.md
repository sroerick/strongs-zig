# G1978 ἐπίσημος

## epísēmos

_(ep-is'-ay-mos | eh-PEE-say-mose | ay-PEE-say-mose)_

### Definition

from [[G1909]] and some form of the base of [[G4591]]; remarkable, i.e., (figuratively) eminent; notable, of note.

- having a mark on it, marked, stamped, coined
- marked
  - in a good sense
    - of note, illustrious
  - in a bad sense
    - notorious, infamous
