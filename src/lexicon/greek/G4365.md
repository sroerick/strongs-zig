# G4365 προσπορεύομαι

## prosporeúomai

_(pros-por-yoo'-om-ahee | prose-poh-REV-oh-meh | prose-poh-RAVE-oh-may)_

### Definition

from [[G4314]] and [[G4198]]; to journey towards, i.e., approach (not the same as [[G4313]]); go before.

- to draw near, approach
