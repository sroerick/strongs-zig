# G1273 διανυκτερεύω

## dianyktereúō

_(dee-an-ook-ter-yoo'-o | thee-ah-nook-teh-REV-oh | thee-ah-nyook-tay-RAVE-oh)_

### Definition

from [[G1223]] and a derivative of [[G3571]]; to sit up the whole night; continue all night.

- to spend the night, to pass the whole night

[[G1223]], [[G3571]]
