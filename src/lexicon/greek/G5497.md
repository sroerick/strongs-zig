# G5497 χειραγωγός

## cheiragōgós

_(khi-rag-o-gos' | hee-ra-goh-GOSE | hee-ra-goh-GOSE)_

### Definition

from [[G5495]] and a reduplicated form of [[G71]]; a hand-leader, i.e., personal conductor (of a blind person); some to lead by the hand.

- leading one by the hand
