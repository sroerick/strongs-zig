# G1473 ἐγώ

## egṓ

_(eg-o' | eh-GOH | ay-GOH)_

### Definition

a primary pronoun of the first person I (only expressed when emphatic); I, me; I, me.

- I, me, my

For the other cases and the plural see [[G1691]], [[G1698]], [[G1700]], [[G2248]], [[G2249]], [[G2254]], [[G2257]], etc.
