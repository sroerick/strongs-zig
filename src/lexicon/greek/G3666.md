# G3666 ὁμοιόω

## homoióō

_(hom-oy-o'-o | hoh-moo-OH-oh | oh-moo-OH-oh)_

### Definition

from [[G3664]]; to assimilate, i.e., compare; passively, to become similar; be (make) like, (in the) liken(-ess), resemble.

- to be made like
- to liken, compare
  - illustrate by comparisons
