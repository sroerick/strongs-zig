# G3861 παράδοξος

## parádoxos

_(par-ad'-ox-os | pa-RA-thoh-ksose | pa-RA-thoh-ksose)_

### Definition

from [[G3844]] and [[G1391]] (in the sense of seeming); contrary to expectation, i.e., extraordinary ("paradox"); strange.

- unexpected, uncommon, incredible, wonderful
