# G5033 τάχιστα

## táchista

_(takh'-is-tah | TA-hee-sta | TA-hee-sta)_

### Definition

neuter plural of the superlative of [[G5036]] (as adverb); most quickly, i.e., (with [[G5613]] prefixed) as soon as possible; [[lexicon-key|+]] with all speed.

- very quickly
