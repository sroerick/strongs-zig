# G2531 καθώς

## kathṓs

_(kath-oce' | ka-THOSE | ka-THOSE)_

### Definition

from [[G2596]] and [[G5613]]; just (or inasmuch) as, that; according to, (according, even) as, how, when.

- according as
  - just as, even as
  - in proportion as, in the degree that
- since, seeing that, agreeably to the fact that
- when, after that
