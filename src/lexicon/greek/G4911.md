# G4911 συνεφίστημι

## synephístēmi

_(soon-ef-is'-tay-mee | soon-eh-FEE-stay-mee | syoon-ay-FEE-stay-mee)_

### Definition

from [[G4862]] and [[G2186]]; to stand up together, i.e., to resist (or assault) jointly; rise up together.

- to place over or appoint together
- to rise up together
  - against one

[[G2186]], [[G4862]]
