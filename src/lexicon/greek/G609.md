# G609 ἀποκόπτω

## apokóptō

_(ap-ok-op'-to | ah-poh-KOH-ptoh | ah-poh-KOH-ptoh)_

### Definition

from [[G575]] and [[G2875]]; to amputate; reflexively (by irony) to mutilate (the privy parts); cut off.

- to cut off, amputate

### Note

Compare [[G2699]].
