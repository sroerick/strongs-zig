# G177 ἀκατακάλυπτος

## akatakályptos

_(ak-at-ak-al'-oop-tos | ah-ka-ta-KA-loo-ptose | ah-ka-ta-KA-lyoo-ptose)_

### Definition

from [[G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of a compound of [[G2596]] and [[G2572]]; unveiled; uncovered.

- not covered, unveiled
