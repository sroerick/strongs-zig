# G3728 ὁρκωμοσία

## horkōmosía

_(hor-ko-mos-ee'ah | hore-koh-moh-SEE-ah | ore-koh-moh-SEE-ah)_

### Definition

from a compound of [[G3727]] and a derivative of [[G3660]]; asseveration on oath; oath.

- affirmation made on oath, the taking of an oath, an oath
