# G315 ἀναγκάζω

## anankázō

_(an-ang-kad'-zo | ah-nahng-KA-zoh | ah-nahng-KA-zoh)_

### Definition

from [[G318]]; to necessitate; compel, constrain.

- to necessitate, compel, drive to, constrain
  - by force, threats, etc.
  - by permission, entreaties, etc.
  - by other means
