# G1227 διαβλέπω

## diablépō

_(dee-ab-lep'-o | thee-ah-VLEH-poh | thee-ah-VLAY-poh)_

### Definition

from [[G1223]] and [[G991]]; to look through, i.e., recover full vision; see clearly.

- to look through, penetrate by vision
  - to look fixedly, stare straight before one
  - to see clearly

[[G991]], [[G1223]]
