# G1117 γόμος

## gómos

_(gom'-os | GOH-mose | GOH-mose)_

### Definition

from [[G1073]]; a load (as filling), i.e., (specially) a cargo, or (by extension) wares; burden, merchandise.

- a lading or freight of a ship, cargo, merchandise conveyed in a ship
- any merchandise
