# G2021 ἐπιχειρέω

## epicheiréō

_(ep-ee-khi-reh'-o | eh-pee-hee-REH-oh | ay-pee-hee-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

from [[G1909]] and [[G5495]]; to put the hand upon, i.e., undertake; go about, take in hand (upon).

- to put the hand to
- to take in hand, undertake, attempt
