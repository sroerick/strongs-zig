# G4880 συναποθνήσκω

## synapothnḗskō

_(soon-ap-oth-nace'-ko | soon-ah-poh-THNAY-skoh | syoon-ah-poh-THNAY-skoh)_

### Definition

from [[G4862]] and [[G599]]; to decease (literally) in company with, or (figuratively), similarly to; be dead (die) with.

- to die together
- to die with one

### See also

[[G599]], [[G4862]]
