# G1275 διαπαντός

## diapantós

_(dee-ap-an-tos' | thee-ah-pahn-TOSE | thee-ah-pahn-TOSE)_

### Definition

from [[G1223]] and the genitive case of [[G3956]]; through all time, i.e., (adverbially) constantly; alway(-s), continually.

- constantly, always, continually
