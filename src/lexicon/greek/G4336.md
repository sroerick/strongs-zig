# G4336 προσεύχομαι

## proseúchomai

_(pros-yoo'-khom-ahee | prose-EV-hoh-meh | prose-AFE-hoh-may)_

### Definition

from [[G4314]] and [[G2172]]; to pray to God, i.e., supplicate, worship; pray ([[lexicon-key|X]] earnestly, for), make prayer.

- to offer prayers, to pray
