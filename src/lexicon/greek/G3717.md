# G3717 ὀρθός

## orthós

_(or-thos' | ore-THOSE | ore-THOSE)_

### Definition

probably from the base of [[G3735]]; right (as rising), i.e., (perpendicularly) erect (figuratively, honest), or (horizontally) level or direct; straight, upright.

- straight, erect
  - upright
  - straight, not crooked
