# G2725 κατήγορος

## katḗgoros

_(kat-ay'-gor-os | ka-TAY-goh-rose | ka-TAY-goh-rose)_

### Definition

from [[G2596]] and [[G58]]; against one in the assembly, i.e., a complainant at law; specially, Satan; accuser.

- an accuser
  - a name given to the devil by the rabbis
