# G4347 προσκολλάω

## proskolláō

_(pros-kol-lah'-o | prose-kole-LA-oh | prose-kole-LA-oh)_

### Definition

from [[G4314]] and [[G2853]]; to glue to, i.e., (figuratively) to adhere; cleave, join (self).

- to glue upon, glue to
- to join one's self to closely, cleave to, stick to
