# G1723 ἐναγκαλίζομαι

## enankalízomai

_(en-ang-kal-id'-zom-ahee | en-ang-ka-LEE-zoh-meh | ane-ang-ka-LEE-zoh-may)_

### Definition

from [[G1722]] and a derivative of [[G43]]; to take in one's arms, i.e., embrace; take up in arms.

- to take into one's arms, embrace

### See also

[[G43]], [[G1722]]
