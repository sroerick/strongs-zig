# G1625 ἐκτρέφω

## ektréphō

_(ek-tref'-o | ek-TREH-foh | ake-TRAY-foh)_

### Definition

from [[G1537]] and [[G5142]]; to rear up to maturity, i.e., (genitive case) to cherish or train; bring up, nourish.

- to nourish up to maturity, to nourish
- to nurture, bring up
