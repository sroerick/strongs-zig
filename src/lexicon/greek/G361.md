# G361 ἀναμάρτητος

## anamártētos

_(an-am-ar'-tay-tos | ah-na-MAHR-tay-tose | ah-na-MAHR-tay-tose)_

### Definition

from [[G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of [[G264]]; sinless; that is without sin.

- sinless
  - of one who has not sinned
  - of one who cannot sin
