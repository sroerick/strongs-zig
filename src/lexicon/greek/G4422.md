# G4422 πτοέω

## ptoéō

_(pto-eh'-o | ptoh-EH-oh | ptoh-A-oh)_

### Definition

probably akin to the alternate of [[G4098]] (through the idea of causing to fall) or to [[G4072]] (through that of causing to fly away); to scare; frighten.

- to terrify
- be terrified
