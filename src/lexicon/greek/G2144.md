# G2144 εὐπρόσδεκτος

## euprósdektos

_(yoo-pros'-dek-tos | ev-PROSE-thek-tose | afe-PROSE-thake-tose)_

### Definition

from [[G2095]] and a derivative of [[G4327]]; well-received, i.e., approved, favorable; acceptable(-ted).

- well received, accepted, acceptable
