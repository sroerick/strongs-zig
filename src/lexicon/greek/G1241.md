# G1241 διαζώννυμι

## diazṓnnymi

_(dee-az-own'-noo-mee | thee-ah-ZONE-noo-mee | thee-ah-ZONE-nyoo-mee)_

### Definition

from [[G1223]] and [[G2224]]; to gird tightly; gird.

- to bind or gird all around
- to gird one's self with a thing, gird a thing around one's self
