# G3595 ὁδηγός

## hodēgós

_(hod-ayg-os' | hoh-thay-GOSE | oh-thay-GOSE)_

### Definition

from [[G3598]] and [[G2233]]; a conductor (literally or figuratively (teacher)); guide, leader.

- a leader of the way, a guide
- a teacher of the ignorant and inexperienced
