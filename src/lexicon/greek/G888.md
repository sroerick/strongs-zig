# G888 ἀχρεῖος

## achreîos

_(akh-ri'-os | ah-HREE-ose | ah-HREE-ose)_

### Definition

from [[G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of [[G5534]] (compare [[G5532]]); useless, i.e., (euphemistically) unmeritorious; unprofitable.

- useless, good for nothing
