# G5346 φημί

## phēmí

_(fay-mee' | fay-MEE | fay-MEE)_

### Definition

properly, the same as the base of [[G5457]] and [[G5316]]; to show or make known one's thoughts, i.e., speak or say; affirm, say.

- to make known one's thoughts, to declare
- to say

### Note

Compare [[G3004]].
