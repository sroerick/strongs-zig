# G2723 κατηγορέω

## katēgoréō

_(kat-ay-gor-eh'-o | ka-tay-goh-REH-oh | ka-tay-goh-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

from [[G2725]]; to be a plaintiff, i.e., to charge with some offence; accuse, object.

- to accuse
  - before a judge: to make an accusation
  - of an extra-judicial accusation
