# G1559 ἐκδιώκω

## ekdiṓkō

_(ek-dee-o'-ko | ek-thee-OH-koh | ake-thee-OH-koh)_

### Definition

from [[G1537]] and [[G1377]]; to pursue out, i.e., expel or persecute implacably; persecute.

- to drive out, banish
- to pursue
  - to persecute, oppress with calamities
