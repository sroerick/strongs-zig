# G413 ἀνέκλειπτος

## anékleiptos

_(an-ek'-lipe-tos | ah-NEH-klee-ptose | ah-NAY-klee-ptose)_

### Definition

from [[G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of [[G1587]]; not left out, i.e., (by implication) inexhaustible; that faileth not.

- unfailing
