## hómoios

_(hom'-oy-os | HOH-moo-ose | OH-moo-ose)_

### Definition

from the base of [[G3674]]; similar (in appearance or character); like, [[lexicon-key|+]] manner.

- like, similar, resembling
  - like: i.e., resembling
  - like: i.e., corresponding to a thing
