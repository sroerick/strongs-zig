# G1573 ἐκκακέω

## ekkakéō

_(ek-kak-eh'-o | ek-ka-KEH-oh | ake-ka-KAY-oh)_

### Definition

from [[G1537]] and [[G2556]]; to be (bad or) weak, i.e., (by implication) to fail (in heart); faint, be weary.

- to be utterly spiritless, to be wearied out, exhausted
