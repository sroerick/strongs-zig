# G4319 προσαιτέω

## prosaitéō

_(pros-ahee-teh'-o | prose-eh-TEH-oh | prose-ay-TAY-oh)_

### Definition

from [[G4314]] and [[G154]]; to ask repeatedly (importune), i.e., solicit; beg.

- to ask for in addition
- to approach one with supplications
- to ask alms
