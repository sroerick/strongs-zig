# G1677 ἐλλογέω

## ellogéō

_(el-log-eh'-o | el-loh-GEH-oh | ale-loh-GAY-oh)_

### Definition

from [[G1722]] and [[G3056]] (in the sense of account); to reckon in, i.e., attribute; impute, put on account.

- to reckon in, set to one's account, lay to one's charge, impute
