# G2342 θηρίον

## thēríon

_(thay-ree'-on | thay-REE-one | thay-REE-one)_

### Definition

diminutive from the same as [[G2339]]; a dangerous animal; (venomous, wild) beast.

- an animal
- a wild animal, wild beast, beast
- metaphorically a brutal, bestial man, savage, ferocious
