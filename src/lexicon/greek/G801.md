# G801 ἀσύνετος

## asýnetos

_(as-oon'-ay-tos | ah-SOO-neh-tose | ah-SYOO-nay-tose)_

### Definition

from [[G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[G4908]]; unintelligent; by implication, wicked; foolish, without understanding.

- unintelligent, without understanding, stupid
