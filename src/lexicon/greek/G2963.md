# G2963 κυριότης

## kyriótēs

_(koo-ree-ot'-ace | koo-ree-OH-tase | kyoo-ree-OH-tase)_

### Definition

from [[G2962]]; mastery, i.e., (concretely and collectively) rulers; dominion, government.

- dominion, power, lordship
- in the New Testament: one who possesses dominion
