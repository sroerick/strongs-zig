# G4282 προετοιμάζω

## proetoimázō

_(pro-et-oy-mad'-zo | proh-eh-too-MA-zoh | proh-ay-too-MA-zoh)_

### Definition

from [[G4253]] and [[G2090]]; to fit up in advance (literally or figuratively); ordain before, prepare afore.

- to prepare before, to make ready beforehand
