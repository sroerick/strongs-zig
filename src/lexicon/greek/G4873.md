# G4873 συνανάκειμαι

## synanákeimai

_(soon-an-ak'-i-mahee | soon-ah-NA-kee-meh | syoon-ah-NA-kee-may)_

### Definition

from [[G4862]] and [[G345]]; to recline in company with (at a meal); sit (down, at the table, together) with (at meat).

- to recline together, feast together
  - of guests
