# G4550 σαπρός

## saprós

_(sap-ros' | sa-PROSE | sa-PROSE)_

### Definition

from [[G4595]]; rotten, i.e., worthless (literally or morally); bad, corrupt.

- rotten, putrefied
- corrupted by one and no longer fit for use, worn out
- of poor quality, bad, unfit for use, worthless

### Note

Compare [[G4190]].
