# G856 ἀφεδρών

## aphedrṓn

_(af-ed-rone' | ah-feh-THRONE | ah-fay-THRONE)_

### Definition

from a compound of [[G575]] and the base of [[G1476]]; a place of sitting apart, i.e., a privy; draught.

- a place where the human waste discharges are dumped
  - a privy, sink, toilet
