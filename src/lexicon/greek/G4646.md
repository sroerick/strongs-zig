# G4646 σκολιός

## skoliós

_(skol-ee-os' | skoh-lee-OSE | skoh-lee-OSE)_

### Definition

from the base of [[G4628]]; warped, i.e., winding; figuratively, perverse; crooked, froward, untoward.

- crooked, curved
- metaphorically
  - perverse, wicked
  - unfair, surly, froward
