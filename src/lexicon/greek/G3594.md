# G3594 ὁδηγέω

## hodēgéō

_(hod-ayg-eh'-o | hoh-thay-GEH-oh | oh-thay-GAY-oh)_

### Definition

from [[G3595]]; to show the way (literally or figuratively (teach)); guide, lead.

- to be a guide, lead on one's way, to guide
- to be a guide or a teacher
  - to give guidance to
