# G1637 ἔλαιον

## élaion

_(el'-ah-yon | EH-leh-one | A-lay-one)_

### Definition

neuter of the same as [[G1636]]; olive oil; oil.

- olive oil
  - for fuel for lamps
  - for healing the sick
  - for anointing the head and body at feasts
  - mentioned among articles of commerce
