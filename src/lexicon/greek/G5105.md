# G5105 τοιγαροῦν

## toigaroûn

_(toy-gar-oon' | too-ga-ROON | too-ga-ROON)_

### Definition

from [[G5104]] and [[G1063]] and [[G3767]]; truly for then, i.e., consequently; there(-where-)fore.

- wherefore then, for which reason, therefore, consequently
