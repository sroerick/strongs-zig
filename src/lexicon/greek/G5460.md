# G5460 φωτεινός

## phōteinós

_(fo-ti-nos' | foh-tee-NOSE | foh-tee-NOSE)_

### Definition

from [[G5457]]; lustrous, i.e., transparent or well-illuminated (figuratively); bright, full of light.

- light
  - composed of light
  - of a bright character
  - full of light
  - well lit
