# G1304 διατρίβω

## diatríbō

_(dee-at-ree'-bo | thee-ah-TREE-voh | thee-ah-TREE-voh)_

### Definition

from [[G1223]] and the base of [[G5147]]; to wear through (time), i.e., remain; abide, be, continue, tarry.

- to rub between, rub hard
- to wear away, consume
- to spend, pass time
