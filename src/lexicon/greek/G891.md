# G891 ἄχρι

## áchri

_(akh'-ree | AH-hree | AH-hree)_

### Definition

akin to [[G206]] (through the idea of a terminus); (of time) until or (of place) up to; as far as, for, in(-to), till, (even) (hither-, un-)to, until, while.

- until, unto, etc.

### Note

Compare [[G3360]].
