# G1801 ἐνωτίζομαι

## enōtízomai

_(en-o-tid'-zom-ahee | en-oh-TEE-zoh-meh | ane-oh-TEE-zoh-may)_

### Definition

middle voice from a compound of [[G1722]] and [[G3775]]; to take in one's ear, i.e., to listen; hearken.

- to receive into the ear, to give ear to, listen

[[G1722]], [[G3775]]
