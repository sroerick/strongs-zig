# G4692 σπεύδω

## speúdō

_(spyoo'-do | SPEV-thoh | SPAVE-thoh)_

### Definition

probably strengthened from [[G4228]]; to "speed" ("study"), i.e., urge on (diligently or earnestly); by implication, to await eagerly; (make, with) haste unto.

- to haste, make haste
- to desire earnestly
