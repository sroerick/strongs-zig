# G179 ἀκατάλυτος

## akatálytos

_(ak-at-al'-oo-tos | ah-ka-TA-loo-tose | ah-ka-TA-lyoo-tose)_

### Definition

from [[G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of [[G2647]]; indissoluble, i.e., (figuratively) permanent; endless.

- indissoluble, not subject to destruction
