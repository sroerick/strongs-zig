## katastrēniáō

_(kat-as-tray-nee-ah'-o | ka-ta-stray-nee-AH-oh | ka-ta-stray-nee-AH-oh)_

### Definition

from [[G2596]] and [[G4763]]; to become voluptuous against; begin to wa[[lexicon-key|x]] wanton against.

- to feel the impulses of sexual desire
