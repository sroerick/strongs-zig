# G255 ἀλυσιτελής

## alysitelḗs

_(al-oo-sit-el-ace' | ah-loo-see-teh-LASE | ah-lyoo-see-tay-LASE)_

### Definition

from [[G1]] (as a negative particle) and the base of [[G3081]]; gainless, i.e., (by implication) pernicious; unprofitable.

- unprofitable, hurtful, pernicious
