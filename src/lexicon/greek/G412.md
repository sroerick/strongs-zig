# G412 ἀνεκλάλητος

## aneklálētos

_(an-ek-lal'-ay-tos | ah-neh-KLA-lay-tose | ah-nay-KLA-lay-tose)_

### Definition

from [[G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of [[G1583]]; not spoken out, i.e., (by implication) unutterable; unspeakable.

- unspeakable
