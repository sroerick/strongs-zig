# G4966 Συχέμ

## Sychém

_(soo-khem' | soo-HEM | syoo-HAME)_

### Definition

shoulder (literal early rising), diligence; of Hebrew origin ([[H7927]]); Sychem (i.e., Shekem), the name of a Canaanite and of a place in Palestine; Sychem.

- a city in Ephraim
- the son of Emmor
