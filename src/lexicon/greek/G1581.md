# G1581 ἐκκόπτω

## ekkóptō

_(ek-kop'-to | ek-KOH-ptoh | ake-KOH-ptoh)_

### Definition

from [[G1537]] and [[G2875]]; to exscind; figuratively, to frustrate; cut down (off, out), hew down, hinder.

- to cut out, cut off
  - of a tree
- metaphorically to cut off occasion
