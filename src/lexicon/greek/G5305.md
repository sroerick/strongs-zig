# G5305 ὕστερον

## hýsteron

_(hoos'-ter-on | HOO-steh-rone | YOO-stay-rone)_

### Definition

neuter of [[G5306]] as adverb; more lately, i.e., eventually; afterward, (at the) last (of all).

- latter, later, coming after, the second
- afterward, after this, later, lastly
