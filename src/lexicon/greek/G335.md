# G335 ἀναίδεια

## anaídeia

_(an-ah'-ee-die-ah' | ah-NEH-thee-ah | ah-NAY-thee-ah)_

### Definition

from a compound of [[G1]] (as a negative particle (compare [[G427]])) and [[G127]]; impudence, i.e., (by implication) importunity; importunity.

- shamelessness, impudence
