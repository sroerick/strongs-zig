# G5203 ὑδρωπικός

## hydrōpikós

_(hoo-dro-pik-os' | hoo-throh-pee-KOSE | yoo-throh-pee-KOSE)_

### Definition

from a compound of [[G5204]] and a derivative of [[G3700]] (as if looking watery); to be "dropsical"; have the dropsy.

- dropsical, suffering from dropsy

[[G3700]], [[G5204]]
