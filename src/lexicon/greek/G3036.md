# G3036 λιθοβολέω

## lithoboléō

_(lith-ob-ol-eh'-o | lee-thoh-voh-LEH-oh | lee-thoh-voh-LAY-oh)_

### Definition

from a compound of [[G3037]] and [[G906]]; to throw stones, i.e., lapidate; stone, cast stones.

- to kill by stoning, to stone
- to pelt one with stones
