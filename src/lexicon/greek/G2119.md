# G2119 εὐκαιρέω

## eukairéō

_(yoo-kahee-reh'-o | ev-keh-REH-oh | afe-kay-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

from [[G2121]]; to have good time, i.e., opportunity or leisure; have leisure (convenient time), spend time.

- to have opportunity
- to have leisure
- to do something
- to give one's time to a thing
