# G3865 παραθεωρέω

## paratheōréō

_(par-ath-eh-o-reh'-o | pa-ra-theh-oh-REH-oh | pa-ra-thay-oh-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

from [[G3844]] and [[G2334]]; to overlook or disregard; neglect.

- to examine things placed beside each other, to compare
- to overlook, neglect
