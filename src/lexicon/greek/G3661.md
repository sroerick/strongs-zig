# G3661 ὁμοθυμαδόν

## homothymadón

_(hom-oth-oo-mad-on' | hoh-moh-thoo-ma-THONE | oh-moh-thyoo-ma-THONE)_

### Definition

adverb from a compound of the base of [[G3674]] and [[G2372]]; unanimously; with one accord (mind).

- with one mind, with one accord, with one passion
