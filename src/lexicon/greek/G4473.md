# G4473 ῥαντισμός

## rhantismós

_(hran-tis-mos' | rahn-tee-SMOSE | rahn-tee-SMOSE)_

### Definition

from [[G4472]]; aspersion (ceremonially or figuratively); sprinkling.

- a sprinkling (purification)
  - blood of sprinkling
    - i.e., appointed for sprinkling (serving to purify)
