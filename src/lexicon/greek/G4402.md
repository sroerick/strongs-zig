# G4402 Πρόχορος

## Próchoros

_(prokh'-or-os | PROH-hoh-rose | PROH-hoh-rose)_

### Definition

leader of the chorus; from [[G4253]] and [[G5525]]; before the dance; Prochorus, a Christian; Prochorus.

- one of the seven deacons chosen by the church at Jerusalem
