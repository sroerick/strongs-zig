# G573 ἁπλοῦς

## haploûs

_(hap-looce' | ha-PLOOS | a-PLOOS)_

### Definition

probably from [[G1]] (as a particle of union) and the base of [[G4120]]; properly, folded together, i.e., single (figuratively, clear); single.

- simple, single
- whole
- good fulfilling its office, sound
  - of the eye
