# G3490 ναύκληρος

## naúklēros

_(now'-klay-ros | NAV-klay-rose | NAF-klay-rose)_

### Definition

from [[G3491]] and [[G2819]] ("clerk"); a captain; owner of a ship.

- a ship owner, ship master
  - one who hires out his vessel, or a portion of it, for purposes of transportation
