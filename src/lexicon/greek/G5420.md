# G5420 φράσσω

## phrássō

_(fras'-so | FRAHS-soh | FRAHS-soh)_

### Definition

apparently a strengthening form of the base of [[G5424]]; to fence or inclose, i.e., (specially), to block up (figuratively, to silence); stop.

- to fence in, block up, stop up, close up
- to put to silence
