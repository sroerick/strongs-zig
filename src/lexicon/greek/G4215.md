# G4215 ποταμός

## potamós

_(pot-am-os' | poh-ta-MOSE | poh-ta-MOSE)_

### Definition

probably from a derivative of the alternate of [[G4095]] (compare [[G4224]]); a current, brook or freshet (as drinkable), i.e., running water; flood, river, stream, water.

- a stream, a river
- a torrent
- floods
