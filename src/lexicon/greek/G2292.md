# G2292 θαῤῥέω

## tharrhéō

_(thar-hreh'-o | thahr-REH-oh | thahr-RAY-oh)_

### Definition

another form for [[G2293]]; to exercise courage; be bold, [[lexicon-key|X]] boldly, have confidence, be confident.

- to be of good courage, be of good cheer
- to be bold
