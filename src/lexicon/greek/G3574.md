# G3574 νυχθήμερον

## nychthḗmeron

_(nookh-thay'-mer-on | nook-THAY-meh-rone | nyook-THAY-may-rone)_

### Definition

from [[G3571]] and [[G2250]]; a day-and-night, i.e., full day of twenty-four hours; night and day.

- a night and a day, the space of twenty four hours
