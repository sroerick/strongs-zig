# G2325 θερίζω

## therízō

_(ther-id'-zo | theh-REE-zoh | thay-REE-zoh)_

### Definition

from [[G2330]] (in the sense of the crop); to harvest; reap.

- to reap, harvest
- proverbial expression for sowing and reaping
- cut off, destroy
  - as crops are cut down with a sickle
