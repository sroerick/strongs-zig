# G314 ἀναγινώσκω

## anaginṓskō

_(an-ag-in-oce'-ko | ah-na-gee-NOH-skoh | ah-na-gee-NOH-skoh)_

### Definition

from [[G303]] and [[G1097]]; to know again, i.e., (by extension) to read; read.

- to distinguish between, to recognize, to know accurately, to acknowledge
- to read
