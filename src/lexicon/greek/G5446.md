# G5446 φυσικός

## physikós

_(foo-see-kos' | foo-see-KOSE | fyoo-see-KOSE)_

### Definition

from [[G5449]]; "physical", i.e., (by implication) instinctive; natural.

- produced by nature, inborn
- agreeable to nature
- governed by (the instincts of) nature

### Note

Compare [[G5591]].
