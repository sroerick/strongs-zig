# G809 ἀσχήμων

## aschḗmōn

_(as-kay'-mone | ah-SKAY-mone | ah-SKAY-mone)_

### Definition

from [[G1]] (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of [[G2192]] (in the sense of its congener [[G4976]]); properly, shapeless, i.e., (figuratively) inelegant; uncomely.

- deformed
- indecent, unseemly
