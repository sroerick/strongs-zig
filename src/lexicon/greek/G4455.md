# G4455 πώποτε

## pṓpote

_(po'-pot-e | POH-poh-teh | POH-poh-tay)_

### Definition

from [[G4452]] and [[G4218]]; at any time, i.e., (with negative particle) at no time; at any time, [[lexicon-key|+]] never(...to any man), [[lexicon-key|+]] yet, never man.

- ever, at any time
