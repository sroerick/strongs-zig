# G4269 πρόγονος

## prógonos

_(prog'-on-os | PROH-goh-nose | PROH-goh-nose)_

### Definition

from [[G4266]]; an ancestor, (grand-)parent; forefather, parent.

- born before, older
  - of ancestors
  - of a mother
  - of grandparents
  - of great-grand parents (if they are still alive)
