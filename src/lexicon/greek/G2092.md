# G2092 ἕτοιμος

## hétoimos

_(het-oy'-mos | HEH-too-mose | AY-too-mose)_

### Definition

from an old noun ἐτεός (fitness); adjusted, i.e., ready; prepared, (made) ready(-iness) (to our hand).

- prepare ready
  - of things
    - ready at hand
    - opportune, seasonable
  - of persons
    - ready prepared
      - to do something
      - to receive one coming
