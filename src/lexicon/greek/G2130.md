# G2130 εὐμετάδοτος

## eumetádotos

_(yoo-met-ad'-ot-os | ev-meh-TA-thoh-tose | ave-may-TA-thoh-tose)_

### Definition

from [[G2095]] and a presumed derivative of [[G3330]]; good at imparting, i.e., liberal; ready to distribute.

- ready or free to impart
- liberal
