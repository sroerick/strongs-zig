# G1753 ἐνέργεια

## enérgeia

_(en-erg'-i-ah | en-ER-gee-ah | ane-ARE-gee-ah)_

### Definition

from [[G1756]]; efficiency ("energy"); operation, strong, (effectual) working.

- working, efficiency
  - in the New Testament used only of superhuman power, whether of God or of the Devil
