# G5231 ὑπεράνω

## hyperánō

_(hoop-er-an'-o | hoo-per-AH-noh | yoo-pare-AH-noh)_

### Definition

from [[G5228]] and [[G507]]; above upward, i.e., greatly higher (in place or rank); far above, over.

- above
  - above a thing
  - of a place
  - of rank or power
