# G273 ἄμεμπτος

## ámemptos

_(am'-emp-tos | AH-mem-ptose | AH-mame-ptose)_

### Definition

from [[G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of [[G3201]]; irreproachable; blameless, faultless, unblamable.

- blameless, deserving no censure, free from fault or defect
