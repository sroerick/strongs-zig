# G731 ἄῤῥητος

## árrhētos

_(ar'-hray-tos | AR-ray-tose | AR-ray-tose)_

### Definition

from [[G1]] (as a negative particle) and the same as [[G4490]]; unsaid, i.e., (by implication) inexpressible; unspeakable.

- unsaid, unspoken
- unspeakable (on account of its sacredness)
