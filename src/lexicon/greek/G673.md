# G673 ἀποχωρίζω

## apochōrízō

_(ap-okh-o-rid'-zo | ah-poh-hoh-REE-zoh | ah-poh-hoh-REE-zoh)_

### Definition

from [[G575]] and [[G5563]]; to rend apart; reflexively, to separate; depart (asunder).

- to separate, sever
- to part asunder
- to separate one's self, depart from
