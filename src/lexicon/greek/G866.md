# G866 ἀφιλάργυρος

## aphilárgyros

_(af-il-ar'-goo-ros | ah-fee-LAHR-goo-rose | ah-fee-LAHR-gyoo-rose)_

### Definition

from [[G1]] (as a negative particle) and [[G5366]]; unavaricious; without covetousness, not greedy of filthy lucre.

- not loving money, not avaricious
