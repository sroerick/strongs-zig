# G176 ἀκατάγνωστος

## akatágnōstos

_(ak-at-ag'-noce-tos | ah-ka-TA-gnoh-stose | ah-ka-TA-gnoh-stose)_

### Definition

from [[G1]] (as a negative particle) and a derivative of [[G2607]]; unblamable; that cannot be condemned.

- that cannot be condemned, not to be censored
