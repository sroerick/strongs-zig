# G1909 ἐπί

## epí

_(ep-ee' | eh-PEE | ay-PEE)_

### Definition

a primary preposition; properly, meaning superimposition (of time, place, order, etc.), as a relation of distribution (with the genitive case), i.e., over, upon, etc.; of rest (with the dative case) at, on, etc.; of direction (with the accusative case) towards, upon, etc; about (the times), above, after, against, among, as long as (touching), at, beside, [[lexicon-key|X]] have charge of, (be-, where-)fore, in (a place, as much as, the time of, -to), (because) of, (up-)on (behalf of), over, (by, for) the space of, through(-out), (un-)to(-ward), with.

- upon, on, at, by, before
- of position, on, at, by, over, against
- to, over, on, at, across, against
