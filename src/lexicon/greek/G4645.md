# G4645 σκληρύνω

## sklērýnō

_(sklay-roo'-no | sklay-ROO-noh | sklay-RYOO-noh)_

### Definition

from [[G4642]]; to indurate, i.e., (figuratively) render stubborn; harden.

- to make hard, harden
- metaphorically
  - to render obstinate, stubborn
  - to be hardened
  - to become obstinate or stubborn
