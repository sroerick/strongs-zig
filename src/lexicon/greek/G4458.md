# G4458 -πώς

## -pṓs

_(poce | pose | pose)_

### Definition

adverb from the base of [[G4225]]; an enclitic particle of indefiniteness of manner; somehow or anyhow; used only in composition; haply, by any (some) means, perhaps.

- by any means, at all, perhaps

### Note

Compare [[G4459]].
