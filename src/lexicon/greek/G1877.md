# G1877 ἐπανάγω

## epanágō

_(ep-an-ag'-o | ep-ah-NA-goh | ape-ah-NA-goh)_

### Definition

from [[G1909]] and [[G321]]; to lead up on, i.e., (technical) to put out (to sea); (intransitively) to return; launch (thrust) out, return.

- to lead up upon
  - a ship upon the deep, to put out
- to lead back
- to return
