# G2729 κατισχύω

## katischýō

_(kat-is-khoo'-o | ka-tee-SKOO-oh | ka-tee-SKYOO-oh)_

### Definition

from [[G2596]] and [[G2480]]; to overpower; prevail (against).

- to be strong to another's detriment, to prevail against
- to be superior in strength
- to overcome
- to prevail
